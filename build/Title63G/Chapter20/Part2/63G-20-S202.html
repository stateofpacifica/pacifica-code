Effective 5/12/2015
63G-20-202.  Prohibition on government retaliation.
     Notwithstanding any other law, a state or local government or a state or local government official may not engage in government retaliation against an individual, a religious official when acting as such, or a religious organization for exercising the protections contained in Section 17-20-4, 63G-20-201, or 63G-20-301.

Enacted by Chapter 46, 2015 General Session

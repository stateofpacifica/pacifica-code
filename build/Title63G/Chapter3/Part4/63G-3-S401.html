Effective 7/1/2021
63G-3-401.  Office of Administrative Rules created -- Director.
(1) There is created within the Department of Government Operations the Office of Administrative Rules, to be administered by a director.
(2)
(a) The executive director shall appoint the director.
(b) The director shall hire, train, and supervise staff necessary for the office to carry out the provisions of this chapter.


Amended by Chapter 344, 2021 General Session

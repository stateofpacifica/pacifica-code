Effective 5/4/2022
63G-23-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Public official" means, except as provided in Subsection (3), the same as that term is defined in Section 36-11-102.
(2) "Public official" includes a judge or justice of:
(a) the Utah Supreme Court;
(b) the Utah Court of Appeals; or
(c) a district court.
(3) "Public official" does not include a local official or an education official as defined in Section 36-11-102.

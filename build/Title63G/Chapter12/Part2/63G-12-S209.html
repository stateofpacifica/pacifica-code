Effective 5/10/2016
63G-12-209.  Proficiency standards for English.
(1) A permit holder shall in good faith use best efforts to become proficient in the English language at or above the equivalent to an intermediate level on a language proficiency assessment test used by the State Board of Education for purposes of secondary school students.
(2) An undocumented individual shall pay the costs of complying with this section.


Amended by Chapter 144, 2016 General Session

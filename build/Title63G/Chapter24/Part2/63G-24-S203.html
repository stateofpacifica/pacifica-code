Effective 1/1/2021
63G-24-203.  Governor selection of nominee.
(1) The governor shall select a nominee based on:
(a) the applicant's fitness for office; and
(b) statutory requirements.
(2) The governor shall follow the process described in Section 67-1-2 to notify the Senate of a nominee for an appointed board member vacancy.


Enacted by Chapter 373, 2020 General Session

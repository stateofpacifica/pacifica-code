Effective 1/1/2021
63G-24-202.  Application.
(1) The application period for an appointed board member position shall last no fewer than 60 days.
(2) An applicant shall use the application feature on the governor's website described in Subsection 67-1-2.5(4) to apply for a vacant appointed board member position.
(3) The application feature described in Subsection (2) shall require the applicant to provide information including:
(a) the applicant's name;
(b) the applicant's current employment; and
(c) the applicant's affiliation with public and private entities, including employment, in the five years on or before the day on which the applicant submits the application.


Enacted by Chapter 373, 2020 General Session

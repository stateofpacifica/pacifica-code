Effective 7/1/2021
63G-10-501.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Executive director" means the individual appointed under Section 63A-1-105 as the executive director of the Department of Government Operations, created in Section 63A-1-104.
(2) "Risk management fund" means the fund created in Section 63A-4-201.
(3) "Risk manager" means the state risk manager appointed under Section 63A-4-101.5.

Effective 5/12/2020
63G-6a-118.  Adoption of rule relating to the procurement of design professional services.
     Each of the following shall adopt a rule relating to the procurement of design professional services, not inconsistent with the provisions of Part 15, Design Professional Services:
(1) an educational procurement unit;
(2) a conservation district;
(3) a local building authority;
(4) a local district;
(5) a special service district; and
(6) a public corporation.


Enacted by Chapter 257, 2020 General Session

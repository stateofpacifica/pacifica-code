Effective 3/28/2016
63G-6a-113.  Price based on established terms.
     A procurement unit acquiring a procurement item may establish the price of the procurement item based on:
(1) a price list, rate schedule, or price catalog:
(a) submitted by a vendor and accepted by the procurement unit; or
(b) mandated by the procurement unit or a federal agency; or
(2) a federal regulation for a health and human services program.


Enacted by Chapter 355, 2016 General Session

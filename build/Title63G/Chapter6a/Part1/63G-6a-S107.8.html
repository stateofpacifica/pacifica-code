Effective 5/12/2020
63G-6a-107.8.  Building board report to legislative interim committee.
     The building board shall make a report on or before July 1 of each year to a legislative interim committee designated by the Legislative Management Committee, created under Section 36-12-6, on the establishment, implementation, and enforcement of the rules made by the building board under this chapter.

Enacted by Chapter 257, 2020 General Session

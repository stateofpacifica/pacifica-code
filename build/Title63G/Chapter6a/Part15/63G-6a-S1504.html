Effective 5/12/2015
63G-6a-1504.  Selection as part of design-build or lease.
     Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, design professional services may be procured under Title 63A, Chapter 5b, Administration of State Facilities, as part of the services obtained in a design-build contract or as part of the services obtained in a lease contract for real property, if the qualifications of those providing the design professional services are part of the consideration in the selection process.

Amended by Chapter 218, 2015 General Session

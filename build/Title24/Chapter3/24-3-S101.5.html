Effective 5/4/2022
24-3-101.5.  Application of this chapter.
     The provisions of this chapter do not apply to property:
(1) that is subject to the retention requirements under Title 53, Chapter 20, Forensic Biological Evidence Preservation; or
(2) for which an agency has filed a notice of intent to seek forfeiture under Section 24-4-103.

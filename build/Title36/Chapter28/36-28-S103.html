Effective 5/13/2014
36-28-103.  Duties.
     The commission shall:
(1) comply with the rules of legislative interim committees;
(2) study and make recommendations to the Legislature on the following issues as they impact active duty servicemembers, veterans, and their dependents:
(a) reintegration from military to civilian status;
(b) employment;
(c) finances;
(d) education;
(e) health and mental health, including suicide prevention;
(f) military affairs, including the impact of military bases; and
(g) coordination of state and local government resources to assist active duty servicemembers, veterans, and their dependents; and
(3) consider the unique role of the Utah National Guard and its servicemembers in regard to the issues in Subsection (2).


Enacted by Chapter 150, 2014 General Session

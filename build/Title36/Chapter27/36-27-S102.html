36-27-102.  Legislative counsel to United States Senators.
(1) The Legislature may provide counsel to United States senators representing Utah.
(2) The Legislature may request that United States senators representing Utah provide a single response or periodic reports to the Legislature on:
(a) each senator's progress with or response to the counsel given under Subsection (1); or
(b) other issues as determined by the Legislature.


Enacted by Chapter 390, 2011 General Session

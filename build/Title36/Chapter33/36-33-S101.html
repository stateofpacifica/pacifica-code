Effective 9/1/2022
36-33-101.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Department" means the Department of Health and Human Services created in Section 26B-1-201.
(2) "Division" means the Division of Child and Family Services created in Section 80-2-201.
(3) "Panel" means the Child Welfare Legislative Oversight Panel created in Section 36-33-102.


Enacted by Chapter 334, 2022 General Session

48-3a-1102.  Application of this part.
(1) If a conflict arises between this part and another provision of this chapter, this part controls.
(2) Notwithstanding the other provisions of this part, on and after January 1, 2016:
(a) a professional services company may not designate series of transferable interests; and
(b) a limited liability company may not form a professional services company as a series of the limited liability company.


Enacted by Chapter 412, 2013 General Session

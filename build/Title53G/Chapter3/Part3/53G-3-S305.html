Effective 5/4/2022
53G-3-305.  Redistricting -- Local school board membership.
(1) Upon the creation of a new school district, the applicable legislative body shall redistrict the affected school districts in accordance with Section 20A-14-201.
(2) Except as provided in Section 53G-3-302, local school board membership in the affected school districts shall be determined under Title 20A, Chapter 14, Part 2, Election of Members of Local Boards of Education.

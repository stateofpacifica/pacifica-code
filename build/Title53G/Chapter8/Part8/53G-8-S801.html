Effective 5/14/2019
53G-8-801.  Definitions.
     As used in this section:
(1) "Bullying" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53G-9-601.
(2) "Law enforcement officer" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53-13-103.
(3) "Program" means the State Safety and Support Program established in Section 53G-8-802.


Enacted by Chapter 441, 2019 General Session

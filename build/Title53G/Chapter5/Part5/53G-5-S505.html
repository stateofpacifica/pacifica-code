Effective 5/14/2019
53G-5-505.  Tort liability.
(1) An employee of a charter school is a public employee and the charter school governing board is a public employer in the same manner as a local school board for purposes of tort liability.
(2) The charter school governing board, the nonprofit corporation under which the charter school is organized and managed, and the school are solely liable for any damages resulting from a legal challenge involving the operation of the school.


Amended by Chapter 293, 2019 General Session

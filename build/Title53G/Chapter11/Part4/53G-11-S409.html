Effective 1/24/2018
53G-11-409.  Legislative audit.
     After the conclusion of the 2018-2019 school year, subject to the prioritization of the Legislative Audit Subcommittee, the legislative auditor general shall conduct a review and issue a report on the extent to which the criminal background check procedures and ongoing monitoring described in this part adequately detect and identify the criminal histories of individuals who are employed by or volunteering in public schools.

Renumbered and Amended by Chapter 3, 2018 General Session

Effective 4/22/2020
53G-11-504.1.  Waiver of employee evaluation requirement.
(1) For the 2019-20 school year, a school district is not required to evaluate or report the performance of each school district employee in accordance with rules of the state board adopted in accordance with this part.
(2) This section supersedes any conflicting provisions of Utah law.


Enacted by Chapter 10, 2020 Special Session 3

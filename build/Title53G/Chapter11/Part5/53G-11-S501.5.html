Effective 5/14/2019
53G-11-501.5.  Legislative findings.
(1) The Legislature finds that the effectiveness of public educators can be improved and enhanced by providing specific feedback and support for improvement through a systematic, fair, and competent annual evaluation and remediation of public educators whose performance is inadequate.
(2) The state board and each local school board shall implement Sections 53G-11-501, 53G-11-506, 53G-11-507, 53G-11-508, 53G-11-509, 53G-11-510, and 53G-11-511 in accordance with Subsections 53E-2-302(7) and 53E-6-103(2)(a) and (b), to:
(a) allow the educator and the school district to promote the professional growth of the educator; and
(b) identify and encourage quality instruction in order to improve student academic growth.


Amended by Chapter 293, 2019 General Session

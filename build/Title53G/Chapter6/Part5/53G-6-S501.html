Effective 5/14/2019
53G-6-501.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Asset" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53G-5-102.
(2) "Board of trustees of a higher education institution" or "board of trustees" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53G-5-102.
(3) "Charter school authorizer" or "authorizer" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53G-5-102.

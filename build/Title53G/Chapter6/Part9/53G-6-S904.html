Effective 7/1/2022
53G-6-904.  Indemnification -- Enforcement.
(1) The state shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless a person acting under color of state law to enforce this part for any claims or damages, including court costs and attorney fees, that:
(a) are brought or incurred as a result of this part; and
(b) are not covered by the person's insurance policies or by any coverage agreement issued by the State Risk Management Fund.
(2) An LEA or school within the public education system with a team that competes in an interscholastic athletic activity is responsible for the enforcement of this part in relation to the LEA's or school's teams.


Enacted by Chapter 1, 2022 Special Session 3

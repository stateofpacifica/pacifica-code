Effective 7/1/2019
53G-7-502.  Schools to be free.
     Except as otherwise provided in this public education code, the public education system shall be free to an individual:
(1) between five and 18 years of age who is a resident; and
(2) over 18 who is domiciled in the state of Utah and has not completed requirements for a high school diploma.


Amended by Chapter 223, 2019 General Session

Effective 7/1/2019
53G-7-606.  Disposal of textbooks.
(1) An LEA may not dispose of textbooks without first notifying all other LEAs in the state of the LEA's intent to dispose of the textbooks.
(2) Subsection (1) does not apply to textbooks that have been damaged, mutilated, or worn out.
(3) The state board shall develop rules and procedures directing the disposal of textbooks.

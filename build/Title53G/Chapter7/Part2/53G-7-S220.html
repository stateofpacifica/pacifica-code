Effective 5/5/2021
53G-7-220.  School bus inspection policy.
     An LEA governing board that provides transportation shall establish a written policy that:
(1) requires a school bus driver to inspect the entire length of the interior of a school bus at the end of every route; and
(2) requires disciplinary action pursuant to LEA policies and practices for failure to perform the inspection described in Subsection (1).


Enacted by Chapter 290, 2021 General Session

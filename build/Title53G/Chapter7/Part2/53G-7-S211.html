Effective 1/24/2018
53G-7-211.  Display of American flag.
(1) Each local school board shall provide each school within the district with a suitable flagpole.
(2) The American flag shall be displayed on every school day and on every state and national holiday.
(3) The flag shall be maintained in a respectable condition.


Renumbered and Amended by Chapter 3, 2018 General Session

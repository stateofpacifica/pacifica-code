16-16-1006.  Redemption or repurchase.
     Property distributed to a member by a limited cooperative association, other than money, may be redeemed or repurchased as provided in the organic rules but a redemption or repurchase may not be made without authorization by the board of directors. The board may withhold authorization for any reason in its sole discretion. A redemption or repurchase is treated as a distribution for purposes of Section 16-16-1007.

Enacted by Chapter 363, 2008 General Session

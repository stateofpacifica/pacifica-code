16-16-1214.  Statement of dissolution.
(1) A limited cooperative association that has dissolved or is about to dissolve may deliver to the division for filing a statement of dissolution that states:
(a) the name of the association;
(b) the date the association dissolved or will dissolve; and
(c) any other information the association considers relevant.
(2) A person has notice of a limited cooperative association's dissolution on the later of:
(a) 90 days after a statement of dissolution is filed; or
(b) the effective date stated in the statement of dissolution.


Enacted by Chapter 363, 2008 General Session

16-15-104.  Registration required -- Certificate of registration.
(1) A business trust shall register with the division before doing business in the state.
(2) The certificate of registration of a business trust shall set forth:
(a) the name of the business trust;
(b) the period of its duration;
(c) the business purpose for which the business trust is organized;
(d) the information required by Subsection 16-17-203(1); and
(e) the name, signature, and street address of all trustees of the business trust.


Amended by Chapter 364, 2008 General Session

Effective 7/1/2021
63H-8-204.  Relation to certain acts.
(1) The corporation is exempt from:
(a)Title 51, Chapter 5, Funds Consolidation Act;
(b)Title 51, Chapter 7, State Money Management Act;
(c)Title 63A, Utah Government Operations Code;
(d)Title 63G, Chapter 6a, Utah Procurement Code;
(e)Title 63J, Chapter 1, Budgetary Procedures Act;
(f)Title 63J, Chapter 2, Revenue Procedures and Control Act; and
(g)Title 63A, Chapter 17, Utah State Personnel Management Act.
(2) The corporation shall comply with:
(a)Title 52, Chapter 4, Open and Public Meetings Act;
(b) Section 67-3-12; and
(c)Title 63G, Chapter 2, Government Records Access and Management Act.

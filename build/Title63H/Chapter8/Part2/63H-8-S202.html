63H-8-202.  Corporation as continuation of agency.
     The corporation is a continuation of the Utah Housing Finance Agency and shall:
(1) possess all rights, title, privileges, powers, immunities, property, and claims of the former Utah Housing Finance Agency; and
(2) fulfill and perform all obligations of the former Utah Housing Finance Agency, including obligations relating to outstanding bonds and notes.

Effective 3/22/2021
63H-1-208.  Former rail line.
(1) A former rail line automatically becomes included within a project area located at an air force base if:
(a) the authority acquires title to the former rail line; and
(b) a portion of the former rail line is adjacent to the project area.
(2) The authority may:
(a) develop the former rail line; or
(b) transfer title of all or part of the former rail line to another governmental entity or nonprofit entity.


Enacted by Chapter 414, 2021 General Session

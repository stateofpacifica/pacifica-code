Effective 7/1/2015
63H-7a-501.  Interoperability Division.
(1) This part is known as the "Interoperability Division."
(2) There is created within the authority the Interoperability Division, which shall be responsible for the duties of the authority as specified in this chapter.
(3) The executive director shall appoint a statewide interoperability coordinator with the approval of the board. The statewide interoperability coordinator shall be funded by the Department of Public Safety within appropriations to the Department of Public Safety for this purpose.

Effective 5/12/2020
63H-7a-206.5.  Report on implementing audit recommendations.
     By October 1, 2020, and again the following year by October 1, 2021, the authority shall report to the Public Utilities, Energy, and Technology Interim Committee and Retirement and Independent Entities Interim Committee of the Legislature on the authority's plan for and progress in implementing the recommendations of the December 2019 performance audit by the Office of the Legislative Auditor General, audit number 2019-15.

Enacted by Chapter 368, 2020 General Session

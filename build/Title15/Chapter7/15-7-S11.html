15-7-11.  Registration records -- Public inspection -- Location.
(1) Records, with regard to the ownership of or security interests in registered public obligations, are not subject to inspection or copying under any law of this state relating to the right of the public to inspect or copy public records.
(2) Registration records of the issuer may be maintained at such locations within or without this state as the issuer determines.


Enacted by Chapter 62, 1983 General Session

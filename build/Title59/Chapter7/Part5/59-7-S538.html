Effective 3/23/2022
59-7-538.  Carry forward of expired or repealed tax credit.
     When a nonrefundable corporate income tax credit under Part 6, Credits, expires or is repealed, the commission shall allow a taxpayer to carry forward any amount of the tax credit that remains for the period of time described in the tax credit for the taxable year in which the taxpayer first claimed the tax credit.

Enacted by Chapter 258, 2022 General Session

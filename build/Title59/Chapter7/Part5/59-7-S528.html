59-7-528.  Failure to make return or supply information -- Penalty.
     Each officer or employee of any corporation, who, without fraudulent intent, fails to make, render, sign, or verify any return, or to supply any information within the time required by or under the provisions of this chapter, shall be liable for a penalty as provided in Section 59-1-401, assessed and collected by the commission in the same manner as is provided in this chapter with regard to delinquent taxes.

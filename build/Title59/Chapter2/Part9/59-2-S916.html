59-2-916.  Tax for development of Colorado River Water Project.
     The governing body of each county, town, city, conservation district, and metropolitan district may levy a tax for participation in the development of the use of Colorado river water in Utah through the Colorado River-Great Basin Project. The tax shall be levied at the same time and collected in the same manner as other taxes.

Effective 5/13/2014
59-12-805.  Distribution of money collected from rural city hospital tax.
(1) Except as provided in Subsection 59-12-804(5) and Subsection (2), all money collected from a tax under Section 59-12-804 shall be distributed quarterly by the city legislative body to rural city hospitals.
(2) If there is more than one rural city hospital in a city, the money collected from a tax under Section 59-12-804 shall be distributed as determined by the city legislative body.


Amended by Chapter 50, 2014 General Session

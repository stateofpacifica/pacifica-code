Effective 1/1/2018
59-28-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Agreement" means the same as that term is defined in Section 59-12-102.
(2) "Certified service provider" means the same as that term is defined in Section 59-12-102.
(3) "Model 2 seller" means the same as that term is defined in Section 59-12-102.
(4) "Purchaser" means the same as that term is defined in Section 59-12-102.
(5) "Sales price" means the same as that term is defined in Section 59-12-102.
(6) "Seller" means the same as that term is defined in Section 59-12-102.

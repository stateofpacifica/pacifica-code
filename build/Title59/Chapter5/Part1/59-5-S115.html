Effective 5/5/2021
59-5-115.  Disposition of taxes collected -- Credit to General Fund.
     Except as provided in Section 51-9-305, 51-9-306, 51-9-307, 59-5-116, or 59-5-119, a tax imposed and collected under Section 59-5-102 shall be paid to the commission, promptly remitted to the state treasurer, and credited to the General Fund.

Amended by Chapter 401, 2021 General Session

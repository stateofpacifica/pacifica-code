Effective 3/23/2022
59-10-552.  Carry forward of expired or repealed tax credit.
     When a nonrefundable individual income tax credit, under Part 10, Nonrefundable Tax Credit Act, expires or is repealed, the commission shall allow a claimant, estate, or trust to carry forward any amount of the tax credit that remains for the period of time described in the tax credit for the taxable year in which the estate, claimant, or estate first claimed the tax credit.

Enacted by Chapter 258, 2022 General Session

Effective 5/5/2021
4-10-104.  Manufacture, repair, or wholesale sale of bedding, upholstered furniture, quilted clothing, or filling material -- Permit required.
(1) It is unlawful for a person to engage in the manufacture, repair, or wholesale sale of bedding, upholstered furniture, quilted clothing, or filling material without a permit issued by the department.
(2) Notwithstanding Subsection (1), a person may engage in the repair of quilted clothing without a permit issued by the department if that person is not otherwise required to obtain a permit issued by the department under this chapter or by department rule.


Amended by Chapter 295, 2021 General Session

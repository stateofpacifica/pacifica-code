4-30-112.  Suspension or revocation of license -- Grounds.
     The department is authorized to suspend or revoke the license of any livestock market or livestock market weighman who:
(1) violates any provision of this chapter or any rule made under this chapter; or
(2) engages in any fraudulent or deceitful activity.

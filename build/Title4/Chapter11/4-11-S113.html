Effective 7/1/2017
4-11-113.  Unlawful acts specified.
     It is unlawful for a person to:
(1) extract honey in any place where bees can gain access either during or after the extraction process;
(2) maintain any neglected or abandoned hives, apiary equipment, or appliances other than in an enclosure that prohibits the entrance of bees;
(3) raise bees without being registered with the department; or
(4) knowingly sell a colony, apiary equipment, or appliance that is inoculated with terminal disease pathogens.

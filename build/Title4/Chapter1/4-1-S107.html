Effective 7/1/2017
4-1-107.  Fees and late charges.
(1) If an annual registration, license, or other fee is imposed under any chapter of this title, it shall be determined by the department pursuant to Subsection 4-2-103(2).
(2) If the renewal of the registration or license is conditioned upon the payment of a renewal fee on or before a specified date, the department shall charge and collect the renewal fee and a late fee on any license or registration that is renewed after the date specified for renewal in the applicable chapter.
(3) The renewal fee and late fee shall be determined by the department pursuant to Subsection 4-2-103(2).

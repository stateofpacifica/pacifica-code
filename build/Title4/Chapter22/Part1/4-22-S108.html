Effective 7/1/2017
4-22-108.  Commission may require surety bond -- Payment of premium.
     The commission may require the administrator, or any commission employees, to post a surety bond conditioned for the faithful performance of the commission's official duties. The amount, form, and kind of such a bond shall be fixed by the commission and each bond premium shall be paid by the commission.

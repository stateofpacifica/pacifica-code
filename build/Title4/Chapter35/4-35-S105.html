Effective 5/12/2020
4-35-105.  Commissioner to act upon declaration of a plant pest emergency.
(1) The commissioner initiates operations to control a plant pest in the designated area or upon declaration of an infestation emergency.
(2) The commissioner and the members of the committee may suspend or terminate control operations upon a determination that the operations will not significantly reduce the plant pest population in the designated emergency area.


Amended by Chapter 326, 2020 General Session

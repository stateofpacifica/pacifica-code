Effective 5/12/2020
4-35-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Committee" means the Decision and Action Committee created by this chapter.
(2) "Department" means the Department of Agriculture and Food.
(3) "Fund" means the Plant Pest Fund created by Section 4-35-106.
(4) "Plant pest" means a biological agent that the commissioner determines to be a threat to agriculture in the state as described in Subsection 4-2-103(1)(k)(i).

Effective 7/1/2017
4-32-102.  Purpose declaration.
(1) It is the purpose of this chapter to provide a meat and poultry inspection program in the state at least equal to the programs imposed under the:
(a) Federal Meat Inspection Act, 21 U.S.C. Sec. 601 et seq.;
(b) Poultry Products Inspection Act, 21 U.S.C. Sec. 451 et seq.;
(c) Humane Slaughter Act, 7 U.S.C. Sec. 1901 et seq.; and
(d) the Egg Product Inspection Act, 21 U.S.C. 1031 et seq.
(2) The commissioner shall administer and enforce this chapter to accomplish the purpose described in Subsection (1).

Effective 7/1/2017
4-39-301.  Health and genetic purity requirements -- Proof of source.
     The department shall require:
(1) that each domesticated elk, including gametes, eggs, or sperm, imported into the state:
(a) test negative for the red deer genetic factor;
(b) be registered with gold or silver status with the North American Elk Breeders Association; or
(c) come from a state which has a red deer genetic factor prevention program approved by the department; and
(2) proof that the domesticated elk originates from a legal source as provided in Section 4-39-302.


Amended by Chapter 345, 2017 General Session

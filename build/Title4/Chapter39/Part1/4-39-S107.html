Effective 7/1/2017
4-39-107.  Powers of state veterinarian.
     The state veterinarian shall:
(1) set up periodic or ongoing surveillance programs considered necessary for:
(a) the recognition, control, monitoring, and elimination of infectious diseases and parasites; and
(b) monitoring genetic purity; and
(2) quarantine or make any disposition of diseased animals that the state veterinarian considers necessary for the control or eradication of that disease.


Amended by Chapter 345, 2017 General Session

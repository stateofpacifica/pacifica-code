Effective 7/1/2017
4-39-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Domesticated elk" means elk of the genus and species cervus elaphus, held in captivity and domestically raised for commercial purposes.
(2) "Domesticated elk facility" means a facility where only domesticated elk are raised.
(3) "Domesticated elk product" means any carcass, part of a carcass, hide, meat, meat food product, antlers, or any part of a domesticated elk.

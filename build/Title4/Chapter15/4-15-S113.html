Effective 7/1/2017
4-15-113.  Suspension or revocation -- Grounds -- Notice and hearing.
(1) Subject to Subsection (2), the department may suspend or revoke the license of any nursery, nursery outlet, or agent that violates Section 4-15-110 or 4-15-111.
(2) A suspension or revocation shall not be effective until after the nursery, nursery outlet, or agent is afforded notice and a hearing.

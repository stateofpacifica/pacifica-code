Effective 7/1/2017
4-18-201.  Title -- Definitions.
(1) This part is known as "Salinity Offset Fund."
(2) As used in this part, "Colorado River Salinity Offset Program" means a program, administered by the Division of Water Quality, allowing oil, gas, or mining companies and other entities to provide funds to finance salinity reduction projects in the Colorado River Basin by purchasing salinity credits as offsets against discharges made by the company under permits issued by the Division of Water Quality.


Enacted by Chapter 345, 2017 General Session

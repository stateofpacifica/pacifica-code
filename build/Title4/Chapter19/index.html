Chapter 19 - Rural Rehabilitation

  • Section 101 - Title.
  • Section 102 - Department responsible for conduct and administration of rural rehabilitation program.
  • Section 103 - Department authorized to approve and make grants and loans, acquire property, and lease or operate property.
  • Section 104 - Loans -- Not to exceed period of 10 years -- Agricultural Advisory Board executive committee to approve loans and renewals, methods of payments, and interest rates -- Guidelines in fixing interest rates declared.
  • Section 105 - Utah Rural Rehabilitation Fund.

4-37-601.  Enforcement and penalties.
(1) Any violation of this chapter is a class B misdemeanor and may be grounds for revocation of the license or denial of any future license as determined by the department.
(2) A violation of any rule made under this chapter may be grounds for revocation of the license or denial for future license as determined by the department.

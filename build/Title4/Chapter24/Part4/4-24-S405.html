Effective 5/5/2021
4-24-405.  Travel permit in lieu of brand inspection certificate -- Fees.
(1) The department may issue a permit upon the payment of a fee determined by the department pursuant to Subsection 4-2-103(2), in lieu of a certificate of brand inspection, for the transport of a show horse, show mule, or show cattle transported from a place within this state to a place outside the state.
(2) The words "travel permit" shall be stamped or printed on the permit.
(3) A permit:
(a) shall accompany a show animal while the show animal is in transit and shall identify the show animal to which the permit applies by age, sex, color, brand, and scars; and
(b) is valid for the calendar year of the date of issuance, which date shall appear on the permit.


Amended by Chapter 295, 2021 General Session

Effective 7/1/2022
4-46-301.  LeRay McAllister Critical Land Conservation Program.
(1) There is created a program entitled the "LeRay McAllister Critical Land Conservation Program."
(2) Funding for the program shall be a line item in the budget of the board. The line item shall be nonlapsing.

Effective 7/1/2022
4-46-103.  Application of chapter to wildlife issues.
     This chapter may not be construed or applied to supersede or interfere with the powers and duties of the Division of Wildlife Resources or the Wildlife Board under Title 23, Wildlife Resources Code of Utah, over:
(1) conservation and management of protected wildlife within the state;
(2) a program or initiative to restore and conserve habitat for fish and wildlife; or
(3) acquisition, ownership, management, and control of real property or a real property interest, including a leasehold estate, an easement, a right-of-way, or a conservation easement.


Enacted by Chapter 68, 2022 General Session

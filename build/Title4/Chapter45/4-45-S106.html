Effective 5/14/2019
4-45-106.  Civil action available.
     In addition to and distinct from any other remedy at law, an individual may bring a civil action, in a competent court of jurisdiction, for damages resulting from a violation of this chapter, including economic, noneconomic, or consequential damages.

Enacted by Chapter 329, 2019 General Session

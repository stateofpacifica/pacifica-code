Effective 1/1/2020
53-10-214.  Reporting requirements.
     The bureau shall submit a record received pursuant to Section 53-10-208.1 for all nonextraditable warrants issued for violent felonies as defined in Section 76-3-203.5 and all nonextraditable warrants issued for knowingly failing to register for a sexual offense pursuant to Section 77-41-107 to the National Crime Information Center within 48 hours of receipt, excluding Saturdays, Sundays, and legal holidays.

Enacted by Chapter 406, 2019 General Session

Effective 5/5/2021
53-2a-106.  Coordination for state development in a flood plain.
     Any state agency that plans to develop or construct a building within a flood plain shall consult and coordinate with the division to ensure compliance with minimum standards of the National Flood Insurance Program, 42 U.S.C. Chapter 50, Subchapter I.

Enacted by Chapter 106, 2021 General Session

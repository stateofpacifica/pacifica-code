Effective 5/5/2021
53-2a-1403.  Emergency operations plan.
(1) Each county shall create and maintain an emergency operations plan.
(2) Each city, town, and metro township shall:
(a) create and maintain an emergency operations plan; or
(b) adopt the emergency operations plan created by the county in which the city, town, or metro township is located.


Enacted by Chapter 106, 2021 General Session

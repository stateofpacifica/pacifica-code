53-2a-305.  Agreements not affected by this part.
     Nothing in this part may be construed:
(1) to limit the state, a county, municipality, local district, special service district, or interlocal entity from entering into an agreement allowed by law for public safety and related purposes; or
(2) to affect an agreement to which the state, a county, municipality, local district, special service district, or interlocal entity is a party.

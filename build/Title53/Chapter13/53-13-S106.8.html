Effective 5/13/2014
53-13-106.8.  State and local law enforcement officers and federal employees -- Use of correctional and communication facilities.
     State and local government agencies may not allow any federal agency access to or use of the correctional and communication facilities and equipment of any state or local law enforcement agency without the express written consent of the appropriate responsible official of the state or local law enforcement agency.

Enacted by Chapter 317, 2014 General Session

Effective 5/5/2021
53-6-401.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Council" means the Peace Officer Standards and Training Council created in Section 53-6-106.
(2) "Qualifying canine certifying entity" means an entity that certifies law enforcement canines and law enforcement canine handlers in accordance with the standards developed under Section 53-6-403.


Enacted by Chapter 201, 2021 General Session

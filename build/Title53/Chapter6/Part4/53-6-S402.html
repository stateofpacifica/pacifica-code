Effective 5/5/2021
53-6-402.  Law enforcement canine and handler certification.
(1) Each law enforcement canine in the state shall be initially certified and annually recertified by a qualifying canine certifying entity.
(2) Each law enforcement canine handler in the state shall be initially certified and annually recertified by a qualifying canine certifying entity.


Enacted by Chapter 201, 2021 General Session

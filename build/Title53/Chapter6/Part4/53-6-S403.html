Effective 5/5/2021
53-6-403.  Canine and handler certification standards and criteria--Rules.
     In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the council shall establish and maintain required standards for the training, certification, and recertification of:
(1) law enforcement canines; and
(2) law enforcement canine handlers.


Enacted by Chapter 201, 2021 General Session

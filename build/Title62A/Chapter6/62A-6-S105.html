62A-6-105.  Persons who may give informed consent.
     For purposes of this chapter, the following persons may give informed consent to sterilization:
(1) a person who is the subject of sterilization, if he is capable of giving informed consent; and
(2) a person appointed by the court to give informed consent on behalf of a subject of sterilization who is incapable of giving informed consent.


Enacted by Chapter 1, 1988 General Session

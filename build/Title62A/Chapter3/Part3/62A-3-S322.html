Effective 12/3/2018
62A-3-322.  Medical cannabis use by a vulnerable adult or guardian.
     A peace officer or an employee or agent of the division may not solicit or provide, and a court may not order, emergency services for a vulnerable adult based solely on:
(1) the vulnerable adult's possession or use of cannabis in accordance with Title 26, Chapter 61a, Utah Medical Cannabis Act; or
(2) the guardian of the vulnerable adult assisting with the use of or possessing cannabis in accordance with Title 26, Chapter 61a, Utah Medical Cannabis Act.


Enacted by Chapter 1, 2018 Special Session 3

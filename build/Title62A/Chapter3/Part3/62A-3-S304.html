62A-3-304.  Cooperation by caretaker.
     A caretaker, facility, or other institution shall, regardless of the confidentiality standards of the caretaker, facility, or institution:
(1) report abuse, neglect, or exploitation of a vulnerable adult in accordance with this chapter;
(2) cooperate with any Adult Protective Services investigation;
(3) provide Adult Protective Services with access to records or documents relating to the vulnerable adult who is the subject of an investigation; or
(4) provide evidence in any judicial or administrative proceeding relating to a vulnerable adult who is the subject of an investigation.


Amended by Chapter 91, 2008 General Session

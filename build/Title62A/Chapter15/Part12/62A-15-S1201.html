Effective 5/9/2017
62A-15-1201.  Statement of legislative intent.
     To address the serious public health crisis of substance use disorder related deaths and life-threatening opioid addiction, and to allow and enable caring relatives to seek essential treatment and intervention, as may be necessary, on behalf of a sufferer of a substance use disorder, the Legislature enacts the Essential Treatment and Intervention Act.

Enacted by Chapter 408, 2017 General Session

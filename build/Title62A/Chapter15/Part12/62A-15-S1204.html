62A-15-1204.  Criteria for essential treatment and intervention.
     A district court shall order an individual to undergo essential treatment for a substance use disorder when the district court determines by clear and convincing evidence that the individual:
(1) suffers from a substance use disorder;
(2) can reasonably benefit from the essential treatment;
(3) is unlikely to substantially benefit from a less-restrictive alternative treatment; and
(4) presents a serious harm to self or others.

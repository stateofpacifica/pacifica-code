Effective 5/5/2021
62A-15-1501.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Account" means the Survivors of Suicide Loss Account created in Section 62A-15-1502.
(2)
(a) "Cohabitant" means an individual who lives with another individual.
(b) "Cohabitant" does not include a relative.
(3) "Relative" means father, mother, husband, wife, son, daughter, sister, brother, grandfather, grandmother, uncle, aunt, nephew, niece, grandson, granddaughter, first cousin, mother-in-law, father-in-law, brother-in-law, sister-in-law, son-in-law, or daughter-in-law.


Amended by Chapter 277, 2021 General Session

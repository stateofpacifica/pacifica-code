Effective 5/4/2022
62A-5-501.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Complainant" means a person who initiates a complaint.
(2) "Complaint" means a complaint initiated with the ombudsman identifying a person who has violated the rights and privileges of an individual with a disability.
(3) "Ombudsman" means the ombudsman appointed in Section 62A-5-502.
(4) "Rights and privileges of an individual with a disability" means the rights and privileges of an individual with a disability described in Subsections 62A-5b-103(1) through (3).


Enacted by Chapter 220, 2022 General Session

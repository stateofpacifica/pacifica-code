Effective 5/10/2016
63A-1-109.5.  Department authority to operate a division or office as an internal service fund agency.
     Subject to Section 63A-1-114 and provisions governing internal service funds or internal service fund agencies under Title 63J, Chapter 1, Budgetary Procedures Act, the department may operate a division or office described in Section 63A-1-109 as an internal service fund agency.

Amended by Chapter 193, 2016 General Session

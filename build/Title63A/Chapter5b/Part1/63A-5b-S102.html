Effective 5/4/2022
63A-5b-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Capitol hill facilities" means the same as that term is defined in Section 63C-9-102.
(2) "Capitol hill grounds" means the same as that term is defined in Section 63C-9-102.
(3) "Compliance agency" means the same as that term is defined in Section 15A-1-202.
(4) "Director" means the division director, appointed under Section 63A-5b-302.
(5) "Division" means the Division of Facilities Construction and Management created in Section 63A-5b-301.
(6) "Institution of higher education" means an institution listed in Subsection 53B-2-101(1).
(7) "Trust lands administration" means the School and Institutional Trust Lands Administration established in Section 53C-1-201.
(8) "Utah Board of Higher Education" means the Utah Board of Higher Education established in Section 53B-1-402.

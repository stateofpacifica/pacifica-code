Effective 5/12/2020
63A-5b-610.  Transfer from project reserve money.
(1) With the approval of and through an appropriation by the Legislature, the division shall transfer at least $100,000 annually from the project reserve money to the General Fund to pay for personal service expenses associated with the management of construction projects.
(2) With the approval of and as directed by the Legislature, the division shall transfer additional money from the project reserve money to pay administrative costs associated with the management of construction projects and other division responsibilities.


Renumbered and Amended by Chapter 152, 2020 General Session

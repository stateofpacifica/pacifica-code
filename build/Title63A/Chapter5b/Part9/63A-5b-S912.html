63A-5b-912.  Report to Infrastructure and General Government Appropriations Subcommittee.
     The division shall, on or before the third Wednesday in November of every even-numbered year, present a written report to the Infrastructure and General Government Appropriations Subcommittee that identifies state land and buildings that are no longer needed and can be sold by the state.

Renumbered and Amended by Chapter 152, 2020 General Session

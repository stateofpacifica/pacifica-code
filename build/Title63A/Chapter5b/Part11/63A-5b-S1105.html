63A-5b-1105.  Testing and inspection firm requirements.
     The director shall ensure that any person performing testing and inspection work governed by the American Society for Testing Materials Standard E-329 on a public building under the director's supervision:
(1) fully complies with the American Society for Testing Materials standard specifications for an agency engaged in the testing and inspection of materials known as ASTM E-329; and
(2) carries a minimum of $1,000,000 of errors and omissions insurance.


Enacted by Chapter 152, 2020 General Session

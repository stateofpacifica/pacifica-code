Effective 5/5/2021
63A-4-101.1.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Captive insurance company" means the same as that term is defined in Section 31A-37-102.
(2) "Covered entity" means a participating entity of:
(a) the Risk Management Fund; or
(b) any captive insurance company created by the risk manager.

Effective 5/4/2022
63A-17-107.  Services and fees -- Submission to rate committee.
     The director shall, before charging a rate for services provided by the division's internal service fund to an executive branch agency:
(1) submit the proposed rates and cost analysis to the rate committee established in Section 63A-1-114; and
(2) obtain the approval of the Legislature as required under Section 63J-1-410.


Amended by Chapter 169, 2022 General Session

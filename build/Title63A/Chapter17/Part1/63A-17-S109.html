63A-17-109.  Study of wildland fire related pay plans.
     By no later than June 30, 2021, the division shall complete a comprehensive comparison of federal, state, and municipal wildland fire agencies or departments to recommend whether salary ranges should be adjusted for state employed wildland firefighters.

Enacted by Chapter 97, 2021 General Session

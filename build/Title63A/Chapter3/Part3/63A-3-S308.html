Effective 5/14/2019
63A-3-308.  Judicial review -- Effect on lien.
(1) Agency and judicial review of decisions from hearings conducted under this part are subject to review in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 4, Administrative Procedures Act.
(2) The state or other governmental entity may retain in its possession a debtor's tax overpayment or refund while a decision from a hearing conducted under this part is being reviewed by an agency, court, or other authority of the state pursuant to Title 63G, Chapter 4, Administrative Procedures Act.


Amended by Chapter 84, 2019 General Session

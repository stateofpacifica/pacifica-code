Effective 5/4/2022
63A-2-501.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Advisory council" means the Educational Interpretation and Translation Services Procurement Advisory Council established in Section 63A-2-502.
(2) "Contract" means a contract entered into by the division for interpretation or translation services in accordance with Section 63A-2-502.
(3) "Local education agency" or "LEA" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53E-1-102.
(4) "State board" means the State Board of Education.


Enacted by Chapter 354, 2022 General Session

63A-15-706.  Action by political subdivision governing body.
     A political subdivision governing body that receives a recommendation in accordance with Section 63A-15-704 shall:
(1) review the recommendation; and
(2) take further action in accordance with a political subdivision's governing ordinance, bylaws, or other applicable governing rule.

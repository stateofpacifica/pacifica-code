Effective 5/13/2014
10-5-106.  Funds for which budget prepared.
     The mayor shall prepare for each budget year a budget for:
(1) the town general fund, including state allocated road funds;
(2) special revenue funds;
(3) debt service funds;
(4) capital improvement funds; and
(5) enterprise funds.


Amended by Chapter 176, 2014 General Session

Effective 5/14/2019
10-5-109.  Adoption of budgets -- Filing.
(1) Before June 30 of each year, or September 1 in the case of a property tax increase under Sections 59-2-919 through 59-2-923, the council shall by resolution or ordinance adopt a budget for the ensuing fiscal year for each fund for which a budget is required under this chapter.
(2) The council shall file a copy of the final budget for each fund with the state auditor within 30 days after adoption.


Amended by Chapter 322, 2019 General Session

10-8-11.  Streets -- Encroachments, lighting, sprinkling, cleaning.
     They may regulate the use of streets, alleys, avenues, sidewalks, crosswalks, parks, and public grounds, install, prevent, or remove obstructions and encroachments thereon, and provide for the lighting, sprinkling, and cleaning of the same.

Amended by Chapter 2, 2017 Special Session 1

Effective 5/5/2021
10-9a-529.  Specified public utility located in a municipal utility easement.
     A specified public utility may exercise each power of a public utility under Section 54-3-27 if the specified public utility uses an easement:
(1) with the consent of a municipality; and
(2) that is located within a municipal utility easement described in Subsections 10-9a-103(41)(a) through (e).

10-9a-509.7.  Transferable development rights.
(1) A municipality may adopt an ordinance:
(a) designating sending zones and receiving zones within the municipality; and
(b) allowing the transfer of a transferable development right from a sending zone to a receiving zone.
(2) A municipality may not allow the use of a transferable development right unless the municipality adopts an ordinance described in Subsection (1).


Amended by Chapter 231, 2012 General Session

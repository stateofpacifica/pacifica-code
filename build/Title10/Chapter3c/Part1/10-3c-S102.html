Effective 5/12/2015
10-3c-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Municipal services district" means a local district created in accordance with Title 17B, Chapter 2a, Part 11, Municipal Services District Act.
(2) "Metro township" means a metro township incorporated in accordance with Chapter 2a, Part 4, Incorporation of Metro Townships and Unincorporated Islands in a County of the First Class on and after May 12, 2015.


Enacted by Chapter 352, 2015 General Session

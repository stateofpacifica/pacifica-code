Effective 5/12/2015
10-3c-205.  Fees.
(1) A metro township may impose a fine, fee, or charge.
(2) For a metro township of which the electors at an election under Section 10-2a-404 chose a metro township that is included in a municipal services district and has limited municipal powers, or if a metro township subsequently joins a municipal services district, the municipal services district of which a metro township is a part shall, upon request by the metro township, collect on behalf of the metro township all fines, fees, charges, levies, and other payments imposed by the metro township.


Enacted by Chapter 352, 2015 General Session

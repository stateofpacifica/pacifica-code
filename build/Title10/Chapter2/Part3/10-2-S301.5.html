Effective 5/12/2015
10-2-301.5.  Classification of metro townships according to population.
(1) Each metro township, as defined in Section 10-2a-403, shall be classified according to its population, as provided in this section.
(2) A metro township with a population of:
(a) 1,000 or more is a metro township of the first class; and
(b) fewer than 1,000 is a metro township of the second class.


Enacted by Chapter 352, 2015 General Session

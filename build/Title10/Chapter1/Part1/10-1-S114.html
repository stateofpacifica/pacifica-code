Effective 5/10/2016
10-1-114.  Repealer.
     Title 10, Chapter 1, General Provisions; Chapter 2, Classification, Boundaries, Consolidation, and Dissolution of Municipalities; Chapter 3, Municipal Government; Chapter 5, Uniform Fiscal Procedures Act for Utah Towns; and Chapter 6, Uniform Fiscal Procedures Act for Utah Cities, are repealed, except as provided in Section 10-1-115.

Amended by Chapter 348, 2016 General Session

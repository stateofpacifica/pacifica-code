Effective 5/12/2015
10-2a-409.  Unincorporated island annexation -- Notice and recording-- Applicable provisions.
(1) If the annexation of an unincorporated island into an eligible city passes, the legislative body of the eligible city shall comply with Section 10-2-425.
(2) The following provisions apply to an annexation under this part:
(a) Section 10-2-420;
(b) Section 10-2-421;
(c) Section 10-2-422;
(d) Section 10-2-426; and
(e) Section 10-2-428.


Enacted by Chapter 352, 2015 General Session

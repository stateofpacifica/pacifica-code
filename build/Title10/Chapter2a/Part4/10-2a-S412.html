Effective 5/12/2015
10-2a-412.  Notification to lieutenant governor of election of officers.
     Within 10 days of the canvass of final election of metro township, city, or town officers under Section 10-2a-411, the county clerk shall send written notice to the lieutenant governor of the name and position of each officer elected and the term for which each has been elected.

Enacted by Chapter 352, 2015 General Session

10-3-921.  Fines -- Collection by bail commissioner -- Disposition.
(1) In addition to the duty of fixing bail, a bail commissioner shall have power to collect and receipt money tendered in payment of the fine of a person serving sentence in default of the payment of such fine, when the court is closed.
(2) Money collected by a bail commissioner shall be delivered to the court that issued the commitment order within three days of receipt of the money.


Amended by Chapter 283, 1990 General Session

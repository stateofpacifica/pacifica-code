Effective 5/12/2020
64-13e-103.1.  Calculating the actual state incarceration rate.
(1) Before September 15 of each year, the department shall calculate, and inform each county and CCJJ of the actual state daily incarceration rate.
(2) The actual state daily incarceration rate may not be less than the rate presented to the Executive Appropriations Committee of the Legislature for purposes of setting the appropriation for the department's budget.


Enacted by Chapter 410, 2020 General Session

79-2-601.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Administrative costs" means the costs of administering the initiative, including costs for staffing, rent, data processing, legal, finance, accounting, travel, maintenance, and office supplies.
(2) "Director" means the director of the initiative who is appointed under Section 79-2-602.
(3) "Division" means the Division of Wildlife Resources created in Section 23-14-1.
(4) "Initiative" means the Watershed Restoration Initiative created in Section 79-2-602.
(5) "Restoration" means to assist the recovery of ecosystems and ecosystem services that have been mismanaged, degraded, or destroyed.
(6) "Watershed" means the geographical surface area that drains water into a stream, river, or other body of water.

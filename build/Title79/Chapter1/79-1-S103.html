Effective 7/1/2021
79-1-103.  Coordination council.
(1) There is created a coordination council that consists of:
(a) the executive director of the department;
(b) the executive director of the Department of Environmental Quality;
(c) the commissioner of the Department of Agriculture and Food;
(d) the director of the Public Lands Policy Coordinating Office; and
(e) the director of the Office of Energy Development.
(2) The coordination council shall:
(a) rotate the position of chair among the members; and
(b) meet at least monthly.
(3) The coordination council shall discuss methods to enhance the coordination of regulation and services of the five entities.


Enacted by Chapter 280, 2021 General Session

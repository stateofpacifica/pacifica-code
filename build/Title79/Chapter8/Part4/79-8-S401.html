Effective 7/1/2022
79-8-401.  Creation and purpose of infrastructure grant program.
(1) There is created the Outdoor Recreational Infrastructure Grant Program administered by the division.
(2) The division may seek to accomplish the following objectives in administering the infrastructure grant program:
(a) build, maintain, and promote recreational infrastructure to provide greater access to low-cost outdoor recreation for the state's citizens;
(b) encourage residents and nonresidents of the state to take advantage of the beauty of Utah's outdoors;
(c) encourage individuals and businesses to relocate to the state;
(d) promote outdoor exercise; and
(e) provide outdoor recreational opportunities to an underserved community in the state.
(3) The advisory committee shall advise and make recommendations to the division regarding infrastructure grants.

Effective 7/1/2022
79-5-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Committee" means the Utah Outdoor Recreation Infrastructure Advisory Committee created in Section 79-7-206.
(2) "Division" means the Division of Outdoor Recreation.
(3) "Recreational trail" or "trail" means a multi-use path used for:
(a) muscle-powered activities, including:
(i) bicycling;
(ii) cross-country skiing;
(iii) walking;
(iv) jogging; and
(v) horseback riding; and
(b) uses compatible with the uses described in Subsection (3)(a), including the use of an electric assisted bicycle or motor assisted scooter, as defined in Section 41-6a-102.

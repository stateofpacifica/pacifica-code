Effective 5/10/2016
58-86-201.  State certification required.
(1) State certification is required to engage in the practice of commercial interior design except as specifically provided in Section 58-1-307 or 58-86-206.
(2) The division shall grant state certification to a person who qualifies under this chapter to engage in the practice of commercial interior design as a state certified commercial interior designer.


Enacted by Chapter 294, 2016 General Session

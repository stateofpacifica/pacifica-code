Effective 5/10/2016
58-86-203.  Term of state certification -- Expiration -- Renewal.
(1)
(a) The division shall grant state certification under this chapter in accordance with a two-year renewal cycle established by rule.
(b) The division may by rule extend or shorten a renewal period by as much as one year to stagger the renewal cycles it administers.
(2) At the time of renewal, an applicant for renewal shall:
(a) submit an application in a form prescribed by the division;
(b) pay a fee determined by the department under Section 63J-1-504; and
(c) provide satisfactory evidence of having completed the continuing education requirements described in Section 58-86-204.


Enacted by Chapter 294, 2016 General Session

58-86-205.  Grounds for denial of state certification -- Disciplinary proceedings.
     Grounds for refusing to issue state certification to an applicant, for refusing to renew state certification to an applicant, for revoking, suspending, restricting, or placing on probation the state certification of an individual certified under this chapter, for issuing a public or private reprimand to an individual certified under this chapter, and for issuing a cease and desist order shall be in accordance with Section 58-1-401.

Enacted by Chapter 294, 2016 General Session

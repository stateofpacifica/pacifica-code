58-64-502.  Unprofessional conduct.
     "Unprofessional conduct" includes:
(1) using any deception detection instrument that does not meet criteria and standards established by rule by the division; and
(2) using any deception detection instrument that does not make a permanent recording as required under Section 58-64-601.


Amended by Chapter 154, 2020 General Session

Effective 5/4/2022
58-88-204.  Administrative inspections of a dispensing practice -- Penalties.
(1) The division shall conduct audits and inspections of licensed dispensing practices in accordance with standards established by the division by rule.
(2) Penalties for a violation of this part, including fines and citations, shall be issued by the division under:
(a) Section 58-1-502; and
(b) the dispensing practitioner's respective licensing chapter.


Enacted by Chapter 353, 2022 General Session

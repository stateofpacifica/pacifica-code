Effective 5/9/2017
58-70a-301.  Licensure required -- License classifications.
(1) A license is required to engage in practice as a physician assistant, except as specifically provided in Section 58-70a-305 or 58-1-307.
(2) The division shall issue to an individual who qualifies under this chapter a license in the classification of physician assistant.


Amended by Chapter 309, 2017 General Session

58-9-707.  Records of trustee -- Contents.
(1) The trustee shall keep an orderly record of all agreements.
(2) The record shall contain:
(a) the name and address of the person making a payment;
(b) the date and the amount of each payment made;
(c) the date and amount of each withdrawal and to whom paid; and
(d) the name of the bank or trust company depository.


Amended by Chapter 144, 2007 General Session

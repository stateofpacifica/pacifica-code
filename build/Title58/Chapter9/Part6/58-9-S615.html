Effective 5/8/2018
58-9-615.  Accepting remains for alkaline hydrolysis.
(1) A funeral service establishment may not make or enforce a rule requiring that human remains be placed in a casket before alkaline hydrolysis.
(2) A funeral service establishment may not refuse to accept human remains for alkaline hydrolysis because the human remains are not in a casket.


Enacted by Chapter 326, 2018 General Session

58-24b-305.  License denial -- Discipline -- Cease and desist order.
     In accordance with Section 58-1-401, the division may:
(1) refuse to issue a license to an applicant;
(2) refuse to renew a license;
(3) revoke, suspend, or restrict a license;
(4) place a license on probation;
(5) issue a public or private reprimand to a licensee; or
(6) issue a cease and desist order.


Enacted by Chapter 220, 2009 General Session

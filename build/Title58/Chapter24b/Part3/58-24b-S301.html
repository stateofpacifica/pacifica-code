58-24b-301.  Authority to practice physical therapy.
     A person may not engage in the practice of physical therapy, unless the person is:
(1) licensed under this chapter and practices within the scope of that license; or
(2) exempted from the licensing requirements of this chapter under Section 58-1-307 or 58-24b-304.


Amended by Chapter 238, 2016 General Session

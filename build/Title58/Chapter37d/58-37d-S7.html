Effective 5/12/2015
58-37d-7.  Seizure and forfeiture.
     Chemicals, equipment, supplies, vehicles, aircraft, vessels, and personal and real property used in furtherance of a clandestine laboratory operation are subject to seizure and forfeiture under the procedures and substantive protections of Title 24, Forfeiture and Disposition of Property Act.

Amended by Chapter 258, 2015 General Session

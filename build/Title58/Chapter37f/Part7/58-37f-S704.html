58-37f-704.  Entering certain convictions into the database.
     Beginning October 1, 2016, if the division receives a report from a court under Subsection 58-37-8(1)(e) or 58-37-8(2)(g), the division shall daily enter into the database the information supplied in the report.

Amended by Chapter 116, 2022 General Session

58-49-5.  Certification of persons currently qualified.
     The requirements of Subsections 58-49-4(1), (2), and (3) are waived and a certificate shall be issued by the division upon application and payment of the appropriate fees by any person who, before December 31, 1986, has provided to the division proof that on May 1, 1985, the person was and is currently registered by the Commission on Dietetic Registration.

Amended by Chapter 339, 2020 General Session

Effective 5/12/2015
58-42a-303.  Term of license -- Expiration -- Renewal.
(1) The division shall issue each license under this chapter in accordance with a two-year renewal cycle established by division rule.
(2) The division may by rule extend or shorten a renewal period by as much as one year to stagger the renewal cycles it administers.
(3) Each license automatically expires on the expiration date shown on the license unless the licensee renews it in accordance with Section 58-1-308.

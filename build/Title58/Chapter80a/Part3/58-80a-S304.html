Effective 5/9/2017
58-80a-304.  Term of certification -- Expiration -- Renewal.
(1) Notwithstanding Section 58-1-308, the division shall issue each certification under this chapter in accordance with a three-year renewal cycle established by rule. The division may by rule extend or shorten a renewal cycle by as much as two years to stagger the renewal cycles that the division administers.
(2) Each certification automatically expires on the expiration date shown on the certification unless the certified medical language interpreter renews the certification in accordance with Section 58-1-308.


Amended by Chapter 305, 2017 General Session

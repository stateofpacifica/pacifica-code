58-80a-401.  Grounds for denial of certification -- Disciplinary proceedings.
     Grounds for refusing to issue a certification to an applicant, for refusing to renew the certification of a certified medical language interpreter, for revoking, suspending, restricting, or placing on probation the certification of a certified medical language interpreter, for issuing a public or private reprimand to a certified medical language interpreter, and for issuing a cease and desist order shall be in accordance with Section 58-1-401.

Enacted by Chapter 127, 2010 General Session

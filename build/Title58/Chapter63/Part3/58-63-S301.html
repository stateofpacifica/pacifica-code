58-63-301.  Licensure required -- License classifications.
(1) A license is required to engage in the practice of a contract security company, an armored car company, an armored car security officer, armed private security officer, or unarmed private security officer, except as otherwise provided in Section 58-1-307, 58-63-304, or 58-63-310.
(2) The division shall issue to a person who qualifies under this chapter a license in the following classifications:
(a) contract security company;
(b) armored car company;
(c) armored car security officer;
(d) armed private security officer; or
(e) unarmed private security officer.


Amended by Chapter 246, 2008 General Session

58-77-201.  Board.
(1) There is created the Licensed Direct-entry Midwife Board consisting of:
(a) four licensed direct-entry midwives; and
(b) one member of the general public.
(2) The board shall be appointed and serve in accordance with Section 58-1-201.
(3)
(a) The duties and responsibilities of the board shall be in accordance with Sections 58-1-202 and 58-1-203.
(b) The board shall designate one of its members on a permanent or rotating basis to:
(i) assist the division in reviewing complaints concerning the unlawful or unprofessional conduct of a licensed direct-entry midwife; and
(ii) advise the division in its investigation of these complaints.
(4) A board member who has, under Subsection (3), reviewed a complaint or advised in its investigation may be disqualified from participating with the board when the board serves as a presiding officer in an adjudicative proceeding concerning the complaint.
(5) Qualified faculty, board members, and other staff of direct-entry midwifery learning institutions may serve as one or more of the licensed Directed-entry midwives on the board.


Amended by Chapter 167, 2013 General Session

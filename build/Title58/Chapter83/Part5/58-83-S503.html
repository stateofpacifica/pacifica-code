58-83-503.  Unlawful conduct.
     Unlawful conduct includes, in addition to the definition in Section 58-1-501:
(1) online prescribing, dispensing or facilitating of a legend drug not authorized by the division in accordance with Section 58-83-306; or
(2) online prescribing, dispensing or facilitating of a controlled substance.


Enacted by Chapter 180, 2010 General Session

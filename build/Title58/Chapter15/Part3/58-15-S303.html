58-15-303.  Exemptions to chapter.
(1) In addition to the exemptions described in Section 58-1-307, this chapter does not apply to:
(a) a facility of a recognized church or denomination that cares for the sick and suffering by mental or spiritual means if no drug or material remedy is used in the care provided; or
(b) the superintendent of the Utah State Developmental Center described in Section 62A-5-201.
(2) Any facility or person exempted under this section shall comply with each statute and rule on sanitation and life safety.

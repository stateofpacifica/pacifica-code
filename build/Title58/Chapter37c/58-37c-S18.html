58-37c-18.  Recordkeeping requirements for sale of crystal iodine.
(1) Any person licensed to engage in a regulated transaction and who sells crystal iodine to another person shall:
(a) comply with the recordkeeping requirements of Section 58-37c-10;
(b) require photo identification of the purchaser;
(c) obtain from the purchaser a signature on a certificate of identification provided by the seller; and
(d) obtain from the purchaser a legible fingerprint, preferably of the right thumb, which shall be placed on the certificate next to the purchaser's signature.
(2) Any failure to comply with Subsection (1) is a class B misdemeanor.


Amended by Chapter 21, 1999 General Session

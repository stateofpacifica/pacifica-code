58-37c-7.  Controlled substance precursor license.
(1) The division shall issue to persons qualified under the provisions of this chapter and rules adopted pursuant to Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, a controlled substance precursor license.
(2) It is unlawful for a person to engage in the distribution, sale, or transfer, or in the purchase or obtaining of a controlled substance precursor in a regulated transaction without being licensed or excepted from licensure under this chapter.


Amended by Chapter 240, 2010 General Session

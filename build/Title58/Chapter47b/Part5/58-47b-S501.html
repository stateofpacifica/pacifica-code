58-47b-501.  Unlawful conduct.
     "Unlawful conduct" includes:
(1) practicing, engaging in, or attempting to practice or engage in massage therapy without holding a current license as a massage therapist or a massage apprentice under this chapter;
(2) advertising or representing himself as practicing massage therapy when not licensed to do so; and
(3) massaging, touching, or applying any instrument or device by a licensee in the course of practicing or engaging in massage therapy to the:
(a) genitals;
(b) anus; or
(c) breasts of a female patron, except when a female patron requests breast massage, as may be further defined by division rule, and signs a written consent form, which must also include the signature of a parent or legal guardian if the patron is a minor, authorizing the procedure and outlining the reason for it before the procedure is performed.


Amended by Chapter 318, 2018 General Session

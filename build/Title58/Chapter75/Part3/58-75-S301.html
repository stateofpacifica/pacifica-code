58-75-301.  Licensure required -- Issuance of licenses -- Effect on insurers.
(1) Beginning January 1, 2002, and except as provided in Sections 58-1-307 and 58-75-304, a license is required to engage in the practice of genetic counseling.
(2) The division shall issue to any person who qualifies under this chapter a license to practice genetic counseling.
(3) Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to require payment from insurers for genetic counseling services.


Enacted by Chapter 100, 2001 General Session

Effective 7/1/2020
22-3-416.  Receipts normally apportioned -- Other financial instrument or arrangement.
(1) A fiduciary shall allocate receipts from or related to a financial instrument or arrangement not otherwise addressed by this chapter.
(2) The allocation must be consistent with Sections 22-3-414 and 22-3-415.


Enacted by Chapter 495, 2019 General Session

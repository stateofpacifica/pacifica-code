Effective 7/1/2020
22-3-103.  Scope.
     Except as otherwise provided in the terms of a trust or this chapter, this chapter applies to:
(1) a trust or estate; and
(2) a life estate or other term interest in which the interest of one or more persons will be succeeded by the interest of one or more other persons.

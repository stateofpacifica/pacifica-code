63B-1b-301.  State treasurer may sell, assign, or liquidate agency bonds -- Marketing plan required.
(1) One or more authorizing agencies may from time to time request the state treasurer to sell, assign, or liquidate agency bonds on behalf of the authorizing agencies as provided in Section 63B-1b-302.
(2)
(a) Agency bonds shall be sold, assigned, transferred, or liquidated by the state treasurer pursuant to a marketing plan provided by the state treasurer under Section 63B-1b-302.
(b) The governor or the governor's designee and the appropriate authorizing agency shall approve the marketing plan, in writing.

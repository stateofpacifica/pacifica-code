Effective 5/10/2016
63B-18-302.  Authorizations to dispose of property.
     The Legislature intends that:
(1) the Department of Workforce Services may, in coordination with the Division of Facilities Construction and Management, sell a Temporary Placement Office in Salt Lake City, Utah, and three vacated buildings in Logan, Utah; and
(2) sales be at fair market value.


Amended by Chapter 301, 2016 General Session

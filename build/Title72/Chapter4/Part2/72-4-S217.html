Effective 5/9/2017
72-4-217.  Minuteman Highway.
(1) There is established the Minuteman Highway composed of the existing Highway 85 in Salt Lake County from Porter Rockwell Boulevard to 7800 South in West Jordan.
(2) In addition to other official designations, the Department of Transportation shall designate the portions of the highway identified in Subsection (1) as the Minuteman Highway on future state highway maps.


Enacted by Chapter 82, 2017 General Session

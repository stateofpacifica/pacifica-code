Effective 5/14/2019
72-4-219.  Grand Army of the Republic Highway.
(1) There is established the Grand Army of the Republic Highway composed of the existing Highway 6 within the state.
(2) In addition to other official designations, the Department of Transportation shall designate the highway identified in Subsection (1) as the Grand Army of the Republic Highway on future state highway maps.


Enacted by Chapter 209, 2019 General Session

Technically renumbered to avoid duplication of section number used in SB 101, Chapter 82.

Effective 5/5/2021
72-2-206.  Department authority to contract.
     The department may, upon approval of the commission:
(1) make all contracts, execute all instruments, and do all things necessary or convenient to provide financial assistance for transportation projects or publicly owned infrastructure projects in accordance with this chapter; and
(2) enter into and perform the contracts and agreements with entities concerning the planning, construction, lease, or other acquisition, installation, or financing of transportation projects or publicly owned infrastructure projects.


Amended by Chapter 121, 2021 General Session

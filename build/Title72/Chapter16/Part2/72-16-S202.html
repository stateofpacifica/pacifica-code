Effective 7/1/2021
72-16-202.  Hiring of director.
(1)
(a) The executive director, subject to approval by the committee, shall hire a director.
(b) The executive director may remove the director at the executive director's will.
(2) The director shall:
(a) be experienced in administration and possess additional qualifications as determined by the committee and the executive director; and
(b) receive compensation in accordance with Title 63A, Chapter 17, Utah State Personnel Management Act.


Amended by Chapter 345, 2021 General Session

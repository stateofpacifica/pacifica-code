Effective 5/14/2019
72-16-103.  Scope and administration.
(1) The provisions of this chapter apply to any amusement ride in the state.
(2) In accordance with the provisions of this chapter, the committee:
(a) shall administer this chapter; and
(b) has jurisdiction over any amusement ride in the state.


Enacted by Chapter 244, 2019 General Session

Effective 7/1/2015
72-9-107.  Medical exemptions for farm vehicle operators.
     Except as provided in Section 53-3-206, an operator of a farm vehicle or combination of farm vehicles is exempt from additional requirements for physical qualifications, medical examinations, and medical certification if the farm vehicle or combination of farm vehicles being operated is:
(1) under 26,001 pounds gross vehicle weight rating;
(2) not operated as a commercial motor vehicle in accordance with Subsection 53-3-102(7)(b)(ii); and
(3) not operated as an interstate commercial motor vehicle.

Effective 7/1/2021
63J-4-202.  Appointment of executive director, state planning coordinator, and state homelessness coordinator.
(1)
(a) The governor shall appoint, to serve at the governor's pleasure:
(i) an executive director of the office; and
(ii) a state homelessness coordinator.
(b) The state homelessness coordinator shall serve as:
(i) an advisor to the governor on homelessness issues; and
(ii) the chief administrative officer of the Office of Homeless Services created in Section 35A-1-202.
(2) The governor shall establish the executive director's salary within the salary range fixed by the Legislature in Title 67, Chapter 22, State Officer Compensation.

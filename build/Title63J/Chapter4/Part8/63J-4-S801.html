Effective 5/28/2021
63J-4-801.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "American Rescue Plan Act" means the American Rescue Plan Act, Pub. L. 117-2.
(2) "COVID-19" means:
(a) severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2; or
(b) the disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2.
(3) "COVID-19 emergency" means the spread of COVID-19 that the World Health Organization declared a pandemic on March 11, 2020.
(4) "Grant program" means the COVID-19 Local Assistance Matching Grant Program established in Section 63J-4-802.
(5) "Local government" means a county, city, town, metro township, local district, or special service district.
(6) "Review committee" means the COVID-19 Local Assistance Matching Grant Program Review Committee established in Section 63J-4-803.


Enacted by Chapter 4, 2021 Special Session 1

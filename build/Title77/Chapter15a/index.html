Chapter 15a - Exemptions From Death Penalty In Capital Cases

  • Section 101 - Intellectually disabled defendant not subject to death penalty -- Defendant with subaverage functioning not subject to death penalty if confession not corroborated.
  • Section 102 - "Intellectually disabled" defined.
  • Section 103 - Court may raise issue of intellectual disability at any time.
  • Section 104 - Hearing -- Notice -- Stay of proceeding -- Examinations of defendant -- Scope of examination -- Report -- Procedures.
  • Section 105 - Defendant's wilful failure to cooperate -- Expert testimony regarding intellectual disability is barred.
  • Section 106 - Limitations on admitting intellectual disability examination evidence.

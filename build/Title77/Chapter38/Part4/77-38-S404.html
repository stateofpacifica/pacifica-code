Effective 5/14/2019
77-38-404.  Scope of part.
     This part governs the disclosure of a confidential communication to a victim advocate, except that:
(1) if Title 53B, Chapter 28, Part 2, Confidential Communications for Institutional Advocacy Services Act, applies, that part governs; and
(2) if Part 2, Confidential Communications for Sexual Assault Act, applies, that part governs.


Enacted by Chapter 361, 2019 General Session

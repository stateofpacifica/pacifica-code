Effective 1/1/2023
77-38-607.  Use of assigned address -- Release of information.
(1) The commission shall forward all mail that the office receives at the assigned address for a program participant to the program participant's actual address.
(2) The commission shall provide, at the request of a program participant or a state or local government entity, confirmation of an individual's status as a program participant.
(3) Except as provided in Sections 77-38-611, 77-38-612, and 77-38-613, the office may not disclose a program participant's actual address to any person.


Enacted by Chapter 215, 2022 General Session

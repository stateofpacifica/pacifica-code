Effective 5/9/2017
77-43-107.  Classification of information.
     Notwithstanding Title 63G, Chapter 2, Government Records Access and Management Act, information under Subsection 77-43-103(4) that is collected and released under Subsection 77-43-108(4) is public information, unless otherwise restricted under Subsection 77-43-103(1).

Enacted by Chapter 282, 2017 General Session

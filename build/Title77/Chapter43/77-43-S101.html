Effective 5/9/2017
77-43-101.  Title.
(1) This chapter is known as the "Child Abuse Offender Registry."
(2) This chapter applies to all child abuse offenders in the custody of the Department of Corrections or on parole or probation on May 9, 2017, or who enter this state on or after May 9, 2017.


Enacted by Chapter 282, 2017 General Session

Effective 5/5/2021
77-27-5.2.  Board authority to order removal from Sex and Kidnap Offender Registry.
(1) If the board grants a pardon for a conviction that is the basis for an individual's registration on the Sex and Kidnap Offender Registry, the board shall issue an order directing the Department of Corrections to remove the individual's name and personal information relating to the pardoned conviction from the Sex and Kidnap Offender Registry.
(2) An order described in Subsection (1), issued by the board, satisfies the notification requirement described in Subsection 77-41-113(1)(b).


Enacted by Chapter 410, 2021 General Session

77-2-2.2.  Signing and filing of information.
(1) The prosecuting attorney shall sign all informations.
(2) The prosecuting attorney may:
(a) sign the information in the presence of a magistrate; or
(b) present and file the information in the office of the clerk where the prosecution is commenced upon the signature of the prosecuting attorney.

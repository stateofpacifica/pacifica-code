Effective 5/9/2017
77-10a-5.  Grand jurors -- Qualification and selection -- Limits on disclosure.
(1) Grand jurors shall meet the qualifications provided for jurors generally in Title 78B, Chapter 1, Part 1, Jury and Witness Act. Grand jurors shall be selected from the prospective jury list as provided in Section 78B-1-107.
(2) The names of grand jurors are classified as protected records under Title 63G, Chapter 2, Government Records Access and Management Act.


Amended by Chapter 115, 2017 General Session

Effective 5/4/2022
77-40a-404.  Use of expunged records -- Individuals -- Use in civil actions.
     A record expunged under this chapter or Section 77-27-5.1 may be released to or viewed by:
(1) the petitioner or an individual who receives an automatic expungement under Section 77-40a-201;
(2) a law enforcement officer, who was involved in the case, for use solely in the officer's defense of a civil action arising out of the officer's involvement with the petitioner in that particular case; and
(3) parties to a civil action arising out of the expunged incident if the information is kept confidential and utilized only in the action.

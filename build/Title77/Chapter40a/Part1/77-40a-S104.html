Effective 5/4/2022
77-40a-104.  Rulemaking authority.
     In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the department may make rules to:
(1) implement procedures for processing an automatic expungement;
(2) implement procedures for applying for certificates of eligibility;
(3) specify procedures for receiving a certificate of eligibility; and
(4) create forms and determine information necessary to be provided to the bureau.

Effective 5/10/2016
77-7a-106.  Prohibited Activities.
     An officer is prohibited from:
(1) using a body-worn camera for personal use;
(2) making a personal copy of a recording created while on duty or acting in an official capacity as a law enforcement officer;
(3) retaining a recording of any activity or information obtained while on duty or acting in an official capacity as a law enforcement officer;
(4) duplicating or distributing a recording except as authorized by the employing law enforcement agency; and
(5) altering or deleting a recording in violation of this chapter.


Enacted by Chapter 410, 2016 General Session

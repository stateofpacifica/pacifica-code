Effective 7/1/2021
77-38b-201.  Law enforcement responsibility for collecting restitution information.
     A law enforcement agency investigating criminal conduct that would constitute a felony or a misdemeanor shall include all information about restitution for any potential victim in the investigative report, including information about:
(1) whether a claim for restitution exists;
(2) the basis for the claim; and
(3) the estimated or actual amount of the claim.


Enacted by Chapter 260, 2021 General Session

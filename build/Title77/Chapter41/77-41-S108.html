77-41-108.  Classification of information.
     Notwithstanding Title 63G, Chapter 2, Government Records Access and Management Act, information under Subsection 77-41-103(4) that is collected and released under Subsection 77-41-110(4) is public information, unless otherwise restricted under Subsection 77-41-103(1).

Enacted by Chapter 145, 2012 General Session

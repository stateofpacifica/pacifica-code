Effective 5/9/2017
25-6-406.  Applicability of chapter.
(1) This chapter applies when any transfer occurs after the effective date of this act.
(2) The amendments to this chapter that take effect on May 9, 2017:
(a) apply to a transfer made or obligation incurred on or after May 9, 2017;
(b) do not apply to a transfer made or obligation incurred before May 9, 2017; and
(c) do not apply to a right of action that has accrued before May 9, 2017.
(3) For purposes of Subsection (2), a transfer is made and an obligation is incurred at the time provided in Section 25-6-302.

Part 2 - Liability Relating to Recreational Use

  • Section 201 - Owner owes no duty of care or duty to give warning -- Exceptions.
  • Section 202 - Use of private land without charge -- Effect.
  • Section 203 - Land leased to state or political subdivision for recreational purposes.
  • Section 204 - Liability not limited where willful or malicious conduct involved or admission fee charged.
  • Section 205 - Person using land of another not relieved from duty to exercise care.

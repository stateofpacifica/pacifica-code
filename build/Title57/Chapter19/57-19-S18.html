57-19-18.  Investigation -- Publication.
(1) The director may make any investigations or requests for information, within or outside of this state, that he considers necessary:
(a) to determine whether any registration under this chapter should be granted, denied, or revoked;
(b) to determine whether any person has violated or is about to violate any of the provisions of this chapter or any rule or order under this chapter; or
(c) to aid in the enforcement of this chapter.
(2) The director may publish information concerning any violation of this chapter or any rule or order under this chapter.


Enacted by Chapter 73, 1987 General Session

Effective 5/14/2019
57-10-11.  Requirement to conform to the Utah Coordinate System.
     A person, corporation, municipality, county, or state agency that is utilizing an existing county coordinate system or establishing a new countywide coordinate network for surveying or mapping, or both, shall, by January 1, 2021, conform to the current Utah Coordinate System, along with the current federal coordinate update.

Amended by Chapter 35, 2019 General Session

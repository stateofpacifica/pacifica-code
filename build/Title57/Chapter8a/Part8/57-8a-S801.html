Effective 5/4/2022
57-8a-801.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Charging system" means a device that is:
(a) used to provide electricity to an electric or hybrid electric vehicle; and
(b) designed to ensure a safe connection between the electric grid and the vehicle.
(2) "General electrical contractor" means the same as that term is defined in Section 58-55-102.
(3) "Residential electrical contractor" means the same as that term is defined in Section 58-55-102.

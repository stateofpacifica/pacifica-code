57-2a-6.  Form of certificate.
     The form of a certificate of acknowledgment used by a person whose authority is recognized under Section 57-2a-3 shall be accepted if:
(1) the certificate is in a form prescribed by the laws or rules of this state;
(2) the certificate is in a form prescribed by the laws or regulations applicable in the place where the acknowledgment is taken; or
(3) the certificate contains the words "acknowledged before me," or their substantial equivalent.


Enacted by Chapter 155, 1988 General Session

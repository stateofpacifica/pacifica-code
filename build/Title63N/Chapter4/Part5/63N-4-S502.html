Effective 5/4/2022
63N-4-502.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Advisory committee" means the Rural Online Working Hubs Grant Advisory Committee created in Section 63N-4-505.
(2) "Coworking and innovation center" means a facility designed to provide individuals with the infrastructure and equipment to participate in the online workforce.
(3) "Entity" means a county, city, nonprofit organization, or institution of higher education.
(4) "Grant" means a grant awarded as part of the Rural Coworking and Innovation Center Grant Program created in Section 63N-4-503.
(5) "Grant program" means the Rural Coworking and Innovation Center Grant Program created in Section 63N-4-503.
(6) "Rural area" means any area in any county in the state except Salt Lake, Utah, Davis, Weber, Washington, Cache, Tooele, and Summit counties.

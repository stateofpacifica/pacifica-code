Effective 7/1/2022
63N-6-103.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Board" means the board of directors of the corporation.
(2) "Corporation" means the Utah Capital Investment Corporation created under Section 63N-6-301.
(3) "Restricted account" means the Utah Capital Investment Restricted Account created in Section 63N-6-204.
(4) "Utah fund of funds" means a limited liability company established under Section 63N-6-401.

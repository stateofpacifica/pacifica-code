Effective 5/4/2022
63N-6-402.  Compensation from the Utah fund of funds to the corporation -- Transfer to restricted account.
(1) The corporation shall be compensated for its involvement in the Utah fund of funds through the payment of the management fee described in Section 63N-6-305.
(2)
(a) On or before June 30, 2022, the Utah fund of funds shall transfer $15,000,000 to the state treasurer.
(b) The state treasurer shall deposit the money described in Subsection (2)(a) into the restricted account.


Amended by Chapter 298, 2022 General Session

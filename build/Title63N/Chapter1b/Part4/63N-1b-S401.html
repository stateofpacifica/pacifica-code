Effective 7/1/2022
63N-1b-401.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "State institution of higher education" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53B-3-102.
(2) "Subcommittee" means the Women in the Economy Subcommittee created in Section 63N-1b-402.

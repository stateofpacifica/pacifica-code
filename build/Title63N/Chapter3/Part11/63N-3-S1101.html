63N-3-1101.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Grant" means a grant awarded under Section 63N-3-1102.
(2) "Program" means the Manufacturing Modernization Grant Program created in Section 63N-3-1102.
(3) "Targeted industry" means an industry or group of industries targeted by the GO Utah board under Section 63N-3-111 for economic development in the state.

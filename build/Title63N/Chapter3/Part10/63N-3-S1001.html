Effective 7/1/2022
63N-3-1001.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Business entity" means a for-profit or nonprofit entity.
(2) "Grant" means a grant awarded as part of the Economic Assistance Grant Program created in Section 63N-3-1002.
(3) "Grant program" means the Economic Assistance Grant Program created in Section 63N-3-1002.

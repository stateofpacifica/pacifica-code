Effective 5/4/2022
63N-2-102.  Purpose.
     This part is enacted to:
(1) foster and develop targeted industries in the state, to stimulate community-focused economic growth, and to diversify and catalyze the state's economy;
(2) create high paying employment opportunities in the state;
(3) provide tax credits to attract new commercial projects and new jobs in economic development zones in the state; and
(4) provide a cooperative and unified working relationship between state and local economic development efforts.


Amended by Chapter 200, 2022 General Session

Effective 5/10/2016
63N-2-507.  Assigning convention incentive.
(1) A host local government that enters into an agreement with the office may, by resolution, assign a convention incentive to a community reinvestment agency, in accordance with rules adopted by the office.
(2) A host local government that adopts a resolution assigning a convention incentive under Subsection (1) shall provide a copy of the resolution to the office.


Amended by Chapter 350, 2016 General Session

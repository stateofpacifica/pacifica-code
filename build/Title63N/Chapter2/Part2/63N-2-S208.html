Effective 7/1/2020
63N-2-208.  Duration of designation.
(1) Each enterprise zone has a duration of five years.
(2) On or after January 1, 2021, neither a municipality nor a county may reapply for an enterprise zone designation for an enterprise zone that has reached the end of the enterprise zone's five-year duration.


Amended by Chapter 360, 2020 General Session

53B-17-1201.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Commission" means the SafeUT and School Safety Commission established in Section 53B-17-1203.
(2) "University Neuropsychiatric Institute" means the mental health and substance abuse treatment institute within the University of Utah Hospitals and Clinics.

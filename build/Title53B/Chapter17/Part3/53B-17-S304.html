Effective 5/8/2018
53B-17-304.  Failure to comply with chapter is a misdemeanor.
     A person who commits the following violations is guilty of a class B misdemeanor:
(1) failure or neglect to give notice required under Subsection 53B-17-301(1); or
(2) failure or neglect to forward a body upon request under Subsection 53B-17-301(3).


Amended by Chapter 148, 2018 General Session

Effective 5/4/2022
53B-35-101.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter, "council" means the Higher Education and Corrections Council created in Section 53B-35-201.

Enacted by Chapter 147, 2022 General Session

Technically renumbered to avoid duplication of enacted Chapter also enacted in SB 226, Chapter 461, HB 333, Chapter 362, and HB 346, Chapter 368.

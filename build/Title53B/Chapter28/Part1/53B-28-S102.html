53B-28-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter, "institution" means a public or private postsecondary institution that is located in Utah, including an institution of higher education listed in Section 53B-1-102.

Enacted by Chapter 188, 2017 General Session

Technically renumbered to avoid duplication of section number also enacted in HB54, Chapter 440.

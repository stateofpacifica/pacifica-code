Effective 5/14/2019
53B-28-302.  Code of conduct violation -- Report of sexual violence.
     An institution may not sanction a student for a code of conduct violation related to the use of drugs or alcohol if:
(1) the student is:
(a) a victim of an act of sexual violence; or
(b) a witness to an act of sexual violence;
(2) the student reports to the institution, in good faith, a covered allegation related to the act of sexual violence described in Subsection (1); and
(3) the institution learns of the student's code of conduct violation due to the student's report described in Subsection (2).


Enacted by Chapter 307, 2019 General Session

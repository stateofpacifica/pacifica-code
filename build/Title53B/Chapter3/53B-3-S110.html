53B-3-110.  Fines and forfeitures -- Disposition.
     All fines and forfeitures collected by any justice court judge and one-half of all the fines and forfeitures collected by the clerk of any district court for a violation of any of this chapter are remitted to the state treasurer to be credited to the general operating fund of the state institution of higher education complaining of the violation.

Amended by Chapter 198, 1996 General Session

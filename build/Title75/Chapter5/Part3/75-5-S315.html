75-5-315.  Copies of public records furnished to veterans administration.
     When a copy of any public record is required by the veterans administration to be used in determining the eligibility of any person to participate in benefits made available by the veterans administration, the official custodian of such public record shall without charge provide the applicant for such benefits or any person acting on behalf of the authorized representative of the veterans administration with a certified copy of such record.

Enacted by Chapter 150, 1975 General Session

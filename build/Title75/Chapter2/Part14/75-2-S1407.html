75-2-1407.  Revocation.
(1) An electronic will may revoke all or part of a previous will.
(2) All or part of an electronic will is revoked by:
(a) a subsequent will that revokes all or part of the electronic will expressly or by inconsistency; or
(b) a physical act if it is established by a preponderance of the evidence that the testator, with the intent of revoking all or part of the will, performed the act or directed another individual who performed the act in the testator's physical presence.


Enacted by Chapter 1, 2020 Special Session 6

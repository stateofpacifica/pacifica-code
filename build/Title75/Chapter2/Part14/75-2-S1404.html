75-2-1404.  Choice of law regarding execution.
     A will executed electronically but not in compliance with Subsection 75-2-1405(1) is an electronic will under this part if executed in compliance with the law of the jurisdiction where the testator is:
(1) physically located when the will is signed; or
(2) domiciled or resides when the will is signed or when the testator dies.


Enacted by Chapter 1, 2020 Special Session 6

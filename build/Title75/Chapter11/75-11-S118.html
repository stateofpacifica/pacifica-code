75-11-118.  Relation to Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act.
     This chapter modifies, limits, or supersedes the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act, 15 U.S.C. Sec. 7001 et seq., but does not modify, limit, or supersede Section 101(c) of that act or 15 U.S.C. Sec. 7001(c), or authorize electronic delivery of any of the notices described in Section 103(b) of that act or 15 U.S.C. Sec. 7003(b).

Enacted by Chapter 16, 2017 General Session

Technically renumbered for proper placement.

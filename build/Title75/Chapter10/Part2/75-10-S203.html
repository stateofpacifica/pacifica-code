75-10-203.  Presumption of unlimited authority.
     Subject to Section 75-10-205, and unless the terms of the instrument creating a power of appointment manifest a contrary intent, the power is:
(1) presently exercisable;
(2) exclusionary; and
(3) except as otherwise provided in Section 75-10-204, general.


Enacted by Chapter 125, 2017 General Session

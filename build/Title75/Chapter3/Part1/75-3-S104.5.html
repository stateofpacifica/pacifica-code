Effective 5/12/2020
75-3-104.5.  Notice to the Office of Recovery Services.
     Within 30 days after the day on which a person files an application or a petition for probate under this chapter for a decedent who was at least 55 years old, the court shall provide notice of the application or petition to the Office of Recovery Services created in Section 62A-1-105 for purposes of presentation or enforcement of a lien or claim under Section 26-19-405.

Amended by Chapter 205, 2020 General Session

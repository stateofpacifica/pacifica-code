Effective 5/14/2019
17C-1-805.  Agency to provide notice of hearings.
(1) Each agency shall provide notice, in accordance with this part, of each:
(a) development impediment hearing;
(b) plan hearing; or
(c) budget hearing.
(2) The notice required under Subsection (1) may be combined with the notice required for any of the other hearings if the hearings are combined under Section 17C-1-802.

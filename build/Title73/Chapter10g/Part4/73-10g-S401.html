Effective 5/4/2022
73-10g-401.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Great Salt Lake watershed" means the area comprised of the Great Salt Lake, the Bear River watershed, the Jordan River watershed, the Utah Lake watershed, the Weber River watershed, and the West Desert watershed.
(2) "Integrated water assessment" means an integrated surface and ground water assessment for the Great Salt Lake watershed developed under Section 73-10g-402.
(3) "Work plan" means the plan developed under Section 73-10g-403 to develop and implement an integrated water assessment.

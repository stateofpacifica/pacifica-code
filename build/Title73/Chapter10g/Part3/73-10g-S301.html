Effective 5/12/2020
73-10g-301.  Title -- Implementation of part.
(1) This part is known as the "Watershed Councils Act."
(2) This part shall be liberally construed to:
(a) develop diverse and balanced stakeholder forums for discussion of water policy and resource issues at watershed and state levels that are not vested with regulatory, infrastructure financing, or enforcement powers or responsibilities; and
(b) use local expertise and resources found in universities and other research institutions or in regional, state, and federal agencies.


Enacted by Chapter 309, 2020 General Session

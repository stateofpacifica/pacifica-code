Effective 5/12/2015
73-10g-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Board" means the Board of Water Resources;
(2) "Division" means the Division of Water Resources; and
(3) "Restricted account" means the Water Infrastructure Restricted Account created in Section 73-10g-103.


Enacted by Chapter 458, 2015 General Session

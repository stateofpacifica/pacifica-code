73-28-401.  Analysis of benefits and costs -- Allocation of costs.
(1) The board shall allocate project costs based on the economic costs and benefits of the developed water and electricity among the following water uses:
(a) municipal and industrial;
(b) electricity;
(c) public recreation; and
(d) fish and wildlife.
(2) The state shall pay the nonreimbursable project costs allocated to recreation and fish and wildlife.


Enacted by Chapter 216, 2006 General Session

Effective 5/12/2020
73-31-103.  Notice -- Website.
(1) A notice required under this chapter shall be posted in accordance with Subsection 73-3-6(1) and to a water bank's website, unless otherwise specified.
(2) The board may create and oversee a website for the purpose of making water banking information available to the public.


Enacted by Chapter 342, 2020 General Session

Effective 5/13/2014
73-3-26.  Violations -- Penalty.
(1) A person engaged in well drilling, as described in Subsection 73-3-25(1)(c), is guilty of a crime punishable under Section 73-2-27 if the person does not have a current license to engage in well drilling, as provided by this title.
(2) Each day that a violation under Subsection (1) continues is a separate offense.


Amended by Chapter 369, 2014 General Session

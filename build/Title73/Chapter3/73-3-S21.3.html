Effective 5/4/2022
73-3-21.3.  Study of preferences during temporary water shortage emergency.
(1) The state engineer shall study how the state should address preferred uses of water during a temporary water shortage emergency including issues such as:
(a) the process for determining whether and how a water use may be given preference; and
(b) compensation for holders of water rights that are affected by preferences being given to certain water uses.
(2) The state engineer may work with stakeholders in conducting the study under this section.
(3) The state engineer shall report the state engineer's findings, including any recommended legislation, to the Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim Committee by no later than the 2022 November interim meeting of the Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim Committee.


Enacted by Chapter 311, 2022 General Session

Effective 7/1/2022
73-18a-12.  Rules made -- Subject to approval by Department of Environmental Quality.
     The division, after notifying the commission, may make rules under Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, which are necessary for the carrying out of duties, obligations, and powers conferred on the division by this chapter. These rules shall be subject to review and approval by the Department of Environmental Quality. This approval shall be recorded as part of the rules.

Amended by Chapter 68, 2022 General Session

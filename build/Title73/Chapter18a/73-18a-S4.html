Effective 7/1/2022
73-18a-4.  Marine toilets -- Pollution control devices required -- Rules established by division.
(1) Every marine toilet on a vessel used or operated upon the waters of this state shall be equipped with an approved pollution control device in operative condition.
(2) The division, after notifying the commission, shall make rules in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, as provided in this chapter, establishing criteria or standards for definition and approval of acceptable pollution control devices for vessels.

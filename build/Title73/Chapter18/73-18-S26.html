Effective 7/1/2020
73-18-26.  Resident aquatic invasive species fee -- Amount -- Deposit.
(1) In addition to the registration fee imposed under Section 73-18-7, there is imposed an annual resident aquatic invasive species fee of $10 on a motorboat or sailboat required to be registered under Section 73-18-7.
(2) The fee imposed under Subsection (1) shall be deposited into the Aquatic Invasive Species Interdiction Account created in Section 23-27-305.


Amended by Chapter 195, 2020 General Session

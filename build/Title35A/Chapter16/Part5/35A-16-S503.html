Effective 5/4/2022
35A-16-503.  Rules.
     In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the office shall make rules governing:
(1) the submission of an overflow plan under Subsection 35A-16-502(1);
(2) the review of an overflow plan for purposes of determining compliance under Subsection 35A-16-502(4);
(3) the process of sending a notice of noncompliance under Subsection 35A-16-502(5); and
(4) the location, establishment, and operation of a temporary overflow shelter under Subsections 35A-16-502(6)(b)(ii) and (c).


Enacted by Chapter 403, 2022 General Session

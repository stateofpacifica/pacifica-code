Effective 7/1/2016
35A-2-201.  Services provided in economic service areas.
     Economic service areas shall:
(1) through their employment centers, be the primary provider of services and support under Chapter 3, Employment Support Act; and
(2) provide access to and assess eligibility for services or training under Chapter 5, Training and Workforce Improvement Act.


Amended by Chapter 296, 2016 General Session

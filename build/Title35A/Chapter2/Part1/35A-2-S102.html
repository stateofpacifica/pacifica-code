Effective 7/1/2016
35A-2-102.  Directors of economic service areas -- Appointment.
(1) The executive director shall appoint a director to oversee each economic service area.
(2) A director appointed under this section may be removed from that position at the will of the executive director.
(3) A director shall be experienced in administration and possess additional qualifications as determined by the executive director, and as provided by law.
(4) The director shall report on a regular basis to the State Workforce Development Board on the delivery of services in the economic service area.


Amended by Chapter 296, 2016 General Session

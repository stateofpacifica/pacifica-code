Effective 5/5/2021
35A-8-310.  Application -- Retroactivity.
(1) The provisions of Laws of Utah 2021, Chapter 339, apply to any claim for which a court of competent jurisdiction has not issued a final unappealable judgment or order.
(2) The Legislature finds that the provisions of Laws of Utah 2021, Chapter 339:
(a) do not enlarge, eliminate, or destroy vested rights; and
(b) clarify legislative intent.

Effective 10/1/2016
35A-13-501.  General provisions.
(1) This part is known as "Services for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing."
(2) For the purposes of this part:
(a) "Assistant director" means the assistant director of the division.
(b) "Division" means the program called the Division of Services for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing created in Section 35A-13-502.


Enacted by Chapter 271, 2016 General Session

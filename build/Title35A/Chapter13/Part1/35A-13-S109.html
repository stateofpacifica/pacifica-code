Effective 10/1/2016
35A-13-109.  Office duties to individuals with disabilities.
     In administering this chapter, the office:
(1) shall ensure that individuals with disabilities are assisted, so far as reasonably possible, to take their rightful place in open society as independent and self-supporting individuals; and
(2) may not assist or support any activity that results in unnecessary continuation of a dependent or isolated state or unnecessarily separates individuals with disabilities from open society.

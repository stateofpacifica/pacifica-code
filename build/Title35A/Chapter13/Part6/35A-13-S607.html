Effective 10/1/2016
35A-13-607.  Certification term -- Expiration -- Renewal.
(1)
(a) The director shall issue each certificate under this part in accordance with a three-year renewal cycle established by rule.
(b) The director may by rule extend or shorten a renewal cycle by as much as one year to stagger the renewal cycles it administers.
(2) At the time of renewal, the certified interpreter must show satisfactory evidence of compliance with renewal conditions established by the director in accordance with Subsection 35A-13-604(2).
(3) Each certificate automatically expires on the expiration date shown on the certificate unless the certified interpreter renews it in accordance with the conditions prescribed by the director.

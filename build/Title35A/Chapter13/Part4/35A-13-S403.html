Effective 5/14/2019
35A-13-403.  Services provided by the division.
     The division may:
(1) provide:
(a) a business enterprise program;
(b) workshops, employment, and training; and
(c) vocational rehabilitation, training and adjustment, sight conservation, prevention of blindness, low vision lenses, and recreational services;
(2) establish and operate community service centers, rehabilitation facilities, and workshops; and
(3) perform other duties assigned by the director or the executive director.


Amended by Chapter 87, 2019 General Session

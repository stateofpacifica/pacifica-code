Effective 5/9/2017
35A-12-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Account" means an account in a qualified ABLE program.
(2) "Qualified ABLE program" means the same as that term is defined in 26 U.S.C. Sec. 529A.
(3) "State Achieving a Better Life Experience Program" means the program created by this chapter.

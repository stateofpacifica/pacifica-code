Effective 5/12/2015
35A-3-106.  Residency requirements.
(1) An applicant is only eligible for public assistance under this chapter if the applicant is living in Utah voluntarily with the intention of making the state the applicant's place of residence.
(2) An applicant is not eligible for public assistance under this chapter if the applicant is living in Utah for a temporary purpose.


Amended by Chapter 221, 2015 General Session

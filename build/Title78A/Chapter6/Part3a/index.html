  • Section 352 - Appearances -- Parents, guardian, or custodian to appear with minor or child -- Failure to appear -- Warrant of arrest, when authorized -- Parent's, guardian's, or custodian's employer to grant time off -- Appointment of guardian ad litem.
  • Section 353 - Contempt -- Penalty -- Enforcement of fine, fee, or restitution.
  • Section 354 - Hearings -- Minors cases heard separately from adult cases -- Minor or parents or custodian heard separately -- Continuance of hearing.
  • Section 355 - Exchange of information with agency or institution having legal custody.
  • Section 356 - Child support obligation when custody of a child is vested in an individual or institution.
  • Section 357 - New hearings -- Modification of order or decree -- Requirements for changing or terminating custody, probation, or protective supervision.
  • Section 358 - Period of effect for a judgment, decree, or order by a juvenile court.

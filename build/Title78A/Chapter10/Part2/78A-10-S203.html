78A-10-203.  Procedure.
(1) Four commissioners are a quorum.
(2) The governor shall appoint a member of the governor's staff to serve as staff to the Appellate Court Nominating Commission.
(3) The governor shall:
(a) ensure that the commission follows the rules promulgated by the Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice; and
(b) resolve any questions regarding those rules.
(4) A member of the commission who is also a member of the Utah State Bar may recuse himself if there is a conflict of interest that makes the member unable to serve.


Amended by Chapter 134, 2010 General Session

Effective 9/1/2021
78A-2-702.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Attorney guardian ad litem" means an attorney employed by the office.
(2) "Director" means the director of the office.
(3) "Guardian ad litem" means an attorney guardian ad litem or a private attorney guardian ad litem.
(4) "Office" means the Office of Guardian ad Litem, created in Section 78A-2-802.
(5) "Private attorney guardian ad litem" means an attorney designated by the office in accordance with Section 78A-2-705 who is not an employee of the office.

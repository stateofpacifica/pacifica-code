Effective 5/8/2018
78A-2-109.  Courts to provide information and statistical data to state court administrator.
     The judges, clerks of the courts, and all other officers, state and local, shall comply with all requests made by the state court administrator or the state court administrator's assistants for information and statistical data bearing on the state of the dockets of the courts and such other information as may reflect the business transacted by them and the expenditure of public money for the maintenance and operation of the judicial system.

Amended by Chapter 25, 2018 General Session

Effective 5/14/2019
78A-2-403.  Appointment of court reporters -- Eligibility.
     A person may not be appointed to the position of court reporter nor act in the capacity of a court reporter in any court of record of this state, or before any referee, master, board, or commission of this state unless the person is a state certified court reporter in accordance with the provisions of Title 58, Chapter 74, State Certification of Court Reporters Act.

Amended by Chapter 379, 2019 General Session

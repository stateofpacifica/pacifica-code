Effective 5/4/2022
78A-5-101.  State District Court Administrative System -- Definitions.
(1) As used in this chapter:
(a) "Court system" means the State District Court Administrative System.
(b) "Single criminal episode" means the same as that term is defined in Section 76-1-401.
(2)
(a) The district court is a trial court of general jurisdiction.
(b) A district court shall be located in the county seat of each county.
(3)
(a) There is established a State District Court Administrative System.
(b) The Judicial Council shall administer the operation of the court system.

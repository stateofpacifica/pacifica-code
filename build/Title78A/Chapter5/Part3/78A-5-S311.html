78A-5-311.  Completion of the participant agreement.
     If the veterans treatment court determines that a defendant has completed the requirements of the defendant's participant agreement, the court shall adjudicate the defendant's case in accordance with the defendant's participant agreement and any applicable plea in abeyance agreement, plea agreement, probation agreement, court order, or judgment.

Enacted by Chapter 62, 2020 General Session

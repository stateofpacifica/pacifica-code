Effective 5/5/2021
63M-15-203.  Commission meetings.
(1) The commission shall annually elect a chair and vice chair from the commission's membership.
(2) The commission shall hold meetings as needed to fulfill the commission's duties.
(3) A meeting may be held on the call of the chair or a majority of the commission members.
(4) A majority of the voting members of the commission constitute a quorum and, if a quorum exists, the action of a majority of commission members present constitutes the action of the commission.


Enacted by Chapter 91, 2021 General Session

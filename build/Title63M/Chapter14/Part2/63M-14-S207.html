Effective 3/16/2021
63M-14-207.  Reports.
(1) The authority shall prepare an annual report of the authority's and river commissioner's activities and submit the report to the following:
(a) the governor; and
(b) the Legislative Management Committee.
(2) The authority shall respond to and participate in meetings as requested by a legislative committee or by the governor.


Enacted by Chapter 179, 2021 General Session

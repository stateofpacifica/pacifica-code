Effective 5/4/2022
63M-7-218.  State grant requirements.
     Beginning July 1, 2023, the commission may not award any grant of state funds to any entity subject to, and not in compliance with, the reporting requirements in Subsections 63A-16-1002(5)(a) through (o).

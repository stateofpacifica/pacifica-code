Effective 5/12/2020
63M-7-518.  Failure to comply.
     If an individual refuses to comply with an order under this part or asserts a privilege, except privileges arising from the attorney-client relationship, to withhold or suppress evidence relevant to a reparations claim, the director or reparations officer may make any appropriate determination including denial of the reparations claim.

Amended by Chapter 149, 2020 General Session

Effective 5/14/2019
63M-7-302.  Chair -- Vacancies -- Quorum -- Expenses.
(1) The Utah Substance Use and Mental Health Advisory Council shall annually select one of its members to serve as chair and one of its members to serve as vice chair.
(2) When a vacancy occurs in the membership for any reason, the replacement shall be appointed for the unexpired term in the same manner as the position was originally filled.
(3) A majority of the members of the council constitutes a quorum.
(4) A member may not receive compensation or benefits for the member's service, but may receive per diem and travel expenses as allowed in:
(a) Section 63A-3-106;
(b) Section 63A-3-107; and
(c) rules made by the Division of Finance according to Sections 63A-3-106 and 63A-3-107.
(5) The council may establish committees as needed to assist in accomplishing its duties under Section 63M-7-303.


Amended by Chapter 246, 2019 General Session

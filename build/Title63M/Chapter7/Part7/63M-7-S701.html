Effective 5/4/2022
63M-7-701.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Board" means the Domestic Violence Offender Treatment Board created in Section 63M-7-702.
(2) "Commission" means the State Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice created in Section 63M-7-201.


Enacted by Chapter 145, 2022 General Session

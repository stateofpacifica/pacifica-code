Effective 5/5/2021
63M-7-605.  Staffing.
(1) The Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice shall provide staff to the council and any subcommittees established by the council.
(2) Staff assigned to the council shall:
(a) provide administrative assistance to the council and any subcommittees; and
(b) receive complaints regarding victim's rights violations from victims and other interested persons and forward the complaints to the appropriate subcommittee within the council.


Amended by Chapter 172, 2021 General Session

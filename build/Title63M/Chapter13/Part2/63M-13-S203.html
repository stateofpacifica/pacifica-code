Effective 5/14/2019
63M-13-203.  Annual report.
(1) On or before October 1 of each year, the commission shall provide a report to the governor and the Economic Development and Workforce Services Interim Committee.
(2) The annual report shall:
(a) describe how the commission fulfilled its statutory duties during the year;
(b) describe the commission's progress in developing and coordinating a comprehensive delivery system of services for children in early childhood; and
(c) include recommendations on how the state should act to address issues related to providing services for children in early childhood.


Enacted by Chapter 34, 2019 General Session

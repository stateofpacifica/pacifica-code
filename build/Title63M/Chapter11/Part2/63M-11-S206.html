Effective 5/14/2019
63M-11-206.  Members serve without pay -- Reimbursement for expenses.
     A member may not receive compensation or benefits for the member's service, but may receive per diem and travel expenses as allowed in:
(1) Section 63A-3-106;
(2) Section 63A-3-107; and
(3) rules made by the Division of Finance according to Sections 63A-3-106 and 63A-3-107.


Amended by Chapter 246, 2019 General Session

Effective 5/14/2019
53F-5-601.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "American Indian and Alaskan Native concentrated school" means a school where at least 29% of the school's students are American Indian or Alaskan Native.
(2) "Native American Legislative Liaison Committee" means the committee created in Section 36-22-1.
(3) "State plan" means the state plan adopted under Laws of Utah 2015, Chapter 53, Section 7.
(4) "Teacher" means an individual employed by a school district or charter school who is required to hold an educator license issued by the state board and who has an assignment to teach in a classroom.

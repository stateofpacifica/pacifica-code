Effective 7/1/2021
53F-2-706.  Small charter school base funding.
(1) Subject to legislative appropriation, the state board shall distribute small charter school base funding in the following amounts to charter schools with 2,000 or less students:
(a) for a charter school with 300 students or less, $40,000;
(b) for a charter school with 301 to 400 students, $35,000;
(c) for a charter school with 401 to 500 students, $30,000;
(d) for a charter school with 501 to 600 students, $25,000;
(e) for a charter school with 601 to 1,000 students, $20,000; and
(f) for a charter school with 1,001 to 2,000 students, $15,000.
(2) A charter school's eligibility for small charter school base funding is determined by the charter school's student enrollment on October 1 of a given year.
(3) Notwithstanding this section and subject to legislative appropriations, the state board may distribute to charter schools, regardless of size, one-time funding that the Legislature appropriates to mitigate funding losses as described in legislative appropriations.

Effective 5/14/2019
53F-7-201.  Appropriations from Automobile Driver Education Tax Account.
     There is appropriated to the state board from the Automobile Driver Education Tax Account, annually, all money in the account, in excess of the expense of administering the collection of the tax, for use and distribution in the administration and maintenance of driver education classes and programs with respect to classes offered in the school district and the establishment of experimental programs, including the purchasing of equipment, by the state board.

Amended by Chapter 186, 2019 General Session

65A-10-3.  Establishment of sovereign land boundaries.
(1) The division, after consultation with the attorney general and affected state agencies, shall develop plans for the resolution of disputes over the location of sovereign land boundaries.
(2) The division, after notice to affected state agencies and any person with an ownership interest in the land, may enter into agreements with owners of land adjoining navigable lakes and streams to establish sovereign land boundaries.


Amended by Chapter 294, 1994 General Session

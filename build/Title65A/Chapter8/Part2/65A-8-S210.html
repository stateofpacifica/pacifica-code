Effective 1/1/2017
65A-8-210.  Fire control on state-owned lands -- Responsibilities of state agencies.
(1) The division shall abate the public nuisance caused by wildfire on state-owned forest, range, watershed, and wildland urban interface lands.
(2) State agencies responsible for the administration of state-owned lands shall recognize the need for providing wildland fire protection and the responsibility for reducing the risk of wildfire through appropriate wildfire prevention, preparedness, and mitigation actions.


Amended by Chapter 174, 2016 General Session

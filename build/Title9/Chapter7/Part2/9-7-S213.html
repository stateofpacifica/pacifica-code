9-7-213.  Rulemaking.
     The division may make rules in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, necessary to implement and administer the provisions of this chapter including:
(1) standards which shall be met by libraries to obtain and retain a designation as a depository library;
(2) the method by which grants are made to individual libraries, but not including appropriations made directly to any other agency or institution;
(3) standards for the certification of public librarians; and
(4) standards for the public library online access policy required in Section 9-7-215.


Amended by Chapter 378, 2010 General Session

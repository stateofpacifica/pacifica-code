Effective 5/4/2022
9-8-205.  Board duties and powers.
(1) The board shall:
(a) with respect to the division:
(i) make policies to direct the division director in carrying out the director's duties;
(ii) approve the division's rules;
(iii) assist the division in development programs consistent with this chapter; and
(iv) review and approve, if appropriate, matching grants under Subsection 9-8-203(3)(c)(ii); and
(b) with respect to the State Historic Preservation Office created in Section 9-8-902:
(i) make policies to direct the state historic preservation officer in carrying out the officer's duties; and
(ii) assist the office in programs consistent with Part 9, State Historic Preservation Office.
(2) The board may establish advisory committees to assist the board, the office, and the division in carrying out the responsibilities under this chapter.

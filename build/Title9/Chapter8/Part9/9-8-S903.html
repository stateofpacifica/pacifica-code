Effective 5/4/2022
9-8-903.  Appointment of state historic preservation officer.
(1) In accordance with 36 C.F.R. Sec. 61.4, the governor shall appoint the state historic preservation officer.
(2) The officer shall administer:
(a) the office; and
(b) the state historic preservation program.


Enacted by Chapter 369, 2022 General Session

Effective 5/4/2022
9-8-902.  State Historic Preservation Office -- Creation -- Purpose.
(1) There is created within the department the State Historic Preservation Office under the administration and supervision of the executive director or the designee of the executive director.
(2) The office shall be under the policy direction of the board.
(3) The office shall be the authority in the state for state history preservation and shall perform those duties set forth in statute.


Enacted by Chapter 369, 2022 General Session

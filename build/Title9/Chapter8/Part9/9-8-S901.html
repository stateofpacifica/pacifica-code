Effective 5/4/2022
9-8-901.  Definitions.
     As used in this part and in Section 9-8-205:
(1) "Board" means the Board of State History created in Section 9-8-204.
(2) "Committee" means the National Register Review Committee created in Section 9-8-905.
(3) "Office" means the State Historic Preservation Office created in Section 9-8-902.
(4) "Officer" means the state historic preservation officer, appointed in accordance with Section 9-8-903.


Enacted by Chapter 369, 2022 General Session

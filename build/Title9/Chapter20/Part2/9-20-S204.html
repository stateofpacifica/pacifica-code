Effective 5/14/2019
9-20-204.  Meetings -- Quorum.
(1) The commission shall meet at least quarterly.
(2) A voting member of the commission who fails to attend at least 75% of called meetings in a calendar year is automatically removed from the commission.
(3) A commission quorum is a simple majority of the voting members.

Effective 7/1/2022
9-24-101.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Advisory committee" means the Utah Main Street Advisory Committee created in Section 9-24-103.
(2) "Center" means the National Main Street Center.
(3) "Program" means the Utah Main Street Program created in Section 9-24-102.

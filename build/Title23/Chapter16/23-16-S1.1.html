Effective 5/4/2022
23-16-1.1.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "72 hours" means a time period that begins with the hour a request for action is made pursuant to Section 23-16-3 and ends 72 hours later with the exclusion of any hour that occurs on the day of a legal holiday that is on a Monday or Friday and listed in Section 63G-1-301.
(2) "Cultivated crops" means:
(a) annual or perennial crops harvested from or on cleared and planted land;
(b) perennial orchard trees on cleared and planted land;
(c) crop residues that have forage value for livestock; and
(d) pastures.
(3) "Depredation mitigation plan" means the plan described in Subsection 23-16-3(2).
(4) "Growing season" means the portion of a year in which local conditions permit normal plant growth.
(5) "Management unit" means a prescribed area of contiguous land designated by the division for the purpose of managing a species of big game animal.
(6) "Mitigation review panel" means the panel created under Section 23-16-3.2.

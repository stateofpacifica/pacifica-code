Effective 7/1/2020
23-31-104.  Reporting.
     The division shall annually report to the governor and the Natural Resources, Agriculture, and Environment Interim Committee on or before September 1 with respect to:
(1) federal grants, state appropriations, and other contributions, grants, gifts, transfers, bequests, and donations received and credited to the legacy fund during the preceding fiscal year; and
(2) expenditures from the legacy fund under Section 23-31-203.


Enacted by Chapter 190, 2020 General Session

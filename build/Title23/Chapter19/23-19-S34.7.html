23-19-34.7.  Nonresident falconry meet license.
(1) A nonresident 12 years of age or older may participate in a falconry meet in this state upon payment of a fee prescribed by the Wildlife Board.
(2)
(a) A nonresident falconry meet license is valid only for five consecutive days, the dates to be designated on the license.
(b) The holder of the license may engage in the sport of falconry on nongame wildlife species and small game species, during the specified five-day period.


Amended by Chapter 256, 2010 General Session

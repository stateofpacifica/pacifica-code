Effective 5/13/2014
23-19-7.  Expiration date of licenses, permits, and certificates of registration.
(1) The Wildlife Board shall establish the term and expiration date for a license, permit, and certificate of registration issued under this title.
(2) The division shall indicate the term and expiration date established under Subsection (1) on each license, permit, and certificate of registration.


Amended by Chapter 21, 2014 General Session

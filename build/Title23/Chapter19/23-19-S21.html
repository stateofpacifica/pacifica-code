Effective 5/13/2014
23-19-21.  Fishing license.
(1) A person 12 years of age or older shall purchase a fishing license before engaging in a regulated fishing activity.
(2) Upon paying the fee prescribed by the Wildlife Board, a person may obtain a license to fish and engage in a regulated fishing activity in accordance with the rules, proclamations, and orders of the Wildlife Board.
(3) A person under 12 years of age may fish without a license in accordance with the rules, proclamations, and orders of the Wildlife Board.


Amended by Chapter 21, 2014 General Session

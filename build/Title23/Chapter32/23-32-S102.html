Effective 5/5/2021
23-32-102.  Definitions.
(1) The definitions in Section 58-79-102 apply to this chapter.
(2)
(a) As used in this chapter, "waterfowl management area" means real property owned or managed by the Division of Wildlife Resources that is:
(i) primarily used for the conservation, production, or recreational harvest of ducks, mergansers, geese, brant, swans, and other waterfowl; and
(ii) designated as a waterfowl management area by the Wildlife Board in accordance with Section 23-32-104.
(b) "Waterfowl management area" includes the Willard Spur Waterfowl Management Area and the Harold Crane Waterfowl Management Area described in Section 23-21-5.

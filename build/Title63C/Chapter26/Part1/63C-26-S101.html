Effective 5/4/2022
63C-26-101.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Board" means the governing board of the project entity.
(2) "Committee" means the Project Entity Oversight Committee created in Section 63C-26-201.
(3) "Project entity" means the same as that term is defined in Section 11-13-103.

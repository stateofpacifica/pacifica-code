Effective 5/12/2020
63C-9-601.  Responsibility for items.
     Furniture, furnishings, fixtures, works of art, and decorative objects for which the board has responsibility under this chapter are not subject to the custody or control of the State Library Board, the State Library Division, the Division of Archives and Records Service, the Division of State History, the Division of Arts and Museums, the arts collection committee of the State of Utah Alice Merrill Horne Art Collection, or any other state agency.

Amended by Chapter 419, 2020 General Session

63C-6-104.  Staffing and appropriated funds.
(1) Staff support to the commission shall be provided by the Division of Emergency Management and the Utah Geological Survey.
(2) Money not expended by the Utah Seismic Safety Commission during a fiscal year is nonlapsing except that any balance of General Fund money greater than $10,000 lapses to the General Fund.

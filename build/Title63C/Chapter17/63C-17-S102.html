Effective 5/10/2016
63C-17-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Commission" means the Point of the Mountain Development Commission, created in Section 63C-17-103.
(2) "Project area" means the area surrounding the border between Salt Lake County and Utah County, commonly referred to as the Point of the Mountain.


Enacted by Chapter 156, 2016 General Session

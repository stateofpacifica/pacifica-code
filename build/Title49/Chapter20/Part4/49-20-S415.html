Effective 5/9/2017
49-20-415.  Prescribing policies for certain opioid prescriptions.
     A plan offered to state employees under this chapter may implement a prescribing policy for certain opioid prescriptions in accordance with Section 31A-22-615.5.

Enacted by Chapter 53, 2017 General Session

Technically renumbered to avoid duplication of section number also enacted in HB154, Chapter 241.

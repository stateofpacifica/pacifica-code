Effective 5/10/2016
49-11-1101.  Public financial information disclosure on website -- Exclusions.
(1) The office shall provide the following financial information for the public on its website:
(a) administrative expense transactions from its general ledger accounting system; and
(b) employee compensation information by individual employee at least annually.
(2) For purposes of this part, the office is not required to provide other information for public access on its website, if the disclosure of the information would conflict with the fiduciary obligations of the board, including:
(a) revenue transactions; and
(b) member and participant information.


Amended by Chapter 281, 2016 General Session

49-11-617.  Original documents.
(1) At the reasonable discretion of the office, any document relating to this title may be treated as an original, whether created by photocopy, facsimile, e-mail, electronic transmission, imaging, or other technology.
(2) The office may communicate with participating employers, members, beneficiaries, and others through electronic means as determined appropriate by the office.


Amended by Chapter 316, 2013 General Session

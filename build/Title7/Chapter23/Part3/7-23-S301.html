7-23-301.  Posting of fee schedules for cashing checks.
(1) A check casher shall post a complete schedule of all fees for cashing a check in a conspicuous location on its premises that can be viewed by a person cashing a check.
(2) The schedule of fees required to be posted under Subsection (1) shall state the fees using dollar amounts.

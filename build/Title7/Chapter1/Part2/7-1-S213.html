Effective 5/8/2018
7-1-213.  Compliance with training and certification requirements.
     The department shall ensure that any training or certification required of a public official or public employee, as those terms are defined in Section 63G-22-102, complies with Title 63G, Chapter 22, State Training and Certification Requirements, if the training or certification is required:
(1) under this title;
(2) by the department; or
(3) by an agency or division within the department.


Enacted by Chapter 200, 2018 General Session

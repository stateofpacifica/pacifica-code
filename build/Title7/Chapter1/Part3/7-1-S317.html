7-1-317.  Reports of condition called for by commissioner.
     The commissioner may call upon any institution under the jurisdiction of the department for a report of its condition at the close of business on any day specified in the call within the preceding three months. The reports required shall be transmitted by the institution to the commissioner within 30 days after the date of the call. Reports prepared by an independent certified public accountant or reports accepted by supervisory agencies of the United States or other states shall be acceptable.

Enacted by Chapter 16, 1981 General Session

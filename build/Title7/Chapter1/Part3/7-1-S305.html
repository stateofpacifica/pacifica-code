7-1-305.  Final decisions on applications for new institutions, branches, relocation, merger, consolidation, acquisition and changes in control of financial institutions.
     The commissioner shall make final decisions consistent with the purposes and provisions of this title on behalf of the department upon all applications to the department for approval of new institutions, branches, relocation, mergers or consolidation, acquisitions and changes in control of institutions subject to the jurisdiction of the department.

Enacted by Chapter 16, 1981 General Session

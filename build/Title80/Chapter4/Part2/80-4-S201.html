Effective 9/1/2021
80-4-201.  Petition -- Who may file -- Dismissal.
(1) Any interested party, including a foster parent, may file a petition for termination of parental rights.
(2) The attorney general shall file a petition for termination of parental rights under this chapter on behalf of the division.
(3) The juvenile court may dismiss a petition for termination of parental rights at any stage of the proceedings.

80-5-104.  Division director -- Qualifications -- Responsibility.
(1) The executive director of the department shall appoint the director of the division.
(2) The director shall have a bachelor's degree from an accredited university or college, be experienced in administration, and be knowledgeable in juvenile justice.
(3) The director is the administrative head of the division.

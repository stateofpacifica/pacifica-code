Effective 9/1/2021
80-5-103.  Creation of division -- Jurisdiction.
(1) There is created the Division of Juvenile Justice Services within the department.
(2) The division shall be under the administration and supervision of the executive director of the department.
(3) The division has jurisdiction over all minors committed to the division under Sections 80-6-703 and 80-6-705.

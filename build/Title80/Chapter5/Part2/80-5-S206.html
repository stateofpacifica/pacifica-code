80-5-206.  Records -- Property of division.
(1) All records maintained by programs that are under contract with the division to provide services to minors, are the property of the division and shall be returned to the division when the minor is terminated from the program.
(2) The division shall maintain an accurate audit trail of information provided to other programs or agencies regarding minors under the division's jurisdiction.

Effective 9/1/2021
80-5-701.  Youth Parole Authority -- Creation -- Members.
(1) There is created the Youth Parole Authority within the division.
(2)
(a) The authority is composed of 10 part-time members and five pro tempore members who are residents of this state.
(b) No more than three pro tempore members may serve on the authority at any one time.

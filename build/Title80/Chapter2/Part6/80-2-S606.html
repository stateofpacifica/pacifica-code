Effective 9/1/2022
80-2-606.  Written reports.
(1)
(a) A person who orally reports under Section 80-2-602, 80-2-603, or 80-2-604 shall, upon request of the division, provide the division with a written version of the oral report.
(b) The person shall provide the written report within 48 hours after the division's request.
(2) If, in connection with an intended or completed abortion, a physician is required to make a report of incest or abuse of a minor, the report may not include information that would in any way disclose that the report was made in connection with:
(a) an abortion; or
(b) a consultation regarding an abortion.
(3) The division shall, immediately after receipt, forward a copy of a written report to the state child abuse and neglect registry on a form supplied by the registry.

80-2-608.  Confidential identity of person who reports.
     Except as provided in Sections 80-2-611 and 80-2-1005, the division and a law enforcement agency shall ensure the anonymity of the person who makes the initial report under this part and any other person involved in the division's or law enforcement agency's subsequent investigation of the report.

Enacted by Chapter 334, 2022 General Session

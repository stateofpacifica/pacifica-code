Effective 9/1/2021
80-6-1007.  Fees.
(1) Except for a filing fee for a petition under this part, the juvenile court may not charge a fee for:
(a) an issuance of an expungement order under this part; or
(b) an expungement of a record under this part.
(2) An agency may not charge a fee for the expungement of a record under this part.

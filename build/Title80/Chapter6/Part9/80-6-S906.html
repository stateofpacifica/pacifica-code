Effective 9/1/2021
80-6-906.  Fees.
(1)
(a) A youth court may require that a minor pay a reasonable fee, not to exceed $50, to participate in the youth court.
(b) A fee under Subsection (1) may be reduced or waived by the youth court in exigent circumstances.
(c) A fee under Subsection (1) shall be paid to and accounted for by the sponsoring entity.
(d) Any fees collected shall be used for supplies and any training requirements.
(2) A minor who participates in youth court is responsible for the all expenses of any classes, counseling, treatment, or other educational programs that are the disposition of the youth court.

80-6-803.  Cost of support and maintenance of a juvenile offender -- Responsibility.
     On commitment of a juvenile offender to the division, and on recommendation of the division to the juvenile court, the juvenile court may order the juvenile offender, or the juvenile offender's parent, guardian, or custodian in accordance with Section 78A-6-356, to share in the costs of support and maintenance for the juvenile offender during the juvenile offender's term of commitment.

Effective 9/1/2022
80-6-1101.  Interstate Compact for Juveniles -- Execution of compact.
(1) This part is known as the "Interstate Compact for Juveniles."
(2) The governor is authorized and directed to execute a compact on behalf of this state with any other state or states substantially in the form of this part.

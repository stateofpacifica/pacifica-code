Effective 5/12/2015
41-22-13.  Prohibited uses.
(1) No person may operate an off-highway vehicle in connection with acts of vandalism, harassment of wildlife or domestic animals, burglaries or other crimes, or damage to the environment which includes excessive pollution of air, water, or land, abuse of the watershed, impairment of plant or animal life, or excessive mechanical noise.
(2) A violation of this section is an infraction.


Amended by Chapter 412, 2015 General Session

Effective 7/1/2022
41-22-10.  Powers of division relating to off-highway vehicles.
     The division may:
(1) appoint and seek recommendations from the advisory council representing the various off-highway vehicle, conservation, and other appropriate interests; and
(2) adopt a uniform marker and sign system for use by agents of appropriate federal, state, county, and city agencies in areas of off-highway vehicle use.


Amended by Chapter 68, 2022 General Session

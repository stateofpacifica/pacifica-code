Effective 5/12/2015
41-22-12.1.  Restrictions on use of snowmobile trails.
(1) A person may not operate a wheeled vehicle with a gross vehicle weight of 800 pounds or more on any snowmobile trail that the division has marked, posted, designated, or maintained as a snowmobile trail.
(2) A violation of this section is an infraction.


Amended by Chapter 412, 2015 General Session

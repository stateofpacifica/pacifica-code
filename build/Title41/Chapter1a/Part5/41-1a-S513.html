41-1a-513.  Examination of registration records and indices of stolen and recovered vehicles, vessels, and outboard motors.
     The division upon receiving application for any certificate of title shall first check the identification number shown in the application against the indices of registered vehicles, vessels, and outboard motors and against indices of stolen and recovered vehicles, vessels, and outboard motors.

Enacted by Chapter 1, 1992 General Session
Amended by Chapter 218, 1992 General Session

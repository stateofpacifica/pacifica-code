41-1a-507.  Exceptions to title requirements for off-highway vehicles.
(1) Each off-highway vehicle operated in this state and identified by the manufacturer as a 1988 year model or newer is subject to the titling provisions of this part except:
(a) off-highway vehicles owned and operated by nonresidents of the state;
(b) off-highway vehicles owned and operated by the federal government; and
(c) off-highway vehicles that are registered for highway use.
(2) The division may not provide title to an off-highway vehicle identified by the manufacturer as a 1987 year model or older.

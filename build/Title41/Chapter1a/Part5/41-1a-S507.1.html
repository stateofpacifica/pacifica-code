Effective 1/1/2015
41-1a-507.1.  Exceptions to title requirements for campers.
(1) Each camper in this state and identified by the manufacturer as a 2015 year model or newer is subject to the titling provisions of this part.
(2) The division may provide title to a camper identified by the manufacturer as a 2014 year model or older if requested by the owner of the camper.


Enacted by Chapter 61, 2014 General Session

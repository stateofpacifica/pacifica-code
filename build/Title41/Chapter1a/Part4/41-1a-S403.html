Effective 5/12/2015
41-1a-403.  Plates to be legible from 100 feet.
(1) License plates and the required letters and numerals on them, except the decals and the slogan, shall be of sufficient size to be plainly readable from a distance of 100 feet during daylight.
(2) A violation of this section is an infraction.


Amended by Chapter 412, 2015 General Session

Effective 5/12/2015
41-1a-220.  Lost or damaged registration card.
(1) If a registration card is lost, mutilated, or becomes illegible the owner of the vehicle for which the registration card was issued, as shown by the records of the division, shall immediately:
(a) apply for a duplicate;
(b) furnish the information satisfactory to the division; and
(c) pay the proper fees.
(2) A violation of this section is an infraction.


Amended by Chapter 412, 2015 General Session

Effective 5/12/2015
41-1a-1304.  Operating motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer in excess of registered gross laden weight -- Infraction.
     It is an infraction for a person to operate, or cause to be operated, a motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer, or combination of them the gross laden weight of which is in excess of the gross laden weight for which the motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer, or combination of vehicles is registered.

Amended by Chapter 412, 2015 General Session

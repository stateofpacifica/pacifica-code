Effective 1/1/2018
41-1a-1306.  Abuse of persons with disabilities parking privileges -- Revocation of special plate or transferable ID card -- Fine.
     A person with a disability who abuses the rights and privileges conferred under Section 41-1a-414 or allows an individual who is not a person with a disability to use those parking privileges may have the person's disability special group license plate, temporary removable windshield placard, removable windshield placard, temporary wheelchair user placard, or wheelchair user placard revoked by the division.

Amended by Chapter 41, 2017 General Session

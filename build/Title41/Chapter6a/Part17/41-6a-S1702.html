Effective 5/14/2019
41-6a-1702.  Sidewalk -- Driving prohibited -- Exception.
(1) Except for a bicycle, a device propelled by human power, or a motor assisted scooter, a person may not operate a vehicle on a sidewalk or sidewalk area. A motor assisted scooter may be operated on a sidewalk only if permitted pursuant to Subsection 41-6a-1115.1(3).
(2) Subsection (1) does not apply on a driveway.


Amended by Chapter 428, 2019 General Session

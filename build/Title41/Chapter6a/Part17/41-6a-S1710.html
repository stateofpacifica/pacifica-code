41-6a-1710.  Following near an authorized emergency vehicle or parking near fire apparatus prohibited.
     Except for a person operating an authorized emergency vehicle, the operator of a vehicle may not:
(1) follow closer than 500 feet any authorized emergency vehicle traveling in response to an emergency; or
(2) stop the vehicle within 500 feet of a fire apparatus which has stopped in answer to a fire alarm.


Amended by Chapter 96, 2012 General Session

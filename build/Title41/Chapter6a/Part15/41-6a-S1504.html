Effective 5/12/2015
41-6a-1504.  Motorcycle or motor-driven cycle -- Footrests for passenger -- Height of handlebars limited.
(1) A motorcycle or motor-driven vehicle carrying a passenger on a public highway, other than in a sidecar or enclosed cab, shall be equipped with footrests for the passenger.
(2) A person may not operate a motorcycle or motor-driven cycle with handlebars above shoulder height.
(3) A violation of this section is an infraction.


Amended by Chapter 412, 2015 General Session

Chapter 4 - Financing Dealers and Purchasers

  • Section 1 - Agreements to finance through designated source which lessen competition or create monopoly declared void.
  • Section 2 - Threat to discontinue sales to retail seller prima facie evidence of violation.
  • Section 3 - Threat to discontinue sales to person engaged in business of financing who is affiliated with manufacturer or distributor.
  • Section 4 - Giving of gratuity by manufacturer or wholesaler to one financing sales which lessens competition, unlawful.
  • Section 5 - Unlawful for person financing sales to accept gratuity.
  • Section 6 - Accepting gratuity, unlawful thereafter to finance sales.
  • Section 7 - Violation by corporation -- Penalty.
  • Section 8 - Violation by foreign corporation -- Penalty.
  • Section 9 - Persons violating provisions -- Penalty.
  • Section 10 - Agreements in violation of provisions are void.
  • Section 11 - Provisions cumulative.
  • Section 12 - Actions for damages.
  • Section 13 - Definitions.
  • Section 14 - Separability clause.

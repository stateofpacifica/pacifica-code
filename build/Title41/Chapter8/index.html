Chapter 8 - Driving by Minors

  • Section 1 - Operation of vehicle by persons under 16 prohibited -- Exceptions for off-highway vehicles and off-highway implements of husbandry.
  • Section 2 - Operation of vehicle by persons under 17 during night hours prohibited -- Exceptions.
  • Section 3 - Operation of vehicle by persons under 16 and six months -- Passenger limitations -- Exceptions -- Penalties.
  • Section 4 - Operation of a vehicle by a person under 18 -- Use of wireless telephones prohibited -- Exceptions -- Penalty.

40-8-10.  Notice.
     Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, any notification required by this chapter shall be:
(1) given by the board or division by personal service to individuals directly affected; and
(a) by one publication in a daily newspaper of general circulation in Salt Lake City, Utah; and
(b) in all newspapers of general circulation published in the county or counties in which the land affected is situated; and
(2) by publication in accordance with Section 45-1-101.


Amended by Chapter 388, 2009 General Session

40-11-8.  Findings to issue a permit.
     Before issuing a permit, the board shall find that:
(1) the application meets all of the requirements described in Section 40-11-6; and
(2) the interested parties described in Subsection 40-11-7(4) all received proper notice.


Enacted by Chapter 62, 2022 General Session

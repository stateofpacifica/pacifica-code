Effective 5/4/2022
40-11-17.  Application of this chapter to enhanced recovery projects.
(1) This chapter does not apply to the injection of carbon dioxide for an enhanced oil or gas recovery project.
(2)
(a) This chapter does apply to the conversion of an enhanced oil or gas recovery project to a storage facility.
(b) To accommodate the conversion described in Subsection (2)(a), the board may make additional rules to allow for circumstances unique to the conversion of an enhanced oil and gas recovery project to a storage facility and not otherwise anticipated under this chapter.


Enacted by Chapter 62, 2022 General Session

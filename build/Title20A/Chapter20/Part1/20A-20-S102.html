20A-20-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Commission" means the Utah Independent Redistricting Commission created in Section 20A-20-201.
(2) "Committee" means the Legislature's redistricting committee.
(3) "Decennial year" means a year during which the United States Bureau of Census conducts a national decennial census.
(4) "Linked PL94-171 data" means legacy file decennial census data for Utah, published by the United States Bureau of Census, that, after publication, is linked to Utah geographic data files in a manner that the data can be used to draw maps for the purpose of redistricting.
(5) "Regular decennial redistricting" means redistricting required due to a national decennial census.
(6) "Special redistricting" means redistricting that is not a regular decennial redistricting.

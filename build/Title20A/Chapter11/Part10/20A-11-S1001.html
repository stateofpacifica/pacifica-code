20A-11-1001.  Electronic form prepared by chief election officer.
     The chief election officer shall:
(1) develop and prepare an electronic form for all financial statements required by this chapter and Chapter 12, Part 2, Judicial Retention Elections; and
(2) provide access to the electronic form to the secretary of every committee, to every candidate, and to all others who request a form.


Amended by Chapter 396, 2011 General Session

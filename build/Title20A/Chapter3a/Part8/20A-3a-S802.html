Effective 5/12/2020
20A-3a-802.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Challenged voter" means an individual whose right to vote is challenged as provided in this part.
(2) "Filer" means an individual who files a written statement challenging another individual's right to vote as provided in Section 20A-3a-804.

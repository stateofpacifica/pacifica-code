Effective 5/13/2014
20A-2-102.  Registration a prerequisite to voting.
(1) Except as provided in Subsection (2), a person may not vote at any election unless that person is registered to vote as required by this chapter.
(2) A person may vote a provisional ballot for an election as provided in Section 20A-2-307.


Amended by Chapter 231, 2014 General Session

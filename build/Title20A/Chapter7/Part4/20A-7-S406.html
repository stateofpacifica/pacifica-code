Effective 5/14/2019
20A-7-406.  Informational materials.
     The lieutenant governor shall create and publish to the lieutenant governor's website instructions on how a person may:
(1) qualify a local initiative for the ballot under Part 5, Local Initiatives - Procedures; or
(2) qualify a local referendum for the ballot under Part 6, Local Referenda - Procedures.


Enacted by Chapter 203, 2019 General Session

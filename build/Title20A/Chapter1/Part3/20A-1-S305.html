20A-1-305.  Publication and distribution of election laws.
(1) The lieutenant governor shall:
(a) publish a sufficient number of copies of Title 20A, Election Code, and any other provisions of law that govern elections; and
(b) transmit copies to each county clerk.
(2) Each county clerk shall:
(a) inform the lieutenant governor of the number of copies needed; and
(b) furnish each election officer in the county with one copy.


Enacted by Chapter 1, 1993 General Session

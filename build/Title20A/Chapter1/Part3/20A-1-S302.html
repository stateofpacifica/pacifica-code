20A-1-302.  Opening and closing of polls on election day.
(1) Polls at all elections on the date of the election shall open at 7 a.m. and shall remain open until 8 p.m. of the same day.
(2) The election judges shall allow every voter who arrives at the polls by 8 p.m. to vote.


Amended by Chapter 264, 2006 General Session

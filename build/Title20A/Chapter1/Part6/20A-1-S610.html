Effective 3/1/2018
20A-1-610.  Abetting violation of chapter -- Penalty.
     In addition to the penalties established in Subsections 20A-1-609(2) and (3), any person who aids, abets, or advises a violation of any provision of this title is guilty of a class B misdemeanor, unless another penalty is specifically provided.

Amended by Chapter 19, 2018 General Session

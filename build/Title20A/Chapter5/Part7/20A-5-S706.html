20A-5-706.  Damage, alteration, or theft of election equipment -- Penalty.
(1) It is unlawful for any person to intentionally or knowingly damage, modify, steal, tamper with, or destroy a voting device, voting equipment, or an automated voting system.
(2) Any person who violates this section is guilty of a third degree felony.


Enacted by Chapter 326, 2006 General Session

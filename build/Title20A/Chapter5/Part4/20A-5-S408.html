Effective 5/12/2020
20A-5-408.  Disposition of election returns.
(1) Each election officer shall produce the packages containing the election returns before the board of canvassers.
(2) As soon as the returns are canvassed, the election officer shall file the election returns and papers produced before the board as required by Section 20A-4-202.


Amended by Chapter 31, 2020 General Session

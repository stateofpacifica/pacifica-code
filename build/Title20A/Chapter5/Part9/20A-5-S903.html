Effective 5/4/2022
20A-5-903.  Cyber security.
(1) An election officer shall ensure that the following election equipment is never connected to the Internet:
(a) tabulation servers;
(b) tabulation equipment;
(c) ballot scanners, including central, precinct, and mobile scanners; and
(d) ballot marking devices.
(2) This section does not prohibit Internet connection of equipment used for voting if the equipment's use of voting is solely for the purpose of:
(a) complying with Title 20A, Chapter 16, Uniform Military and Overseas Voters Act; or
(b) administering the Internet Voting Pilot Project, described in Section 20A-6-103.


Enacted by Chapter 156, 2022 General Session

Effective 5/4/2022
20A-5-904.  Voter fraud.
     An election officer shall:
(1) check available resources to determine whether an individual registers to vote, or votes, in more than one state or precinct; and
(2) report the information to law enforcement or a prosecutor if the election officer has reason to believe that an individual has intentionally committed election fraud.


Enacted by Chapter 156, 2022 General Session

20A-5-204.  Duplicate registration.
(1) Upon finding duplicate registration, the county clerk shall first check for errors in the record.
(2) If duplicate registration does exist, the clerk shall:
(a) eliminate one registration entry; and
(b) change the date of voter registration on the voter's file to the most recent registration date.

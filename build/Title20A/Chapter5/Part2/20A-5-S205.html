Effective 5/12/2020
20A-5-205.  Delivery of official register.
(1) Before delivering the official register to the poll workers, the county clerk shall verify the accuracy and completeness of the official register.
(2) The county clerk shall, before the polls open at an early voting center or any other polling place:
(a) deliver the official register to each polling place; and
(b) provide verification of the official register's accuracy and completeness.
(3) This section does not prohibit a county clerk from updating an official register as necessary.


Amended by Chapter 31, 2020 General Session

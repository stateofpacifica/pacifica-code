Effective 5/8/2018
26-36b-203.  Quarterly notice.
(1) Quarterly assessments imposed by this chapter shall be paid to the division within 15 business days after the original invoice date that appears on the invoice issued by the division.
(2) The department may, by rule, extend the time for paying the assessment.

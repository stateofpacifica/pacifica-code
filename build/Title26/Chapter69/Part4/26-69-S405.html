26-69-405.  Powers of UMEC.
     The UMEC may:
(1) appoint advisory committees of broad representation on interdisciplinary clinical education, workforce mix planning and projections, funding mechanisms, and other topics as is necessary;
(2) use federal money for necessary administrative expenses to carry out UMEC's duties and powers as permitted by federal law;
(3) distribute program money in accordance with Subsection 26-69-404(4); and
(4) as is necessary to carry out UMEC's duties under Section 26-69-404, adopt rules in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act.

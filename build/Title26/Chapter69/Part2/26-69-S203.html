Effective 7/1/2022
26-69-203.  Members serve without pay -- Reimbursement for expenses.
     A member of the council or a subcommittee created by the council may not receive compensation or benefits for the member's service but may receive per diem and travel expenses as allowed in:
(1) Section 63A-3-106;
(2) Section 63A-3-107; and
(3) rules made by the Division of Finance according to Sections 63A-3-106 and 63A-3-107.


Enacted by Chapter 224, 2022 General Session

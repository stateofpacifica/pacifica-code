Effective 5/13/2014
26-15-5.1.  Exemptions to food handler requirements.
(1) The requirements of Section 26-15-5 do not apply to an individual who handles food:
(a) at an event sponsored by a charitable organization where the organization provides food to a disadvantaged group free of charge; and
(b) in compliance with rules established by the department under Subsection (2).
(2) The department may establish additional requirements, in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, for individuals handling food at an event sponsored by a charitable organization under Subsection (1).

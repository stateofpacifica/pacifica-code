Effective 7/1/2020
26-57-104.  Labeling of nicotine products containing nicotine.
(1) Any nicotine product shall contain the statement described in Subsection (2) if the nicotine product:
(a)
(i) is not a tobacco product as defined in 21 U.S.C. Sec. 321 and related federal regulations; or
(ii) is not otherwise required under federal or state law to contain a nicotine warning; and
(b) contains nicotine.
(2) A statement shall appear on the exterior packaging of a nicotine product described in Subsection (1) as follows:
     "This product contains nicotine."


Enacted by Chapter 347, 2020 General Session

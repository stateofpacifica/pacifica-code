Effective 7/1/2015
26-7-7.  Radon awareness campaign.
     The department shall, in consultation with the Division of Waste Management and Radiation Control, develop a statewide electronic awareness campaign to educate the public regarding:
(1) the existence and prevalence of radon gas in buildings and structures;
(2) the health risks associated with radon gas;
(3) options for radon gas testing; and
(4) options for radon gas remediation.


Amended by Chapter 451, 2015 General Session

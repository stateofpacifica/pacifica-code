Effective 5/8/2018
26-19-507.  Expenditures included in lien -- Other proceedings.
(1) A TEFRA lien imposed on real property under this part includes all expenses relating to medical assistance provided or paid for under the state plan from the first day that the individual is placed in a care facility, regardless of when the lien is imposed or filed on the property.
(2) Nothing in this part affects or prevents the department from bringing or pursuing any other legally authorized action to recover medical assistance or to set aside a fraudulent or improper conveyance.


Enacted by Chapter 443, 2018 General Session

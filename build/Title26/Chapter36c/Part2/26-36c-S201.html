Effective 2/11/2019
26-36c-201.  Assessment.
(1) An assessment is imposed on each private hospital:
(a) beginning upon the later of:
(i) April 1, 2019; and
(ii) CMS approval of the assessment under this chapter;
(b) in the amount designated in Sections 26-36c-204 and 26-36c-205; and
(c) in accordance with Section 26-36c-202.
(2) The assessment imposed by this chapter is due and payable in accordance with Subsection 26-36c-202(4).


Amended by Chapter 1, 2019 General Session

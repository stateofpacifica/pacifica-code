Effective 7/1/2022
26-60-104.  Enforcement.
(1) The Division of Professional Licensing created in Section 58-1-103 is authorized to enforce the provisions of Section 26-60-103 as it relates to providers licensed under Title 58, Occupations and Professions.
(2) The department is authorized to enforce the provisions of:
(a) Section 26-60-103 as it relates to providers licensed under this title; and
(b) Section 26-60-103 as it relates to providers licensed under Title 62A, Chapter 2, Licensure of Programs and Facilities.

Effective 5/8/2018
26-21a-205.  Department duties.
     The department shall:
(1) enforce rules established under this part;
(2) implement and enforce the notice requirement in Section 26-21a-206;
(3) authorize qualified department agents to conduct inspections of mammogram facilities under department rules;
(4) collect and credit fees for certification established by the department in accordance with Section 63J-1-504; and
(5) provide necessary administrative and staff support to the committee.


Amended by Chapter 217, 2018 General Session

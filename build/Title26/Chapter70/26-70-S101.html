Effective 5/4/2022
26-70-101.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "HIPAA" means the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996, Pub. L. No. 104-191, 110 Stat. 1936, as amended.
(2) "Patient" means the individual whose information is being requested.
(3) "Personal representative" means an individual described in 45 C.F.R. Sec. 164.502(g).

26-2-14.2.  Delayed registration of birth resulting in stillbirth.
     When a birth resulting in stillbirth occurring in this state has not been registered within one year after the date of delivery, a certificate marked "delayed" may be filed and registered in accordance with department rule relating to evidentiary and other requirements sufficient to substantiate the alleged facts of birth resulting in stillbirth.

Enacted by Chapter 69, 2002 General Session

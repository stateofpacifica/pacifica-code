Effective 5/5/2021
26-8a-401.  State regulation of emergency medical services market -- License term.
(1) To ensure emergency medical service quality and minimize unnecessary duplication, the department shall regulate the emergency medical services market by creating and operating a statewide system that:
(a) consists of exclusive geographic service areas as provided in Section 26-8a-402; and
(b) establishes maximum rates as provided in Section 26-8a-403.
(2) A license issued or renewed under this part is valid for four years.


Amended by Chapter 265, 2021 General Session

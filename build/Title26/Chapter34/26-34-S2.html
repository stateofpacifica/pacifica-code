Effective 5/12/2020
26-34-2.  Definition of death -- Determination of death.
(1) An individual is dead if the individual has sustained either:
(a) irreversible cessation of circulatory and respiratory functions; or
(b) irreversible cessation of all functions of the entire brain, including the brain stem.
(2) A determination of death shall be made in accordance with this chapter and accepted medical standards.


Amended by Chapter 353, 2020 General Session

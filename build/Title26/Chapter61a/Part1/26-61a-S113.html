Effective 5/12/2020
26-61a-113.  No effect on use of hemp extract -- Cannabidiol -- Approved drugs.
(1) Nothing in this chapter prohibits an individual from purchasing, selling, possessing, or using a cannabinoid product in accordance with Section 4-41-402.
(2) Nothing in this chapter restricts or otherwise affects the prescription, distribution, or dispensing of a product that the United States Food and Drug Administration has approved.

Effective 7/1/2022
26-15c-104.  Safety and health inspections and permits.
(1) A local health department with jurisdiction over an area in which a microenterprise home kitchen is located may grant a microenterprise home kitchen permit to the operator.
(2) Nothing in this section prevents a local health department from revoking a microenterprise home kitchen permit issued by the local health department if the operation of the microenterprise home kitchen violates the terms of the permit or Section 26-15c-105.


Enacted by Chapter 417, 2021 General Session

Effective 5/12/2015
26-18-605.  Utah Office of Internal Audit.
     The Utah Office of Internal Audit:
(1) may not be placed within the division;
(2) shall be placed directly under, and report directly to, the executive director of the Department of Health; and
(3) shall have full access to all records of the division.


Amended by Chapter 135, 2015 General Session

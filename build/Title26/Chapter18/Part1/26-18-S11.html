Effective 5/14/2019
26-18-11.  Rural hospitals.
(1) For purposes of this section "rural hospital" means a hospital located outside of a standard metropolitan statistical area, as designated by the United States Bureau of the Census.
(2) For purposes of the Medicaid program, the Division of Medicaid and Health Financing may not discriminate among rural hospitals on the basis of size.


Amended by Chapter 393, 2019 General Session

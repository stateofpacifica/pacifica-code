Effective 5/14/2019
26-18-2.1.  Division -- Creation.
     There is created, within the department, the Division of Medicaid and Health Financing which shall be responsible for implementing, organizing, and maintaining the Medicaid program and the Children's Health Insurance Program established in Section 26-40-103, in accordance with the provisions of this chapter and applicable federal law.

Amended by Chapter 393, 2019 General Session

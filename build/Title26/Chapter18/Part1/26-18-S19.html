Effective 5/10/2016
26-18-19.  Medicaid vision services -- Request for proposals.
     The department may select one or more contractors, in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 6a, Utah Procurement Code, to provide vision services to the Medicaid populations that are eligible for vision services, as described in department rules, without restricting provider participation, and within existing appropriations from the Legislature.

Amended by Chapter 114, 2016 General Session

Effective 5/9/2017
26-18-22.  Screening, Brief Intervention, and Referral to Treatment Medicaid reimbursement.
(1) As used in this section:
(a) "Controlled substance prescriber" means a controlled substance prescriber, as that term is defined in Section 58-37-6.5, who:
(i) has a record of having completed SBIRT training, in accordance with Subsection 58-37-6.5(2), before providing the SBIRT services; and
(ii) is a Medicaid enrolled health care provider.
(b) "SBIRT" means the same as that term is defined in Section 58-37-6.5.
(2) The department shall reimburse a controlled substance prescriber who provides SBIRT services to a Medicaid enrollee who is 13 years of age or older for the SBIRT services.

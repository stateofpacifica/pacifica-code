Effective 5/5/2021
26-18-15.  Process to promote health insurance coverage for children.
(1) The department, in collaboration with the Department of Workforce Services and the State Board of Education, shall develop a process to promote health insurance coverage for a child in school when:
(a) the child applies for free or reduced price school lunch;
(b) a child enrolls in or registers in school; and
(c) other appropriate school related opportunities.
(2) The department, in collaboration with the Department of Workforce Services, shall promote and facilitate the enrollment of children identified under Subsection (1) without health insurance in the Utah Children's Health Insurance Program, the Medicaid program, or the Utah Premium Partnership for Health Insurance Program.


Amended by Chapter 163, 2021 General Session

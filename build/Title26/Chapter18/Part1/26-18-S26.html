Effective 5/14/2019
26-18-26.  Reimbursement for nonemergency secured behavioral health transport providers.
     The department may not reimburse a nonemergency secured behavioral health transport provider that is designated under Section 26-8a-303.

Enacted by Chapter 265, 2019 General Session

Technically renumbered to avoid duplication of section number used in HB 435, Chapter 320.

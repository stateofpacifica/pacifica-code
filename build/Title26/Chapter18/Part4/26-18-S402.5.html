Effective 5/4/2022
26-18-402.5.  Nonlapsing Medicaid funds.
(1) Notwithstanding Subsection 26-18-402(3), for fiscal years 2019-20, 2020-21, and 2021-22, the funds described in Subsections 26-18-402(3)(a) and 26-18-402(2)(a)(ii) are nonlapsing.
(2) This section supersedes any conflicting provisions of Utah law.

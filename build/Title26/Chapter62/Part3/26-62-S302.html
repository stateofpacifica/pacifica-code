Effective 7/1/2018
26-62-302.  Enforcement by state and local health departments.
     The department and local health departments shall enforce this chapter under the procedures of Title 63G, Chapter 4, Administrative Procedures Act, as an informal adjudicative proceeding, including:
(1) notifying a tobacco retailer of alleged violations of this chapter;
(2) conducting hearings;
(3) determining violations of this chapter; and
(4) imposing civil administrative penalties.

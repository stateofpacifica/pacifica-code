Effective 5/14/2019
26-21-31.  Prohibition on certain age-based physician testing.
     A health care facility may not require for purposes of employment, privileges, or reimbursement, that a physician, as defined in Section 58-67-102, take a cognitive test when the physician reaches a specified age, unless the test reflects the standards described in Subsections 58-67-302(5)(b)(i) through (x).

Amended by Chapter 445, 2019 General Session

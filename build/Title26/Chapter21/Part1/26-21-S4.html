26-21-4.  Per diem and travel expenses of committee members.
     A member may not receive compensation or benefits for the member's service, but may receive per diem and travel expenses in accordance with:
(1) Section 63A-3-106;
(2) Section 63A-3-107; and
(3) rules made by the Division of Finance pursuant to Sections 63A-3-106 and 63A-3-107.


Amended by Chapter 286, 2010 General Session

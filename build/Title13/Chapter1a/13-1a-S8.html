13-1a-8.  Violation of restraining or injunctive order -- Civil penalty.
     If any restraining order or injunction issued under this chapter is violated, the division may submit a motion for, or the court on its own motion may impose, a civil penalty of not more than $100 for each day a restraining order, preliminary injunction, or permanent injunction issued under this chapter is violated, if the party has received notice of the restraining order or injunction.

Enacted by Chapter 9, 1990 General Session

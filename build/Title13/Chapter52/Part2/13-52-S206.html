Effective 5/8/2018
13-52-206.  Good faith estimate allowed.
     A solar retailer that does not, at the time of providing a disclosure statement required in Subsection 13-52-201(1), have information required under Section 13-52-202, 13-52-203, 13-52-204, or 13-52-205 to be included in the disclosure statement may make a good faith estimate of that information, if the solar retailer clearly indicates that the information is an estimate and provides the basis for the estimate.

Enacted by Chapter 290, 2018 General Session

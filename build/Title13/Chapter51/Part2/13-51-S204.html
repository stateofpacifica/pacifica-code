Effective 5/10/2016
13-51-204.  State not liable.
     The state, a state agency, or a political subdivision is not liable for:
(1) the granting or denial of a claim under Section 13-51-203;
(2) a claim made against the fund; or
(3) a failure of the fund to pay an amount that the division orders paid from the fund.


Enacted by Chapter 359, 2016 General Session

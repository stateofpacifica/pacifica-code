Effective 1/1/2020
13-56-103.  Scope.
(1) This chapter does not apply to an event or venue of an exempt entity.
(2) Nothing in this chapter prohibits a venue operator from maintaining and enforcing one or more policies regarding conduct or behavior at or in connection with the venue.

Effective 1/1/2020
13-56-301.  Enforcement powers -- Penalty.
(1) The division may enforce the provisions of this chapter in accordance with Chapter 2, Division of Consumer Protection.
(2) A person who violates a provision of this chapter is subject to a fine of up to $250 per violation.

Effective 5/4/2022
13-58-302.  Cure of default.
(1) If a motorboat dealer defaults as described in Section 13-58-301, the manufacturer or distributor who is part of the agreement shall:
(a) give the dealer written notice of the dealer's default; and
(b) allow the dealer to cure the default within the period described in Subsection (2).
(2) A motorboat dealer may cure a default no later than:
(a) 30 days after the day on which the dealer receives the notice described in Subsection (1), if the dealer defaulted as described in Subsection 13-58-301(1)(b) or (2);
(b) 60 days after the day on which the dealer receives the notice described in Subsection (1), if the dealer defaulted as described in Subsection 13-58-301(1)(a); and
(c) 160 days after the day on which the dealer receives the notice described in Subsection (1), if the dealer defaulted as described in Subsection 13-58-301(1)(c).

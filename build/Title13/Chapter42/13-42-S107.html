13-42-107.  Application for registration -- Obligation to update information.
     An applicant or registered provider shall notify the administrator no later than 10 days after a change in the information specified in Subsection 13-42-105(2)(d) or (f) or Subsection 13-42-106(1), (3), (6), (12), or (13).

Amended by Chapter 152, 2012 General Session

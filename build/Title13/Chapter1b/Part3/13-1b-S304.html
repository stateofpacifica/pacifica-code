Effective 5/4/2022
13-1b-304.  Reporting.
(1) Beginning in 2024, before October 1, the office shall annually prepare and submit a written report to the Business and Labor Interim Committee that describes the office's work during the prior year.
(2) In a written report described in Subsection (1), the office shall include:
(a) a summary of each periodic review, each sunrise review, and each response to a legislator inquiry; and
(b) each recommendation the office made to another state executive branch agency regarding a regulated occupation.


Enacted by Chapter 413, 2022 General Session

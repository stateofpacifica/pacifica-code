Effective 5/5/2021
13-59-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Enrollee" means the same as that term is defined in Section 31A-1-301.
(2) "Health benefit plan" means the same as that term is defined in Section 31A-1-301.
(3) "Health care provider" means a person licensed to provide health care under:
(a)Title 26, Chapter 21, Health Care Facility Licensing and Inspection Act; or
(b)Title 58, Occupations and Professions.

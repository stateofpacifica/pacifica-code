Effective 5/4/2022
13-32a-118.  Payment limitation for catalytic converter purchases.
(1) A catalytic converter purchaser, when making a catalytic converter purchase, may not pay the seller for the catalytic converter with cash or a gift card.
(2) Subsection (1) does not apply to a catalytic converter purchase in which the amount paid to the seller is under $100.


Enacted by Chapter 201, 2022 General Session

Effective 5/8/2018
13-53-105.  Prohibited acts.
     A residential, vocational and life skills program may not:
(1) operate without a registration issued under Section 13-53-103;
(2) utilize any behavioral intervention that is not peer-led or that uses the services of any professional or any person purporting to be a professional;
(3) accept a participant before providing to the participant the disclosure described in Section 13-53-106; or
(4) use physical force or permit the use of physical force.


Enacted by Chapter 252, 2018 General Session

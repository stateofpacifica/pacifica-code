Effective 5/5/2021
13-60-301.  Enforcement powers of the attorney general.
(1) The attorney general may enforce this chapter.
(2) The attorney general may initiate a civil enforcement action against a person for violating this chapter.
(3) In an action to enforce this chapter, the attorney general may recover:
(a) actual damages to the consumer;
(b) costs;
(c) attorney fees; and
(d) $2,500 for each violation of this chapter.


Enacted by Chapter 361, 2021 General Session

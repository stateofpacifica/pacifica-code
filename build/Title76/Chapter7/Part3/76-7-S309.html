Effective 5/14/2019
76-7-309.  Pathologist's report.
     Any human tissue removed during an abortion shall be submitted to a pathologist who shall make a report, including whether:
(1) the pregnancy was aborted by evacuating the uterus; and
(2) a medical record indicates that, through a prenatal screening or other diagnostic test, the aborted fetus had or may have had Down syndrome.


Amended by Chapter 124, 2019 General Session

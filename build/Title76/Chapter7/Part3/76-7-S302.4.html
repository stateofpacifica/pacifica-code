Effective 6/24/2022
76-7-302.4.  Abortion restriction of an unborn child with Down syndrome.
     Notwithstanding any other provision of this part, an abortion may not be performed if the pregnant mother's sole reason for the abortion is that the unborn child has or may have Down syndrome, unless the abortion is permissible for a reason described in Subsection 76-7-302(3)(b).

Enacted by Chapter 124, 2019 General Session

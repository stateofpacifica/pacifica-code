Effective 5/14/2019
76-7-302.5.  Circumstances under which abortion prohibited.
     Notwithstanding any other provision of this part, a person may not perform or attempt to perform an abortion after the unborn child reaches 18 weeks gestational age unless the abortion is permissible for a reason described in Subsection 76-7-302(3)(b).

Enacted by Chapter 208, 2019 General Session

76-5-415.  Educator's license subject to action for violation of this part.
     Commission of any offense under this Title 76, Chapter 5, Part 4, Sexual Offenses, by an educator as defined in Section 53E-6-102, is grounds under Section 53E-6-604 for disciplinary action against the educator, including revocation of the educator's license.

Amended by Chapter 415, 2018 General Session

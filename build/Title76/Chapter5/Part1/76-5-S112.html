Effective 5/4/2022
76-5-112.  Reckless endangerment -- Penalty.
(1) Terms defined in Section 76-1-101.5 apply to this section.
(2) An actor commits reckless endangerment if, under circumstances not amounting to a felony offense, the actor recklessly engages in conduct that creates a substantial risk of death or serious bodily injury to another individual.
(3) A violation of Subsection (2) is a class A misdemeanor.

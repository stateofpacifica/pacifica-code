Effective 5/4/2022
76-5-105.  Mayhem.
(1) Terms defined in Section 76-1-101.5 apply to this section.
(2) An actor commits mayhem if the actor unlawfully and intentionally:
(a) deprives an individual of a member of the individual's body;
(b) disables or renders useless a member of an individual's body;
(c) cuts out or disables an individual's tongue;
(d) puts out an individual's eye; or
(e) slits an individual's nose, ear, or lip.
(3) A violation of Subsection (2) is a second degree felony.

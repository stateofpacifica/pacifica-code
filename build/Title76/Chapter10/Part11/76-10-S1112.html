Effective 3/28/2020
76-10-1112.  Local control.
(1) Nothing in this part preempts or otherwise limits the authority of a county or municipality to enact a local ordinance related to gambling or fringe gambling.
(2) In accordance with Title 24, Forfeiture and Disposition of Property Act, a county or municipality may seize gambling debts, gambling proceeds, or fringe gaming devices that are reasonably identifiable as being obtained or provided in violation of this part or a local ordinance.


Enacted by Chapter 291, 2020 General Session

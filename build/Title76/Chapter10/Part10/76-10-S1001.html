76-10-1001.  Definitions.
     For the purpose of this part:
(1) "Forged trademark," "forged trade name," "forged trade device," and "counterfeited trademark," "counterfeited trade name," "counterfeited trade device," or their equivalents, as used in this part, include every alteration or imitation of any trademark, trade name, or trade device so resembling the original as to be likely to deceive.
(2) "Trademark" or "trade name" or "trade device," as used in this part, includes every trademark registrable with the Division of Corporations and Commercial Code.

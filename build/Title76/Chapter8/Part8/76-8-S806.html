76-8-806.  Facts kept secret until complaint filed.
     A person may not make public any evidence of fact or the name of the person accused of violating the provisions of Sections 76-8-802, 76-8-803, 76-8-804, and 76-8-805 prior to the filing of a formal complaint by the prosecuting attorney or committing magistrate charged with the performance of that duty.

Amended by Chapter 296, 1997 General Session

Effective 5/12/2015
76-8-1201.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Client" means a person who receives or has received public assistance.
(2) "Overpayment" has the same meaning as defined in Section 35A-3-102.
(3) "Provider" has the same meaning as defined in Section 62A-11-103.
(4) "Public assistance" has the same meaning as defined in Section 35A-1-102.

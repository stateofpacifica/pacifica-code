76-8-403.  Failure to keep and pay over public money.
     Except as otherwise provided in Subsection 76-8-402(4), a person who receives, safekeeps, transfers, or disburses public money who neglects or fails to keep and pay over the money in the manner prescribed by law is guilty of a felony of the third degree.

Amended by Chapter 61, 2020 General Session

12-1-7.  Exceptions.
     This title does not apply to:
(1) an attorney authorized to practice in this state;
(2) a national bank;
(3) a bank or trust company incorporated under the laws of this state; or
(4) the following licensed under Title 31A, Insurance Code:
(a) a title insurance agency; or
(b) a title insurance producer.


Amended by Chapter 297, 2009 General Session

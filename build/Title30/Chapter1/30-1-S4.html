Effective 5/14/2019
30-1-4.  Validity of foreign marriages -- Exceptions.
     A marriage solemnized in any other country, state, or territory, if valid where solemnized, is valid in this state, unless it is a marriage:
(1) that would be prohibited and declared void in this state, under Subsection 30-1-2(1) (a); or
(2) between parties who are related to each other within and including three degrees of consanguinity, except as provided in Subsection 30-1-1(2).

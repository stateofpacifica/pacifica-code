Effective 5/4/2022
30-1-3.  Legal recognition of a child when marriage is void.
     When a marriage is void under Subsection 30-1-2(1)(a) and the parties entered into the marriage in good faith, a child of the marriage, who is born or conceived before the parties had actual knowledge that the marriage was void, shall be legally recognized as the child of the parties.

Effective 5/12/2015
78B-15-708.  Access to identifying information and medical history.
(1) A person conceived through assisted reproduction who is at least 18 years of age shall be provided, upon the person's request, access to the nonidentifying medical history of the donor who assisted in the reproduction process that resulted in the person's birth.
(2) Under no circumstance may a person who donated to a fertility clinic for the purpose of assisted reproduction be liable for financial support to the child conceived through assisted reproduction or the child's parent.
(3) Except as provided in this section, a donor's request to remain anonymous shall be given full deference.


Enacted by Chapter 159, 2015 General Session

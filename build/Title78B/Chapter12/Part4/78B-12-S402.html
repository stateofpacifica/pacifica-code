Effective 5/14/2019
78B-12-402.  Duties -- Report -- Staff.
(1) The advisory committee shall review the child support guidelines to ensure the application of the guidelines results in the determination of appropriate child support award amounts.
(2) The advisory committee shall submit, in accordance with Section 68-3-14, a written report to the legislative Judiciary Interim Committee on or before October 1, 2021, and then on or before October 1 of every fourth year subsequently.
(3) The advisory committee's report shall include recommendations of the majority of the advisory committee, as well as specific recommendations of individual members of the advisory committee.
(4) Staff for the advisory committee shall be provided from the existing budget of the Department of Human Services.


Amended by Chapter 136, 2019 General Session

Effective 5/12/2020
78B-5-458.  Statute of limitations.
     An action to recognize a foreign-country judgment shall be commenced within the earlier of:
(1) the time during which the foreign-country judgment is effective in the foreign country; or
(2) 15 years from the day on which the foreign-country judgment became effective in the foreign country.


Enacted by Chapter 370, 2020 General Session

78B-5-814.  Release, settlement, or statement by injured person -- When rescission or disavowal provisions inapplicable.
     Sections 78B-5-812 through 78B-5-816 do not apply if at least five days prior to signing the settlement agreement, liability release, or statement, the injured person signed a statement in writing indicating willingness and agreement to the settlement agreement, liability release, or statement.

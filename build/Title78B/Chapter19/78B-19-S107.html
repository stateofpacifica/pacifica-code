78B-19-107.  Emergency orders.
     During a collaborative law process, a court may issue emergency orders, including protective orders in accordance with Title 78B, Chapter 7, Part 6, Cohabitant Abuse Protective Orders, or Part 2, Child Protective Orders, to protect the health, safety, welfare, or interest of a party or member of a party's household.

Amended by Chapter 142, 2020 General Session

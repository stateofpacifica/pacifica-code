Effective 5/14/2019
78B-2-105.  Effect of death.
     If an individual entitled to bring an action dies before the expiration of the statute of limitations and the cause of action survives, an action may be brought by the individual's representatives within the later of:
(1) the statute of limitations; or
(2) one year after the day on which the individual died.


Amended by Chapter 46, 2019 General Session

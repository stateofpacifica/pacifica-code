Effective 7/1/2015
78B-14-503.  Employer's compliance with two or more income-withholding orders.
     If an obligor's employer receives two or more income-withholding orders with respect to the earnings of the same obligor, the employer satisfies the terms of the multiple orders if the employer complies with the law of the state of the obligor's principal place of employment to establish the priorities for the withholding and allocating income withheld for two or more child support obligees.

Amended by Chapter 45, 2015 General Session

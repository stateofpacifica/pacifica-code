Effective 5/9/2017
78B-21-109.  Status of receiver as lien creditor.
     On appointment of a receiver, the receiver has the status of a lien creditor under:
(1)Title 70A, Chapter 9a, Uniform Commercial Code - Secured Transactions, as to receivership property that is personal property or fixtures; and
(2)Title 57, Chapter 9, Marketable Record Title, as to receivership property that is real property.


Enacted by Chapter 431, 2017 General Session

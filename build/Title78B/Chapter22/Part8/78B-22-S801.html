Effective 9/1/2021
78B-22-801.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Contracted parental representation attorney" means an attorney who represents an indigent individual who is a parent in a child welfare case under a contract with the office or a contributing county.
(2) "Contributing county" means a county that complies with this part for participation in the fund described in Section 78B-22-804.
(3) "Fund" means the Child Welfare Parental Representation Fund created in Section 78B-22-804.
(4) "Program" means the Child Welfare Parental Representation Program created in Section 78B-22-802.

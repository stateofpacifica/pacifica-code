  78B-6-2205.  Damages -- Class action.
(1) If a court finds that a manufacturer is liable under Section 78B-6-2204, the court may award the plaintiff actual damages.
(2) A class action may be brought under this part in accordance with Utah Rules of Civil Procedure, Rule 23.

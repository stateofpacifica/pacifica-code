78B-6-515.  Distribution of award -- Execution -- Annulment of proceedings on failure to pay.
     Payment may be made to the defendants entitled to payment, or the money may be deposited in court for the defendants and distributed to those entitled to payment. If the money is not paid or deposited, the defendants may have execution as in civil cases; and if the money cannot be made on execution, the court upon a showing to that effect shall set aside and annul the entire proceedings, and restore possession of the property to the defendants, if possession has been taken by the plaintiff.

Effective 5/12/2015
78B-6-209.  Dispute Resolution Account -- Appropriation.
     There is created a restricted account within the General Fund known as the "Dispute Resolution Account." Five dollars of the fees established in Subsections 78A-2-301(1)(a) through (e), (1)(g), and (1)(s) shall be allocated to and deposited into the Dispute Resolution Account. The Legislature shall annually appropriate money from the Dispute Resolution Account to the Administrative Office of the Courts to implement the purposes of Title 78B, Chapter 6, Part 2, Alternative Dispute Resolution Act.

Amended by Chapter 74, 2015 General Session

78B-6-1220.  Proceeds of sale of encumbered property -- Disposition of.
     The proceeds of the sale of encumbered property shall be applied under the direction of the court, as follows:
(1) to pay its just proportion of the general costs of the action;
(2) to pay the costs of the reference;
(3) to satisfy and cancel all recorded liens in their order of priority, by payment of the sums due and to become due; the amount due to be verified by affidavit at the time of payment;
(4) the residue among the owners of the property sold according to their respective shares therein.

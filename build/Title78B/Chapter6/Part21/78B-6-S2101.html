Effective 5/9/2017
78B-6-2101.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Minor" means an individual less than 18 years of age.
(2) "Pornographic material" means material that:
(a) the average person, applying contemporary community standards, finds that, taken as a whole, appeals to prurient interest in sex;
(b) is patently offensive in the description or depiction of nudity, sexual conduct, sexual excitement, sadomasochistic abuse, or excretion; and
(c) taken as a whole does not have serious literary, artistic, political, or scientific value.


Enacted by Chapter 464, 2017 General Session

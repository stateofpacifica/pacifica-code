Effective 5/4/2022
78B-6-1273.  Referees.
     If the court appoints referees, each referee, in addition to the requirements and disqualifications applicable to referees in Title 78B, Chapter 6, Part 12, Partition, shall be disinterested and impartial and not a party to or a participant in the action.

Enacted by Chapter 304, 2022 General Session

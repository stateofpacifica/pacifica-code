78B-6-902.  Deficiency judgment -- Execution.
     If it appears that the proceeds of the sale are insufficient and a balance still remains due, the judgment shall be docketed by the clerk and execution may be issued for the balance as in other cases. A general execution may not be issued until after the sale of the mortgaged property and the application of the amount realized to the preceding judgment.

Effective 5/4/2022
78B-24-307.  Child-placing agency compliance.
(1) The Office of Licensing, created in Section 62A-2-103, may investigate an allegation that a child-placing agency has failed to comply with this part and commence an action for injunctive or other relief or initiate administrative proceedings against the child-placing agency to enforce this part.
(2)
(a) The Office of Licensing may initiate a proceeding to determine whether a child-placing agency has failed to comply with this part.
(b) If the Office of Licensing finds that the child-placing agency has failed to comply, the Office of Licensing may suspend or revoke the child-placing agency's license or take other action permitted by law of the state.


Enacted by Chapter 326, 2022 General Session

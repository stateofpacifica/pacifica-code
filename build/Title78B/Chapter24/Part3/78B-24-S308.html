Effective 5/4/2022
78B-24-308.  Rulemaking authority.
     The Office of Licensing, created in Section 62A-2-103, may adopt rules under Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, to implement Sections 78B-24-303, 78B-24-304, 78B-24-305, and 78B-24-306.

Enacted by Chapter 326, 2022 General Session

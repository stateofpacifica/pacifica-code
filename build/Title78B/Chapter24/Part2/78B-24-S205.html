Effective 5/4/2022
78B-24-205.  Prohibited soliciting or advertising.
(1) A person may not solicit or advertise to:
(a) find a person to which to make a transfer of custody in violation of Subsection 78B-24-203(1);
(b) identify a child for a transfer of custody in violation of Subsection 78B-24-203(3); or
(c) act as an intermediary in a transfer of custody in violation of Subsection 78B-24-203(3).
(2) A violation of this section is a class B misdemeanor.


Enacted by Chapter 326, 2022 General Session

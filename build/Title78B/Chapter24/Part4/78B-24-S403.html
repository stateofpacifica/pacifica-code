Effective 5/4/2022
78B-24-403.  Transitional provisions.
(1)Part 2, Prohibition of Unregulated Custody Transfer, applies to:
(a) a transfer of custody on or after May 4, 2022; and
(b) soliciting or advertising on or after May 4, 2022.
(2)Part 3, Information and Guidance, applies to placement of a child for adoption more than 60 days after May 4, 2022.


Enacted by Chapter 326, 2022 General Session

Effective 5/4/2022
63I-1-207.  Repeal dates: Title 7.
(1) Section 7-1-203, which creates the Board of Financial Institutions, is repealed July 1, 2031.
(2) Section 7-3-40, which creates the Board of Bank Advisors, is repealed July 1, 2032.
(3) Section 7-9-43, which creates the Board of Credit Union Advisors, is repealed July 1, 2023.


Amended by Chapter 20, 2022 General Session

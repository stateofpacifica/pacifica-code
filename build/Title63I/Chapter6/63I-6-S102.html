63I-6-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Formula" means a description of or directions for a computation in the Utah Code.
(2) "Mathematical equation" means a symbolic expression of a formula that is created by the legislative fiscal analyst under Subsection 63I-6-103(1).


Enacted by Chapter 49, 2015 General Session

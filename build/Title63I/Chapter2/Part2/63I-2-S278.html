Effective 5/4/2022
63I-2-278.  Repeal dates: Title 78A and Title 78B.
(1) If Title 78B, Chapter 6, Part 22, Cause of Action to Protect Minors from Unfiltered Devices, is not in effect before January 1, 2031, Title 78B, Chapter 6, Part 22, Cause of Action to Protect Minors from Unfiltered Devices, is repealed January 1, 2031.
(2) Sections 78B-12-301 and 78B-12-302 are repealed on January 1, 2025.

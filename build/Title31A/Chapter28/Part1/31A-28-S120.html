Effective 1/1/2019
31A-28-120.  Prospective application.
     Notwithstanding any prior or subsequent law, the provisions of this part that are in effect on the date on which the association first becomes obligated for the policies or contracts of an insolvent or impaired insurer govern the association's rights and obligations to the policy owners, contract owners, certificate holders, and enrollees of the insolvent or impaired insurer.

Amended by Chapter 391, 2018 General Session

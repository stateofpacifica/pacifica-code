31A-23a-907.  Market conduct and penalties.
     A limited lines travel insurance producer and any travel retailer offering and disseminating travel insurance under the limited lines travel insurance producer license are subject to Sections 31A-2-308, 31A-23a-402, and 31A-23a-402.5.

Enacted by Chapter 277, 2014 General Session

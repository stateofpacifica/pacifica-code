Effective 5/4/2022
31A-23a-911.  Classification of travel insurance -- Standards -- Status.
(1) An insurer shall classify and file travel insurance under an inland marine line of insurance.
(2) An insurer may:
(a) issue travel insurance as an individual, group, or blanket policy; or
(b) develop eligibility and underwriting standards for travel insurance based on travel protection plans designed for individual or identified marketing or distribution channels, if the standards also meet underwriting standards for inland marine insurance.
(3) Under this part, the following are not insurance:
(a) a cancellation fee waiver; and
(b) a travel assistance service.


Enacted by Chapter 364, 2022 General Session

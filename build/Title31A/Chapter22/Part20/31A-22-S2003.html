Effective 5/12/2020
31A-22-2003.  Scope.
(1) The requirements of this part apply to limited long-term care insurance policies and certificates marketed, delivered, or issued for delivery in this state on or after July 1, 2020.
(2) Laws and regulations designed or intended to apply to Medicare supplement insurance policies may not be applied to limited long-term care insurance.


Enacted by Chapter 32, 2020 General Session

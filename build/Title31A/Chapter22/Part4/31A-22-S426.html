31A-22-426.  Coverage description.
(1) Each life insurance policy, annuity contract, and certificate of life insurance shall contain a brief description printed on the cover page.
(2) The description shall include:
(a) the type of insurance;
(b) whether it is participating or nonparticipating;
(c) a significant limitation stated or included in the filed policy, contract, or certificate; and
(d) a significant specific feature stated or included in the filed policy, contract, or certificate.


Enacted by Chapter 125, 2005 General Session

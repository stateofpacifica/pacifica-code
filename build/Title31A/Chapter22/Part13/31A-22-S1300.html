Effective 5/5/2021
31A-22-1300.  Aircraft public liability insurance.
     Policies containing aircraft public liability insurance coverage for an aircraft shall include minimum coverage of:
(1) $50,000 per person for bodily injury or death in any one accident;
(2) $50,000 for property damage in any one accident; and
(3) $100,000 in any one accident, whether for property damage, or bodily injury or death.


Amended by Chapter 253, 2021 General Session

Effective 5/9/2017
31A-22-614.7.  Uniform claims processing -- Electronic exchange of prescription drug pre-authorization.
     The commissioner shall consult with national and state organizations involved with the standardized exchange of health data, and the electronic exchange of health data, to study and review:
(1) the process of prior authorization of prescription drugs; and
(2) the standards for the use and electronic exchange of a uniform prescription drug prior authorization form that meet federal mandatory minimum standards and follow the adoption of national requirements for transaction and data elements in the federal Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act.


Amended by Chapter 18, 2017 General Session

Effective 7/1/2021
31A-22-656.  Coverage of epinephrine auto-injector.
     A health benefit plan entered into or renewed on or after July 1, 2021, that provides coverage of an epinephrine auto-injector is not required to reimburse a participant, as that term is defined in Section 49-20-421, for an epinephrine auto-injector the participant obtains through the discount program described in Section 49-20-421.

Enacted by Chapter 255, 2021 General Session

31A-44-203.  Renewal process.
     In order to renew a registration under this section, a provider shall:
(1) pay an annual fee established by the department in accordance with Section 63J-1-504;
(2) submit an updated provider disclosure statement that complies with Section 31A-44-301;
(3) submit a copy of the most recent version of the continuing care contract the provider will propose to a prospective facility resident; and
(4) comply with rules made by the department under Subsection 31A-44-202(2).


Enacted by Chapter 270, 2016 General Session

31A-4-105.  Deposit required from domestic insurers.
     Domestic insurers organized or operating under Chapter 5, Domestic Stock and Mutual Insurance Corporations, Chapter 7, Nonprofit Health Service Insurance Corporations, or Chapter 9, Insurance Fraternals, shall maintain a deposit under Section 31A-2-206 in the amount of the insurer's required capital for stock insurers, or minimum permanent surplus for mutuals. The commissioner may not issue a certificate of authority to an insurer operating under one of these chapters until the insurer complies with this section.

Amended by Chapter 20, 1995 General Session

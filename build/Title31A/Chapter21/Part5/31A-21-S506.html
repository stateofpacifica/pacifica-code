31A-21-506.  Enforcement -- Private rights.
(1) An insurer that violates this part is subject to any penalty permitted under this title.
(2) This part does not:
(a) create a private right of action for a violation of this part; or
(b) limit or impair the right of an individual to sue and recover damages from the insurer in a civil action for a cause of action that is not based on a violation of this part.


Enacted by Chapter 132, 1997 General Session

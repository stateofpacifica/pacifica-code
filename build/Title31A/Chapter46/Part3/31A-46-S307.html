Effective 5/12/2020
31A-46-307.  Pharmacy benefit manager reporting.
(1) A pharmacy benefit manager may not enter into or renew a contract with an insurer on or after January 1, 2021, to administer or manage rebate contracting or rebate administration unless the pharmacy benefit manager agrees to regularly report to the insurer information regarding pharmaceutical manufacturer rebates received by the pharmacy benefit manager under the contract.
(2) The quality and type of information required under Subsection (1) shall be detailed, claims level information unless the pharmacy benefit manager and insurer agree to waive this requirement in a separate written agreement.


Enacted by Chapter 198, 2020 General Session

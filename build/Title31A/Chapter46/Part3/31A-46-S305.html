Effective 5/12/2020
31A-46-305.  Pharmacy reimbursement.
     A pharmacy benefit manager shall reimburse a network pharmacy, in the aggregate, in an amount no less than the amount that the pharmacy benefit manager reimburses an affiliate of the pharmacy benefit manager in the same network, in the aggregate, for providing the same or equivalent pharmacy service.

Enacted by Chapter 198, 2020 General Session

This was also enacted in HB272, Chapter 198 and SB138, Chapter 275, however, this section number will not be updated and will remain as 58-17b-619.

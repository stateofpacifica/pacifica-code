Effective 7/1/2021
31A-2-103.  Commissioner's appointees.
(1) The commissioner may appoint up to three persons to assist the commissioner. The commissioner may designate a person appointed under this section as a "deputy," "administrative assistant," "secretary," or any other title chosen by the commissioner.
(2) Persons appointed under this section are exempt from career service status under Section 63A-17-301 and serve at the pleasure of the commissioner.


Amended by Chapter 345, 2021 General Session

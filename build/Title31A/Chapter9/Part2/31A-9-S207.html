31A-9-207.  Termination of organization permit and payment of organization expenses.
     Section 31A-5-209, other than Subsection (3)(c), applies to fraternals, except that:
(1) the word "mutual" shall be read "fraternal"; and
(2) the reference to "Section 31A-5-212" shall be read "Section 31A-9-210."


Enacted by Chapter 242, 1985 General Session

31A-19a-308.  Rate service organizations -- Filing requirements.
(1) A rate service organization shall file with the commissioner any of the following that is used in this state:
(a) any statistical plan;
(b) all prospective loss costs;
(c) provisions for special assessments;
(d) all supplementary rating information; and
(e) any change, amendment, or modification of an item described in Subsections (1)(a) through (d).
(2) The filings required under Subsection (1) shall be subject to Sections 31A-19a-203 and 31A-19a-206 and other provisions of this chapter relating to filings made by insurers.


Enacted by Chapter 130, 1999 General Session

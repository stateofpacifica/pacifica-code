Effective 5/10/2016
31A-17-513.  Minimum standards for accident and health insurance contracts.
(1) For an accident and health insurance contract issued before the operative date of the valuation manual, the minimum standard of valuation is the standard adopted by the commissioner by rule.
(2) For an accident and health insurance contract issued on or after the operative date of the valuation manual, the standard prescribed in the valuation manual is the minimum standard of valuation required under Subsection 31A-17-502(2).

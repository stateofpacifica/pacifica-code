31A-17-404.4.  Transition -- Application to reinsurance agreement.
     The amendments to this part made in Laws of Utah 2008, Chapter 257, apply to a cession made on or after July 1, 2008 under a reinsurance contract that has an inception, anniversary, or renewal date no sooner than January 1, 2009.

Enacted by Chapter 257, 2008 General Session

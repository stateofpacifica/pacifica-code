31A-6a-108.  Obligation of reimbursement insurance issuers.
     Providers under this chapter are considered to be the agent of the issuer of the reimbursement insurance for purposes of Section 31A-23a-410. In cases where a provider is acting as an administrator and enlists other providers, the provider acting as the administrator shall notify the issuer of the reimbursement insurance of the other providers.

Amended by Chapter 298, 2003 General Session

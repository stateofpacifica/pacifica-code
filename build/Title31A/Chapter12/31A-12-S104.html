31A-12-104.  Insurance policies issued to school districts.
     Insurance policies issued by the Risk Management Fund to school districts under Section 63A-4-204 shall conform to Chapter 21, Insurance Contracts in General, and Chapter 22, Contracts in Specific Lines. Policy forms issued to the school district shall be filed under Section 31A-21-201. The policy form filing fees of Section 31A-3-103 shall be paid to the Insurance Department by the Risk Management Fund.

Amended by Chapter 212, 1993 General Session

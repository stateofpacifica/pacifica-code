31A-12-103.  Rates charged to school districts.
     The rates charged to school districts for policies issued under Section 63A-4-204 are not subject to Chapter 19a, Utah Rate Regulation Act, except for the filing requirement of Subsection 31A-19a-203(1) and the public availability requirement of Section 31A-19a-204. Rate filing fees under Section 31A-3-103 shall be paid to the department by the Risk Management Fund.

Amended by Chapter 130, 1999 General Session

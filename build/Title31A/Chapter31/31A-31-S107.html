Effective 5/14/2019
31A-31-107.  Workers' compensation insurance fraud.
(1) In any action involving workers' compensation insurance, Section 34A-2-110 supersedes this chapter.
(2) Nothing in this section prohibits the department from investigating and pursuing civil or criminal penalties in accordance with Section 31A-31-109 and Title 34A, Utah Labor Code, for violations of Section 34A-2-110.


Amended by Chapter 193, 2019 General Session

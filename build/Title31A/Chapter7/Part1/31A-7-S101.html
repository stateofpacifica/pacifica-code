31A-7-101.  Definition.
     As used in this chapter, unless stated otherwise:
     "Subscriber" means the person entitled by contract to health care benefits from a corporation licensed under this chapter. "Subscriber" is used interchangeably with "policyholder" in individual contracts or "certificate holder" in group contracts.

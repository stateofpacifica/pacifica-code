Effective 5/10/2016
31A-35-704.  Submission of bail bond agencies and producers to jurisdiction of court.
     By applying for and receiving a license or certificate to engage in the bail bond insurance business in accordance with this chapter, a bail bond agency or bail bond producer:
(1) submits to the jurisdiction of the court;
(2) irrevocably appoints the clerk of the court as agent upon whom any papers affecting the bail bond agency's or bail bond producer's liability on the undertaking may be served; and
(3) acknowledges that liability may be enforced on motion and upon notice as the court may require, without the necessity of an independent action.


Amended by Chapter 234, 2016 General Session

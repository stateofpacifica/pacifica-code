Effective 1/1/2021
19-1-106.  Boards within department.
(1) The following policymaking boards are created within the department:
(a) the Air Quality Board, appointed under Section 19-2-103;
(b) the Drinking Water Board, appointed under Section 19-4-103;
(c) the Water Quality Board, appointed under Section 19-5-103; and
(d) the Waste Management and Radiation Control Board, appointed under Section 19-6-103.
(2) The authority of the boards created in Subsection (1) is limited to the specific authority granted them under this title.
(3) A vacancy that occurs during an expired term in a board described in Subsection (1) shall be filled in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 24, Part 2, Vacancies.

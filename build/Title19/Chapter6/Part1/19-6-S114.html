19-6-114.  Service of notice, order, or other document.
     In accordance with procedural rules adopted by the department, service of any notice, order, or other document issued by, or under the authority of, the director may be made by forwarding a copy of that notice, order, or other document by registered mail, directed to the person's designated address.

Amended by Chapter 256, 2020 General Session

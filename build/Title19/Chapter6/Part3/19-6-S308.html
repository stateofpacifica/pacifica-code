19-6-308.  Hazardous Substances Mitigation Fund -- Prohibited uses.
     The executive director may not use fund money to pay for:
(1) property damage or bodily injury resulting from a hazardous material or substance release; or
(2) property damage or bodily injury resulting from remedial studies or abatement action taken to address a hazardous material or substance release.

19-5-124.  Phosphorus limit for household dishwashing detergent.
(1) As used in this section, "household dishwashing detergent" means a substance used:
(a) to clean an item commonly used for preparing food, eating, or drinking; and
(b) by an individual for a personal, family, or household purpose.
(2) Except as provided by Subsection (3), a person may not sell a household dishwashing detergent that contains 0.5% or more phosphorus by weight on or after July 1, 2010.
(3) This section does not apply to a dishwashing detergent designed and used for a commercial or industrial purpose.


Enacted by Chapter 81, 2008 General Session

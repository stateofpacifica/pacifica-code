19-5-112.  Hearings conducted by an administrative law judge -- Decisions on denial or revocation of permit conducted by executive director.
(1) Except as provided by Subsection (2), an administrative law judge shall conduct hearings authorized by Section 19-5-111 in accordance with Section 19-1-301.
(2)
(a) An administrative law judge shall conduct, on the executive director's behalf, a hearing regarding an appeal of a permit decision for which the state has assumed primacy under the Federal Water Pollution Control Act, 33 U.S.C. Sec. 1251 et seq.
(b) The decision of the executive director is final and binding on all parties unless stayed or overturned on appeal.


Amended by Chapter 360, 2012 General Session

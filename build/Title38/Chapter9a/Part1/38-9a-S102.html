38-9a-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter, "wrongful lien" refers to a lien made in violation of Section 76-6-503.5, and includes:
(1) a wrongful lien as defined in Section 38-9-102; and
(2) a nonconsensual common law document as defined in Section 38-9-102.

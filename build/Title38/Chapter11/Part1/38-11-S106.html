Effective 5/8/2018
38-11-106.  State not liable.
     The state and the state's agencies, instrumentalities, and political subdivisions are not liable for:
(1) issuance or denial of any certificate of compliance;
(2) any claims made against the fund; or
(3) failure of the fund to pay any amounts ordered by the director to be paid from the fund, including failure of the fund to pay any amounts ordered by the director to be paid because there is insufficient money in the fund.


Amended by Chapter 229, 2018 General Session

Effective 5/4/2022
39A-5-232.  Military court sentence -- Execution by convening authority.
(1) Except under Sections 39A-5-306 and 39A-5-231, a military court sentence may be ordered executed by the convening authority when approved, unless suspended or deferred.
(2) The convening authority has discretion to approve the sentence or a part or commuted form of the sentence.
(3) After approval, the convening authority may suspend the execution of the sentence.

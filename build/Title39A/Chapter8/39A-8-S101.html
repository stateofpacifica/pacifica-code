Effective 5/4/2022
39A-8-101.  West Traverse Sentinel Landscape Act -- Purpose.
     The purpose of this act is to:
(1) identify lands adjacent to Camp Williams that are important to the nation's defense mission;
(2) preserve and enhance the relationship between adjacent landowners and Camp Williams; and
(3) create incentives to encourage adjacent landowners to adopt land management practices consistent with Camp Williams's military mission.

Effective 5/4/2022
39A-8-102.  Area designation -- West Traverse Sentinel Landscape.
(1) The compatible use buffer area surrounding Camp Williams shall be known as the West Traverse Sentinel Landscape.
(2) Lands designated by the committee established in Section 39A-8-103 and lands acquired or encumbered through the Camp Williams Army Compatible Use Buffer (ACUB) Program shall be added to the buffer area.

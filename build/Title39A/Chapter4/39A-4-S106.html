Effective 5/4/2022
39A-4-106.  Compensation of force members.
(1) Members of the Utah State Defense Force, when called into active service by the governor, shall receive compensation as prescribed by the governor.
(2) The compensation may not exceed the rate of pay prescribed for officers and other members of the National Guard when called into active service of the state by the governor.

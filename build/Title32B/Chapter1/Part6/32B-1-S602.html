32B-1-602.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Malted beverage" means:
(a) beer;
(b) a flavored malt beverage; and
(c) heavy beer.
(2) "Packaging" means the outer packaging that is visible to a consumer such as a carton, case, or other wrapper of a container.

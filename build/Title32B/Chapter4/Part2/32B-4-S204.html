32B-4-204.  Arrests.
(1) Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, an arrest of a person for a violation of this title shall be made in accordance with:
(a)Title 77, Chapter 7, Arrest, by Whom, and How Made; and
(b) Rules 6 and 7, Utah Rules of Criminal Procedure.
(2) A summons in lieu of a warrant of arrest shall be in accordance with Rule 6, Utah Rules of Criminal Procedure.


Enacted by Chapter 276, 2010 General Session

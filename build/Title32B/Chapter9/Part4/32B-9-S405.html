32B-9-405.  Specific permitting requirements for temporary beer event permit issued by commission.
(1) To obtain a temporary beer event permit, in addition to complying with Part 2, Event Permitting General Provisions, a person shall state in the person's written application the purpose of the event for which the person seeks a temporary beer event permit.
(2) The application fee for a beer permit is $100.
(3) The bond amount required for a beer permit is the penal sum of $500.


Amended by Chapter 334, 2011 General Session

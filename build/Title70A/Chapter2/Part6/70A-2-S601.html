Effective 5/8/2018
70A-2-601.  Buyer's rights on improper delivery.
     Subject to the provisions of Section 70A-2-612, and unless otherwise agreed under Sections 70A-2-718 and 70A-2-719, if the goods or the tender of delivery fail in any respect to conform to the contract, the buyer may:
(1) reject the whole;
(2) accept the whole; or
(3) accept any commercial unit or units and reject the rest.


Amended by Chapter 281, 2018 General Session

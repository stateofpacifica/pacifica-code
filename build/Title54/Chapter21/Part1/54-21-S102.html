Effective 9/1/2018
54-21-102.  Scope.
     Nothing in this chapter:
(1) permits an entity to provide a service regulated under 47 U.S.C. Secs. 521 through 573, in a right-of-way without compliance with all applicable legal obligations;
(2) imposes a new requirement on the activity of a cable provider in a right-of-way for a cable service provided in this state;
(3) governs:
(a) a pole that an electrical corporation owns or a wireless support structure that an electrical corporation owns; or
(b) the attachment of a small wireless facility to a pole that an electrical corporation owns or to a wireless support structure that an electrical corporation owns; or
(4) confers on an authority any new jurisdiction over an electrical corporation.


Enacted by Chapter 299, 2018 General Session

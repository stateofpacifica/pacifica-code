Effective 9/1/2018
54-21-301.  Applicability -- General.
(1) This part applies to:
(a) the collocation of a small wireless facility in a right-of-way;
(b) the collocation of a small wireless facility on a wireless support structure in a right-of-way; and
(c) the installation, modification, or replacement of a utility pole associated with a small wireless facility in a right-of-way.
(2) Except as provided in this chapter, an authority may not prohibit, regulate, or charge for the collocation of a small wireless facility.


Enacted by Chapter 299, 2018 General Session

Effective 9/1/2018
54-21-402.  Prohibition on exclusive use.
(1) A person owning, managing, or controlling an authority pole in a right-of-way may not enter into an exclusive arrangement with a person for the right to collocate a small wireless facility to the authority pole.
(2) A person who purchases or otherwise acquires an authority pole is subject to the requirements of this part.
(3) An authority shall allow the collocation of a small wireless facility on an authority pole in a right-of-way:
(a) as provided in this chapter; and
(b) subject to the permitting process in Part 3, Permitting Process for Small Wireless Facilities.


Enacted by Chapter 299, 2018 General Session

Effective 9/1/2018
54-21-205.  Height limitations in a right-of-way.
(1) A new or modified utility pole that has a collocated small wireless facility, and that is installed in a right-of-way, may not exceed 50 feet above ground level.
(2) An antenna of a small wireless facility may not extend more than 10 feet above the top of a utility pole existing on or before September 1, 2018.


Enacted by Chapter 299, 2018 General Session

Part 5 - Irrigation District Act

  • Section 501 - Title.
  • Section 502 - Provisions applicable to irrigation districts.
  • Section 503 - Additional irrigation district powers -- No authority to levy property tax.
  • Section 504 - Irrigation district board of trustees -- Bond for board of trustees members and district if the district is appointed as fiscal or other agent for the United States.
  • Section 505 - Divisions.
  • Section 506 - Different use charges for different units -- Use charges based on the size of the land served -- Use charge may not be based on property value.
  • Section 507 - Right-of-way over state land.
  • Section 508 - Inclusion of state land in an irrigation district.
  • Section 509 - State engineer not prohibited from increasing water allotment.
  • Section 510 - Rules for the distribution and use of water.
  • Section 511 - Distribution of water under a contract with the United States.
  • Section 512 - Removal of land from the assessor's roll.
  • Section 513 - Temporary application of water to land.
  • Section 514 - Assignment of the right to water.
  • Section 515 - Distribution of water when supply is inadequate.
  • Section 516 - Diversions of water subject to eminent domain law.

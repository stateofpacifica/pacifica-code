17B-2a-702.  Provisions applicable to mosquito abatement districts.
(1) Each mosquito abatement district is governed by and has the powers stated in:
(a) this part; and
(b)Chapter 1, Provisions Applicable to All Local Districts.
(2) This part applies only to mosquito abatement districts.
(3) A mosquito abatement district is not subject to the provisions of any other part of this chapter.
(4) If there is a conflict between a provision in Chapter 1, Provisions Applicable to All Local Districts, and a provision in this part, the provision in this part governs.


Enacted by Chapter 329, 2007 General Session

Effective 5/12/2015
17B-2a-1112.  Audit.
     The board of trustees shall provide a copy of an accounting report, as defined in Section 51-2a-102, to each political subdivision that is provided municipal services by the municipal services district that is filed with the state auditor on behalf of the municipal services district in accordance with Section 51-2a-203.

Enacted by Chapter 352, 2015 General Session

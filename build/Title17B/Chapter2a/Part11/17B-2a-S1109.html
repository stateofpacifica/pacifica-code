Effective 5/14/2019
17B-2a-1109.  Counties and municipalities authorized to provide funds to a municipal services district.
(1) A county or, subject to Section 17B-2a-1108, a municipality involved in the establishment or operation of a municipal services district may fund the operation and maintenance of the district through the sharing of sales tax and other revenue for district purposes.
(2) A municipal services district may use sales tax or other revenue that the district receives from a county or a municipality under Subsection (1) to fund expenses and activities of a county or municipality that is part of the district.


Amended by Chapter 37, 2019 General Session

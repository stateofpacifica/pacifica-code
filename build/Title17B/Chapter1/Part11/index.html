Part 11 - Local District Bonds

  • Section 1101 - Provisions applicable to a local district's issuance of bonds.
  • Section 1102 - General obligation bonds.
  • Section 1103 - Levy to pay for general obligation bonds.
  • Section 1104 - Pledge of revenues to pay for bonds.

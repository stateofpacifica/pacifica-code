Effective 5/14/2019
17B-1-113.  Liability insurance.
(1) Each local district with an annual operating budget of $50,000 or more shall obtain liability insurance as considered appropriate by the local district board.
(2) Each local district with an annual operating budget of less than $50,000 is not required to obtain liability insurance, but liability insurance is encouraged, as considered appropriate by the local district board.


Amended by Chapter 37, 2019 General Session

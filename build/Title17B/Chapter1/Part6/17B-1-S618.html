Effective 5/4/2022
17B-1-618.  Purchasing procedures.
     All purchases or encumbrances by a local district shall be made or incurred according to the purchasing procedures established for each district by the district's rulemaking authority, as that term is defined in Section 63G-6a-103, and only on an order or approval of the person or persons duly authorized.

Amended by Chapter 381, 2022 General Session

Effective 7/1/2021
15A-3-113.  Amendments to Chapters 32 through 35 of IBC.
(1) In IBC, Chapter 35, the referenced standard for NFPA 70-17 is deleted and replaced with NFPA 70-20.
(2) In IBC, Chapter 35, the referenced standard ICCA117.1-09, Section 606.2, Exception 1 is modified to include the following sentence at the end of the exception:
     "The minimum clear floor space shall be centered on the sink assembly."


Amended by Chapter 199, 2021 General Session

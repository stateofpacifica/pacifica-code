15A-1-208.  Standards for specialized buildings.
(1) This chapter may not be implied to repeal or otherwise affect the authority granted to a state agency to make or administer standards for specialized buildings, as provided in:
(a)Title 26, Chapter 21, Health Care Facility Licensing and Inspection Act;
(b)Title 26, Chapter 39, Utah Child Care Licensing Act;
(c)Title 62A, Chapter 2, Licensure of Programs and Facilities;
(d)Title 64, Chapter 13, Department of Corrections - State Prison; or
(e) another statute that grants a state agency authority to make or administer other special standards.
(2) If a special standard conflicts with a code, the special standard prevails.
(3) This chapter does not apply to the administration of the statutes described in Subsection (1).


Enacted by Chapter 14, 2011 General Session

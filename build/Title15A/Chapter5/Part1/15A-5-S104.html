Effective 5/28/2021
15A-5-104.  Exemptions from State Fire Code.
(1) As used in this section, "remote yurt" means the same as that term is defined in Section 15A-1-202.
(2) A remote yurt is exempt from the State Fire Code unless otherwise provided by ordinance in accordance with Subsection 15A-1-204(12)(b).
(3) An owner of a remote yurt shall ensure that a fire extinguisher is in the remote yurt.

Effective 5/12/2020
15A-2-101.  Title -- Adoption of code.
(1) This chapter is known as the "Adoption of State Construction Code."
(2) In accordance with Chapter 1, Part 2, State Construction Code Administration Act, the Legislature repeals the State Construction Code in effect on July 1, 2010, and adopts the following as the State Construction Code:
(a) this chapter;
(b)Chapter 2a, Tall Wood Buildings of Mass Timber Construction Incorporated as Part of State Construction Code;
(c)Chapter 3, Statewide Amendments Incorporated as Part of State Construction Code;
(d)Chapter 4, Local Amendments Incorporated as Part of State Construction Code; and
(e)Chapter 6, Additional Construction Requirements.


Amended by Chapter 43, 2020 General Session

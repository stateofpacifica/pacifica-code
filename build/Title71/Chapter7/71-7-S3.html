71-7-3.  Development, operation, and maintenance of Utah Veterans Cemetery and Memorial Park -- Responsibilities of Department of Veterans and Military Affairs -- Costs -- Definition.
(1) The Department of Veterans and Military Affairs shall develop, operate, and maintain a veterans cemetery and memorial park.
(2) To help pay the costs of developing, constructing, operating, and maintaining a veterans cemetery and memorial park, the Department of Veterans and Military Affairs may:
(a) by following the procedures and requirements of Title 63J, Chapter 5, Federal Funds Procedures Act, receive federal funds, and may receive state funds, contributions from veterans organizations, and other private donations; and
(b) charge fees for at least the cost of the burial of a veteran's spouse and any other persons, whom the department determines are eligible to be buried in a veterans cemetery established by the state.
(3) "Veteran" has the same meaning as defined in Section 68-3-12.5.


Amended by Chapter 154, 2020 General Session

Effective 5/12/2015
71-13-104.  Education requirements.
(1) All individuals and attorneys providing assistance to a veteran shall complete three hours of qualifying education as specified in 38 C.F.R. 14.629(b) during the first 12 month period following the date of initial accreditation; and
(2) an additional three hours of qualifying continuing education every two years following the initial 12-month period.


Enacted by Chapter 123, 2015 General Session

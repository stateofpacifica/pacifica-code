71-13-106.  Exempt organizations.
     Accredited representatives of the following organizations are exempt from the provisions of this chapter:
(1) American Legion;
(2) Veterans of Foreign Wars;
(3) Disabled American Veterans;
(4) Vietnam Veterans of America;
(5) American Veterans (AMVET);
(6) Military Order of the Purple Heart; and
(7) other VA recognized service organizations as determined by the executive director.


Enacted by Chapter 123, 2015 General Session

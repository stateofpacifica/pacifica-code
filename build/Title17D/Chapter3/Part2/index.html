Part 2 - Creation, Consolidation, Division, and Dissolution of Conservation Districts

  • Section 201 - Commission authority to approve the creation, consolidation, division, and dissolution of conservation districts -- Notice and hearing requirements.
  • Section 202 - Petition to create conservation district -- Petition or commission motion to consolidate, divide, or dissolve conservation districts -- Petition requirements.
  • Section 203 - Considerations in determining whether to approve conservation district creation, consolidation, division, or dissolution -- Denial or approval -- Notice and plat to lieutenant governor -- Recording requirements -- Prohibition against considering similar creation, consolidation, division, or dissolution if previously denied.
  • Section 204 - Commission action if conservation districts are consolidated, divided, or dissolved.

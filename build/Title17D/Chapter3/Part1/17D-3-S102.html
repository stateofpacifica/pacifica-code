Effective 5/12/2020
17D-3-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Commission" means the Conservation Commission, created in Section 4-18-104.
(2) "Commissioner" means the commissioner of the department.
(3) "Conservation district" means a limited purpose local government entity, as described in Section 17D-3-103, that operates under, is subject to, and has the powers set forth in this chapter.
(4) "Department" means the Department of Agriculture and Food, created in Section 4-2-102.

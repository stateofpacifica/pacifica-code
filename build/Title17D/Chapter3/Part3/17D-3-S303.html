Effective 5/12/2020
17D-3-303.  Nominating committee nomination of candidates for appointment to the board of supervisors.
     The nominating committee under Subsection 17D-3-302(1) shall:
(1) nominate for a conservation district a slate of candidates for appointment to the board of supervisors of the conservation district equal or greater in number to the number of board of supervisors members to be appointed; and
(2) submit the names of candidates to the commissioner no later than the date set by the commission as the close of nominations.


Amended by Chapter 311, 2020 General Session

17D-1-210.  Services that special service district is authorized to provide.
(1) After its creation, a special service district may provide the service or services:
(a) specified in the resolution or ordinance creating the special service district; or
(b) added under Part 4, Annexing a New Area and Adding a New Service.
(2) Notwithstanding Subsection (1), a special service district created before May 5, 2008 may continue on and after that date to provide a service that the special service district was authorized before May 5, 2008 to provide.


Enacted by Chapter 360, 2008 General Session

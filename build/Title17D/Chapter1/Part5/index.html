Part 5 - Special Service District Bonds

  • Section 501 - Provisions applicable to a special service district's issuance of bonds.
  • Section 502 - General obligation bonds.
  • Section 503 - Levy to pay for general obligation bonds.
  • Section 504 - Pledge of revenues to pay for bonds.
  • Section 505 - Revenue bonds -- Requirement to impose rates and charges to cover revenue bonds -- Authority to make agreements and covenants to provide for bond repayment.
  • Section 506 - Governing body required to fix rates to cover district expenses and bonds.
  • Section 507 - Guaranteed bonds.
  • Section 508 - Special service district obligations are not obligations of any other entity.
  • Section 509 - Ratification of previously issued bonds and previously entered contracts.

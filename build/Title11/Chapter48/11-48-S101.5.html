Effective 5/5/2021
11-48-101.5.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1)
(a) "911 ambulance services" means ambulance services rendered in response to a 911 call received by a designated dispatch center that receives 911 or E911 calls.
(b) "911 ambulance services" does not mean a seven or ten digit telephone call received directly by an ambulance provider licensed under Title 26, Chapter 8a, Utah Emergency Medical Services System Act.
(2) "Municipality" means a city, town, or metro township.
(3) "Political subdivision" means a county, city, town, local district, or special district.

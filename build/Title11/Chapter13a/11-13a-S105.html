Effective 5/8/2018
11-13a-105.  Registration as a limited purpose entity.
(1) Each governmental nonprofit corporation shall register and maintain the governmental nonprofit corporation's registration as a limited purpose entity, in accordance with Section 67-1a-15.
(2) A governmental nonprofit corporation that fails to comply with Subsection (1) or Section 67-1a-15 is subject to enforcement by the state auditor, in accordance with Section 67-3-1.


Enacted by Chapter 256, 2018 General Session

Effective 5/8/2018
11-59-301.  Authority board -- Delegation of power.
(1) The authority shall be governed by a board, which shall manage and conduct the business and affairs of the authority and shall determine all questions of authority policy.
(2) All powers of the authority are exercised through the board.
(3) The board may by resolution delegate powers to authority staff.

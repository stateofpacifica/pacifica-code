Effective 5/12/2015
11-13-511.  Continuing authority of governing body.
     After the conclusion of the public hearing held in accordance with Section 11-13-510, the governing board:
(1) may:
(a) continue to review the tentative budget;
(b) insert any new item; or
(c) increase or decrease items of expenditure in the tentative budget; and
(2) shall adopt a final budget.


Enacted by Chapter 265, 2015 General Session

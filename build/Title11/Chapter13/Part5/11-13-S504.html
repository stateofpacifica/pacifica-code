Effective 5/12/2015
11-13-504.  Uniform accounting system.
     An interlocal entity shall:
(1) establish and maintain the interlocal entity's accounting records, and financial statements prepared from those records, as required by generally accepted accounting principles; and
(2) adopt and implement internal accounting controls in light of the needs and resources of the interlocal entity.


Enacted by Chapter 265, 2015 General Session

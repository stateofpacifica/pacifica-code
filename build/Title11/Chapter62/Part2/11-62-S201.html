Effective 7/1/2019
11-62-201.  Limits on penalties for failure to pay a fine.
     An individual assessed a parking citation, civil citation, or administrative fine may not be charged:
(1) by the original jurisdiction that imposes the fine, late fees and interest in the aggregate that are more than 25% of the initial fine amount; and
(2) by a court, interest in the aggregate that is more than 25% of the initial fine amount.

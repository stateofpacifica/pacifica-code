34A-2-506.  Loss of hearing -- Time for filing claim.
     An employee's occupational hearing loss shall be reported to the employer pursuant to Section 34A-2-407 within 180 days of the date the employee:
(1) first suffered altered hearing; and
(2) knew, or in the exercise of reasonable diligence should have known, that the hearing loss was caused by employment.

Effective 5/9/2017
34A-2-424.  Prescribing policies for certain opioid prescriptions.
(1) This section applies to a person regulated by this chapter or Chapter 3, Utah Occupational Disease Act.
(2) A self-insured employer, as that term is defined in Section 34A-2-201.5, an insurance carrier, and a managed health care program under Section 34A-2-111 may implement a prescribing policy for certain opioid prescriptions in accordance with Section 31A-22-615.5.


Enacted by Chapter 53, 2017 General Session

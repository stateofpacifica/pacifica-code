Effective 5/8/2018
34A-1-102.  Definitions.
     Unless otherwise specified, as used in this title:
(1) "Certified mail" means a method of mailing by any carrier that is accompanied by proof of delivery.
(2) "Commission" means the Labor Commission created in Section 34A-1-103.
(3) "Commissioner" means the commissioner of the commission appointed under Section 34A-1-201.

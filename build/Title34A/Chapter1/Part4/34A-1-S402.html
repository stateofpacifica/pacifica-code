34A-1-402.  Publication of orders, rules, and rates.
(1)
(a) The commission shall make available in proper form for distribution to the public, its orders and rules; and
(b) furnish the information made available under Subsection (1) to any person upon request.
(2) The commission may in accordance with Section 63G-2-203 charge a fee for furnishing materials under this section.


Amended by Chapter 382, 2008 General Session

34A-1-307.  Action permitted in adjudicative proceedings.
     For the purposes mentioned in this title, the commission may take any action permitted:
(1) if a formal adjudicative proceeding, under Section 63G-4-205 or 63G-4-206; or
(2) if an informal adjudicative proceeding, under Section 63G-4-203.


Amended by Chapter 382, 2008 General Session

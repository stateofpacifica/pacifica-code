17-25a-2.  Constable -- Qualifications -- Duties.
(1) To qualify as a constable, a person shall be certified as a special function peace officer in the state.
(2) A constable shall:
(a) avoid all conflicts of interest; and
(b) maintain a public office and be accessible to the public and to the court during the hours the court is open.


Enacted by Chapter 44, 1990 General Session

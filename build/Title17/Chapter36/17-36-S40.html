17-36-40.  Notice that audit complete.
(1) Within 10 days after the receipt of the audit report furnished by the independent auditor, the county auditor shall prepare and publish a notice to the public that the county audit is complete:
(a) at least twice in a newspaper of general circulation within the county; and
(b) as required in Section 45-1-101.
(2) A copy of the county audit may be inspected at the office of the county auditor.


Amended by Chapter 388, 2009 General Session

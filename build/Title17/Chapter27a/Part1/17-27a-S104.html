Effective 5/14/2019
17-27a-104.  County standards.
(1) This chapter does not prohibit a county from adopting the county's own land use standards.
(2) Notwithstanding Subsection (1), a county may not impose a requirement, regulation, condition, or standard that conflicts with a provision of this chapter, other state law, or federal law.

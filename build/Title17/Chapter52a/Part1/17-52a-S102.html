Effective 3/24/2020
17-52a-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Optional plan" means a plan establishing an alternate form of government for a county as provided in Section 17-52a-404.
(2) "Study committee" means the committee that has five members appointed and charged with the duties as provided in Section 17-52a-403.

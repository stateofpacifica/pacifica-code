Effective 11/16/2021
17-32-2.  Collection of fines by bail commissioners -- Disposition.
(1) A bail commissioner shall have power to receive money tendered in payment of the fine of an individual serving sentence in default of the payment of the fine when the court is closed.
(2) A bail commissioner shall deliver any money received by a bail commissioner under Subsection (1) to the court that issued the commitment order within three days after the day on which the money is received.


Amended by Chapter 4, 2021 Special Session 2

Effective 5/12/2020
17-50-335.  Energy efficiency upgrade, renewable energy system, or electric vehicle charging infrastructure.
     A county may provide or finance an energy efficiency upgrade, a renewable energy system, or electric vehicle charging infrastructure as defined in Section 11-42a-102, in a designated voluntary assessment area in accordance with Title 11, Chapter 42a, Commercial Property Assessed Clean Energy Act.

Amended by Chapter 354, 2020 General Session

Effective 5/13/2014
17-30a-205.  Comprehensive job classification plan.
(1) The commission shall formulate a comprehensive job classification plan covering all merit system officers employed by the sheriff or by the police interlocal entity.
(2) The plan shall:
(a) place all positions requiring substantially the same duties and qualifications in the same classification;
(b) include minimum physical and educational qualifications of the applicants for each position; and
(c) provide standards for promotion.
(3) In the event a new position is created and approved, the commission shall classify the position in the classification plan.


Enacted by Chapter 366, 2014 General Session

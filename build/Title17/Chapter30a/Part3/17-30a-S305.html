Effective 5/13/2014
17-30a-305.  Preparation and expiration of eligible appointment register.
(1) Upon completion of an examination, the commission shall prepare and adopt an eligible appointment register containing the names of applicants receiving a passing grade ranked in the order of grades earned, beginning with the highest.
(2)
(a) An eligible appointment register shall expire not later than two years after the date of the examination unless the commission, for good reason, extends the time not to exceed one additional year.
(b) If the commission adopts a new eligible appointment register, a previous appointment register for the same class or position is cancelled.


Enacted by Chapter 366, 2014 General Session

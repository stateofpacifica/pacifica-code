17-21-10.  Judgments affecting real estate.
(1) A county recorder shall record for real property, any part of which is located in the county:
(a) a judgment affecting the real property;
(b) a release, assignment, renewal, or extension of a judgment lien affecting the real property; or
(c) a certified copy of a final judgment or decree partitioning or affecting the title or possession of the real property.
(2) A document recorded in accordance with this section is subject to the requirements of Section 57-3-106.


Amended by Chapter 88, 2011 General Session

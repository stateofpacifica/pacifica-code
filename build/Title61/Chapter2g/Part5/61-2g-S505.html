61-2g-505.  Penalty for violating this chapter -- Automatic revocation.
(1) In addition to being subject to a disciplinary action, a person required to be licensed, certified, or registered under this chapter who violates this chapter:
(a) is guilty of a class A misdemeanor, upon a conviction of a first violation of this chapter; and
(b) is guilty of a third degree felony, upon conviction of a second or subsequent violation of this chapter.
(2) A license, certification, or registration issued by the division to a person convicted of a violation of Section 76-6-1203 is automatically revoked.

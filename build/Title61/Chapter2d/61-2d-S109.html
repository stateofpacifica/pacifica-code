61-2d-109.  Copies.
     The lender shall provide, at no cost to the borrower, a complete set of all documents pertaining to the transaction, including copies of all documents to be executed or recorded and any loan documents changed at the closing:
(1) at the time of the closing if done in person; or
(2) no later than three business days from the closing if done by mail or any other format.


Enacted by Chapter 252, 2004 General Session

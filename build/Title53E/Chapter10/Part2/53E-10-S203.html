Effective 5/14/2019
53E-10-203.  Director of adult education.
(1) Upon recommendation of the state superintendent, the state board may appoint a full-time director for adult education to work under the supervision of the state board.
(2) The director may coordinate the adult education program authorized under Sections 53E-10-202 through 53E-10-206 with other adult education programs.


Amended by Chapter 186, 2019 General Session

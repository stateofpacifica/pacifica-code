Effective 7/1/2020
53E-10-304.  Concurrent enrollment participation form -- Parental permission.
(1) The Utah Board of Higher Education shall create a higher education concurrent enrollment participation form that includes a parental permission form.
(2) Before allowing an eligible student to participate in concurrent enrollment, an LEA and an institution of higher education shall ensure that the eligible student has, for the current school year:
(a) submitted the participation form described in Subsection (1);
(b) signed an acknowledgment of program participation requirements; and
(c) obtained parental permission as indicated by the signature of a student's parent on the parental permission form.


Amended by Chapter 365, 2020 General Session

Effective 7/1/2020
53E-10-308.  Reporting.
     The state board and the Utah Board of Higher Education shall submit an annual written report to the Higher Education Appropriations Subcommittee and in accordance with Section 53E-1-203 on student participation in the concurrent enrollment program, including:
(1) data on the higher education tuition not charged due to the hours of higher education credit granted through concurrent enrollment;
(2) tuition or fees charged under Section 53E-10-305;
(3) an accounting of the money appropriated for concurrent enrollment; and
(4) a justification of the distribution method described in Subsections 53F-2-409(3)(d) and (e).


Amended by Chapter 365, 2020 General Session

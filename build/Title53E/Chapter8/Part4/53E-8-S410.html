Effective 5/14/2019
53E-8-410.  School districts to provide space for programs.
     A school district with students who reside within the school district's boundaries and are served by the Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind shall make a good faith effort to provide the Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind with space required for programs offered by the Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind.

Amended by Chapter 314, 2019 General Session

Effective 5/14/2019
53E-8-402.  Entrance policies and procedures.
     With input from the Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind, school districts, parents, and the advisory council, the state board shall establish entrance policies and procedures that IEP teams and Section 504 teams are to consider in making placement recommendations at the Utah Schools for the Deaf and the Blind.

Amended by Chapter 186, 2019 General Session

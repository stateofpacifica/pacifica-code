Effective 1/1/2021
53E-1-202.1.  Report to the Public Education Appropriations Subcommittee on the Special Needs Opportunity Scholarship Program.
(1) Beginning in 2021, the State Board of Education shall, in accordance with Section 68-3-14, annually submit the report described in Section 53E-7-404 to the Public Education Appropriations Subcommittee.
(2) This section supersedes any conflicting provisions of Utah law.


Enacted by Chapter 3, 2020 Special Session 4

Effective 5/5/2021
53E-1-205.  Reporting impact analysis.
(1) As used in this section, "proposed report" means a report that:
(a) an LEA is required to prepare or submit to the state board;
(b) a rule proposed by the state board requires; and
(c) is not required by federal law, Utah Code, or another state entity.
(2) The state board shall establish a policy or procedures to evaluate the impact a proposed report may have on reporting requirements for an LEA.
(3) The impact described in Subsection (2) may include:
(a) the estimated cost to an LEA associated with the proposed report;
(b) the estimated time an LEA administrator will spend preparing the proposed report; and
(c) any disproportionate impact the proposed report may have on an LEA because of the LEA's size, location, or other factors.

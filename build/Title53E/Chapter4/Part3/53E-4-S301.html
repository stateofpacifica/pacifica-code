Effective 5/14/2019
53E-4-301.  Definitions.
     As used in this part:
(1) "Core standards for Utah public schools" means the standards established by the state board as described in Section 53E-4-202.
(2) "Statewide assessment" means one or more of the following, as applicable:
(a) a standards assessment described in Section 53E-4-303;
(b) a high school assessment described in Section 53E-4-304;
(c) a college readiness assessment described in Section 53E-4-305; or
(d) an assessment of students in grade 3 to measure reading grade level described in Section 53E-4-307.

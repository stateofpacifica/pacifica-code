Effective 5/14/2019
53E-4-309.  Grade level specification change.
(1) The state board may change a grade level specification for the administration of specific assessments under this part to a different grade level specification or a competency-based specification if the specification is more consistent with patterns of school organization.
(2)
(a) If the state board changes a grade level specification described in Subsection (1), the state board shall submit a report to the Education Interim Committee explaining the reasons for changing the grade level specification.
(b) The state board shall submit the report at least six months before the anticipated change.

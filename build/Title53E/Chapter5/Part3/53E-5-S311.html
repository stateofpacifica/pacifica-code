Effective 5/4/2022
53E-5-311.  Coordination with the Partnerships for Student Success Grant Program.
     If a springboard school or elevate school is a member of a partnership that receives a grant under Title 53F, Chapter 5, Part 4, Partnerships for Student Success Grant Program, the school improvement committee shall:
(1) coordinate the school improvement committee's efforts with the efforts of the partnership; and
(2) ensure that the goals and outcomes of the partnership are aligned with the school improvement plan described in this part.

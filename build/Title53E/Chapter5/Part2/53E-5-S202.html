Effective 5/12/2020
53E-5-202.  Statewide school accountability system -- State board rulemaking.
(1) There is established a statewide school accountability system.
(2) In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking Act, the state board shall make rules to implement the school accountability system in accordance with this part.


Amended by Chapter 408, 2020 General Session

Effective 5/14/2019
53E-6-403.  Tie-in with the Criminal Investigations and Technical Services Division.
(1) The state board shall:
(a) designate employees to act, with state board supervision, as an online terminal agency with the Department of Public Safety's Criminal Investigations and Technical Services Division under Section 53-10-108; and
(b) provide relevant information concerning current or prospective employees or volunteers upon request to other school officials as provided in Section 53E-6-402.
(2) The cost of the online service shall be borne by the entity making the inquiry.


Amended by Chapter 186, 2019 General Session

Effective 5/14/2019
53E-3-302.  Compensation of state superintendent -- Other state board employees.
(1) The state board shall establish the compensation of the state superintendent.
(2) The state board may, as necessary for the proper administration and supervision of the public school system:
(a) appoint other employees; and
(b) delegate appropriate duties and responsibilities to state board employees.
(3) The compensation and duties of state board employees shall be established by the state board and paid from money appropriated for that purpose.


Amended by Chapter 186, 2019 General Session

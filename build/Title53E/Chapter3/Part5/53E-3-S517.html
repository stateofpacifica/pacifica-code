Effective 5/14/2019
53E-3-517.  Educator credential database.
(1) As used in this section:
(a) "Educator" means the same as that term is defined in Section 53E-6-102.
(b) "Educator credential database" means a database used by the state board that:
(i) contains educator credential information and LEA information; and
(ii) is used by the state board to determine funding distribution.
(2) Before July 1, 2020, the state board shall ensure that a technical limitation of the educator credential database does not prevent an educator from accepting employment at more than one LEA.


Amended by Chapter 186, 2019 General Session

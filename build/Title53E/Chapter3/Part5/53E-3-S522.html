Effective 7/1/2020
53E-3-522.  Substance abuse prevention in public school programs -- Funds allocated.
     The state board shall provide for:
(1) substance abuse prevention and education;
(2) substance abuse prevention training for teachers and administrators; and
(3) district and school programs to supplement, not supplant, existing local prevention efforts in cooperation with local substance abuse authorities.


Enacted by Chapter 230, 2020 General Session

Technically renumbered to avoid duplication of section number also enacted in HB114, Chapter 174.

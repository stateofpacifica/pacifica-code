Effective 5/14/2019
53E-3-504.  Child literacy program -- Coordinated activities.
(1) The state board, through the state superintendent, shall provide for a public service campaign to educate parents on the importance of providing their children with opportunities to develop emerging literacy skills through a statewide "Read to Me" program.
(2) The state board shall coordinate its activities under this section with other state and community entities that are engaged in child literacy programs in order to maximize its efforts and resources, including the Utah Commission on National and Community Service.


Amended by Chapter 186, 2019 General Session

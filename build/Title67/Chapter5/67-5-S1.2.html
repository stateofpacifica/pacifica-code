Effective 5/12/2020
67-5-1.2.  Local investigation assistance.
     The attorney general may:
(1) assist or intervene in a local investigation only if:
(a) the local law enforcement agency requests assistance; or
(b) the county or district attorney requests assistance; and
(2) provide Rapid DNA assistance for a local investigation in accordance with Section 53-10-403.6 upon request of and as authorized by, both the investigating agency and the county or district attorney.


Enacted by Chapter 415, 2020 General Session

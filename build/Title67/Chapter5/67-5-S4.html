Effective 5/10/2016
67-5-4.  Interaccount billings included in budget -- Payment of staff members.
(1) The attorney general shall include in his annual budget all interaccount billings and pay directly out of his funds all members of his staff, whether housed in his offices or not.
(2) The attorney general may operate the Office of the Attorney General or any portion of the Office of the Attorney General as an internal service fund agency in accordance with Section 63J-1-410 for legal services that the Office of the Attorney General provides.


Amended by Chapter 120, 2016 General Session

67-19d-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Board of trustees" or "board" means the board of trustees created in Section 67-19d-202.
(2) "Income" means the revenues received by the state treasurer from investments of the trust fund principal.
(3) "Trust fund" means the State Post-Retirement Benefits Trust Fund created by Section 67-19d-201.


Enacted by Chapter 99, 2007 General Session

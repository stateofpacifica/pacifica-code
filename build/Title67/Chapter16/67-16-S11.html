Effective 5/10/2016
67-16-11.  Applicability of provisions.
(1) As used in this section, "government position" means the position of a legislator, public officer, or public employee.
(2) The provisions of this chapter:
(a) apply to all public officers and public employees; and
(b) do not apply to a conflict of interest that exists between two or more government positions held by the same individual, unless the conflict of interest is also due to a personal interest of the individual that is not shared by the general public.


Amended by Chapter 360, 2016 General Session

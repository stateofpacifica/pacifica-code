Effective 5/9/2017
67-4a-906.  Action by person whose claim is denied.
     Not later than one year after filing a claim under Subsection 67-4a-903(1), the claimant may commence an action against the administrator in the district court to establish a claim that has been denied or considered denied under Subsection 67-4a-904(2).

Enacted by Chapter 371, 2017 General Session

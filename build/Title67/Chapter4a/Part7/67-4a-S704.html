Effective 5/9/2017
67-4a-704.  Purchaser owns property after sale.
(1) A purchaser of property at a sale conducted by the administrator under this chapter takes the property free of all claims of the owner, a previous holder, or a person claiming through the owner or holder.
(2) The administrator shall execute documents necessary to complete the transfer of ownership to the purchaser.

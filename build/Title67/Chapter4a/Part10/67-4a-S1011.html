Effective 5/9/2017
67-4a-1011.  Determination of liability for unreported reportable property.
     If the administrator determines from an examination conducted under Section 67-4a-1002 that a putative holder failed or refused to pay or deliver to the administrator property that is reportable under this chapter, the administrator shall issue a determination of the putative holder's liability to pay or deliver and give notice in a record to the putative holder of the determination.

Enacted by Chapter 371, 2017 General Session

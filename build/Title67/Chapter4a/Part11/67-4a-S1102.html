67-4a-1102.  Review of administrator's determination.
     A putative holder may seek relief from a determination under Section 67-4a-1011 by:
(1) administrative review under Section 67-4a-1103; or
(2) judicial review under Section 67-4a-1104.


Enacted by Chapter 371, 2017 General Session

Effective 7/1/2021
67-19f-102.  Definitions.
     As used in this chapter:
(1) "Annual leave II" means the same as that term is defined in Section 63A-17-510.
(2) "Board of trustees" or "board" means the board of trustees created in Section 67-19f-202.
(3) "Income" means the revenues received by the state treasurer from investments of the trust fund principal.
(4) "Trust fund" means the State Employees' Annual Leave Trust Fund created in Section 67-19f-201.


Amended by Chapter 344, 2021 General Session
